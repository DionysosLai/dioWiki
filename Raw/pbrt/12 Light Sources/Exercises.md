1. ① The functionality of the SpotLight could be replicated by using a suitable image in conjunction with the [ProjectionLight](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Sources/Point_Lights.html#ProjectionLight) or [GoniometricLight](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Sources/Point_Lights.html#GoniometricLight). Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of providing this specific functionality separately with the SpotLight class.

> 1. ① SpotLight 的功能可以通过使用合适的图像配合 ProjectionLight 或 GoniometricLight 来复制。讨论用 SpotLight 类单独提供这种特定功能的优缺点。

2. ② Modify the [ProjectionLight](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Sources/Point_Lights.html#ProjectionLight) to also support orthographic projections. This variant is particularly useful even without an image map, since it gives a directional light source with a beam of user-defined extent.

> 2. ② 修改 ProjectionLight 以同时支持正交投影。即使没有图像贴图，这个变体也特别有用，因为它提供了具有用户定义范围光束的方向光源。

3. ③ The current light source implementations do not support animated transformations. Modify pbrt to include this functionality and render images showing off the effect of animating light positions. Note that if you would like to use the [BVHLightSampler](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Sources/Light_Sampling.html#BVHLightSampler) with animated light sources, it will require substantial modifications.

> 3. ③ 当前的光源实现不支持动画变换。修改 pbrt 以包含此功能，并渲染展示动画光源位置效果的图像。注意，如果你希望将 BVHLightSampler 与动画光源一起使用，将需要大量修改。

4. ② Implement an area light that generalizes the [DiffuseAreaLight](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Sources/Area_Lights.html#DiffuseAreaLight) to support directionally varying emitted radiance. You might, for example, allow focusing the light by computing the cosine of the outgoing direction and the surface normal and raising it to some power. Derive a model such that the total power of the light is left unchanged as the directional distribution of emitted radiance varies and update the light's SampleLe() methods for sampling rays leaving the light to account for the emission distribution. Discuss the implications of your approach for sampling via SampleLi(); should that method be aware of the changed directional distribution?

> 4. ② 实现一个面积光源，将 DiffuseAreaLight 推广为支持方向变化的发射辐射亮度。例如，你可以通过计算出射方向与表面法线的余弦并将其提升到某个幂来允许聚焦光线。推导一个模型，使得当发射辐射亮度的方向分布变化时，光源的总功率保持不变，并更新光源的 SampleLe() 方法以考虑发射分布。讨论你的方法对通过 SampleLi() 采样的影响。

5. ② Read some of the papers in the "Further Reading" section that discuss the shadow cache, and add this optimization to pbrt. Measure how much it speeds up the system for a variety of scenes. What techniques can you come up with that make it work better in the presence of multiple levels of reflection?

> 5. ② 阅读"进一步阅读"部分中讨论阴影缓存的论文，并将此优化添加到 pbrt。测量它对各种场景的加速效果。你能想出什么技术使其在存在多级反射时更好地工作？

6. ② One of the advantages of the linearity assumption in radiometry is that the final image of a scene is the same as the sum of individual images that account for each light source's contribution (assuming a floating-point image file format is used that does not clip pixel radiance values). An implication of this property is that if a renderer creates a separate image for each light source, it is possible to write interactive lighting design tools that make it possible to quickly see the effects of scaling the contributions of individual lights in the scene without needing to re-render it from scratch. Instead, a light's individual image can be scaled and the final image regenerated by summing all the light images again. (This technique was first applied for opera lighting design by Dorsey, Sillion, and Greenberg ([1991](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Sources/Further_Reading.html#cite:Dorsey:1991:DAS)).) Modify pbrt to output a separate image for each of the lights in the scene, and write an interactive lighting design tool that uses them in this manner.

> 6. ② 辐射度学中线性假设的一个优势是，场景的最终图像等于各个光源贡献的独立图像之和。这意味着如果渲染器为每个光源创建单独的图像，就可以编写交互式照明设计工具，快速查看缩放场景中各个光源贡献的效果，而无需从头重新渲染。修改 pbrt 以为场景中的每个光源输出单独的图像，并编写一个以此方式使用它们的交互式照明设计工具。

7. ③ Read the paper by Velázquez-Armendáriz et al. ([2015](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Sources/Further_Reading.html#cite:Velazquez-Armendariz2015)), and implement their method for efficiently rendering scenes with complex light sources. Create or find models of a few complex lights that include many shapes that exhibit specular reflection or transmission in order to evaluate your implementation.

> 7. ③ 阅读 Velázquez-Armendáriz 等人 (2015) 的论文，并实现他们高效渲染具有复杂光源场景的方法。

8. ② Generalize the [PortalImageInfiniteLight](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Sources/Infinite_Area_Lights.html#PortalImageInfiniteLight) to allow the specification of multiple portals. Note that multiple coplanar portals can be supported without resampling the environment map, but non-coplanar portals will require multiple copies of it. As noted by Bitterli et al. ([2015](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Sources/Further_Reading.html#cite:Bitterli2015)), the summed area table representation makes it easy to compute the total power passing through a portal given a receiving point. Modify your implementation to use the relative power of multiple portals as a sampling distribution to choose which portal to sample. Render images that show the benefit of this improvement.

> 8. ② 将 PortalImageInfiniteLight 推广为允许指定多个入口。注意，多个共面入口可以在不重新采样环境贴图的情况下支持，但非共面入口将需要多份环境贴图。修改你的实现，使用多个入口的相对功率作为采样分布来选择采样哪个入口。

9. ② Sampling wavelengths according to the XYZ matching functions is a reasonable approach for many scenes, though if the light sources in the scene have highly peaked spectra (as, for example, many fluorescent lights do), error may be reduced by instead sampling wavelengths according to the lights' spectral distributions. Implement this approach and compare the results to pbrt 's current wavelength sampling implementation using a variety of spectral emission profiles. How much does the alternative sampling strategy help in the best case versus the worst case? Can you find a way to improve the results further by applying multiple importance sampling?

> 9. ② 根据 XYZ 匹配函数采样波长对许多场景来说是合理的方法，但如果场景中的光源具有高度尖峰的光谱（例如许多荧光灯），则根据光源的光谱分布采样波长可能会减少误差。实现此方法并使用各种光谱发射分布将结果与 pbrt 当前的波长采样实现进行比较。

10. ③ The [BVHLightSampler](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Sources/Light_Sampling.html#BVHLightSampler) is missing a number of features in the BVH sampling scheme described by Conty Estevez and Kulla ([2018](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Sources/Further_Reading.html#cite:Conty2018:bvh)), including an importance factor specialized for participating media and adaptive splitting, where multiple lights may be returned from the sampling operation when it is difficult to determine which child node is a better choice at the upper levels of the tree. Read their paper and improve pbrt 's implementation. What is the change in Monte Carlo efficiency? Does the reduction in error justify the increase in computation?

> 10. ③ BVHLightSampler 缺少 Conty Estevez 和 Kulla (2018) 描述的 BVH 采样方案中的若干特性，包括专门用于参与介质的重要性因子和自适应分裂。阅读他们的论文并改进 pbrt 的实现。

11. ③ Another shortcoming of the current [BVHLightSampler](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Sources/Light_Sampling.html#BVHLightSampler) implementation is that it does not account for the BSDF at the reference point but instead effectively assumes a diffuse surface. Read the paper by Liu et al. ([2019b](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Sources/Further_Reading.html#cite:Liu2019)) and improve the BVH light sampler's implementation by using their approach to account for this factor. Measure the change in MSE for a variety of scenes with this improvement.

> 11. ③ 当前 BVHLightSampler 实现的另一个不足是它没有考虑参考点处的 BSDF，而是实际上假设为漫反射表面。阅读 Liu 等人 (2019b) 的论文，使用他们的方法改进 BVH 光源采样器的实现以考虑此因素。
