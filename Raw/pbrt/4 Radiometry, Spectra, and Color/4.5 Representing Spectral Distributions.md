## 4.5 Representing Spectral Distributions

Figure 4.16: Spectral Distribution of Reflection from Lemon Skin.

> 图 4.16：柠檬果皮反射的光谱分布。

Spectral distributions in the real world can be complex. In order to render images of scenes that include a variety of complex spectra, a renderer must have efficient and accurate representations of spectral distributions. This section will introduce pbrt's abstractions for representing and performing computation with them.

> 现实世界中的光谱分布可能很复杂。为了渲染包含各种复杂光谱的场景图像，渲染器必须具有高效且准确的光谱分布表示。本节将介绍 pbrt 用于表示和计算光谱分布的抽象。

We will start by defining constants that give the range of visible wavelengths. Wavelengths are specified in nanometers.

> 我们首先定义给出可见光波长范围的常量。波长以纳米为单位指定。

```
constexpr Float Lambda_min = 360, Lambda_max = 830;
```

### 4.5.1 Spectrum Interface

We will find a variety of spectral representations useful in pbrt, ranging from spectral sample values tabularized by wavelength to functional descriptions such as the blackbody function. This brings us to our first interface class, Spectrum.

> 我们将发现 pbrt 中有多种有用的光谱表示，从按波长列表的光谱采样值到黑体函数等功能性描述。这引出了我们的第一个接口类 Spectrum。

Spectrum inherits from TaggedPointer, which handles the details of runtime polymorphism. The most important method that Spectrum defines is `operator()`, which takes a single wavelength and returns the value of the spectral distribution for that wavelength.

> Spectrum 继承自 TaggedPointer，后者处理运行时多态性的细节。Spectrum 定义的最重要方法是 `operator()`，它接受单个波长并返回该波长处光谱分布的值。

Spectrum implementations must also provide a `MaxValue()` method that returns a bound on the maximum value of the spectral distribution over its wavelength range. This method's main use is for computing bounds on the power emitted by light sources so that lights can be sampled according to their expected contribution.

> Spectrum 实现还必须提供 `MaxValue()` 方法，返回光谱分布在其波长范围内最大值的上界。该方法的主要用途是计算光源发射功率的上界，以便根据光源的预期贡献进行采样。

### 4.5.2 General Spectral Distributions

**ConstantSpectrum** is the simplest: it represents a constant spectral distribution over all wavelengths. The most common use is to define a zero-valued spectral distribution in cases where a particular form of scattering is not present.

> **ConstantSpectrum** 是最简单的：它表示所有波长上的恒定光谱分布。最常见的用途是在不存在特定散射形式时定义零值光谱分布。

**DenselySampledSpectrum** stores a spectral distribution sampled at 1 nm intervals over a given range of integer wavelengths. It can be useful if the provided spectral distribution is computationally expensive to evaluate, as it allows subsequent evaluations to be performed by reading a single value from memory.

> **DenselySampledSpectrum** 存储在给定整数波长范围内以 1 nm 间隔采样的光谱分布。如果提供的光谱分布计算成本高昂，它会很有用，因为后续求值可以通过从内存中读取单个值来执行。

**PiecewiseLinearSpectrum** offers another representation that is often more compact; its distribution is specified by a set of (λ, value) pairs where the spectral distribution is defined by linearly interpolating between them (Figure 4.17). For spectra that are smooth in some regions and change rapidly in others, this representation makes it possible to specify the distribution at a higher rate in regions where its variation is greatest.

> **PiecewiseLinearSpectrum** 提供了另一种通常更紧凑的表示；其分布由一组 (λ, value) 对指定，光谱分布通过在它们之间线性插值来定义（图 4.17）。对于某些区域平滑而其他区域变化迅速的光谱，这种表示使得在变化最大的区域以更高的速率指定分布成为可能。

Figure 4.17: PiecewiseLinearSpectrum defines a spectral distribution using a set of sample values. A continuous distribution is then defined by linearly interpolating between them.

> 图 4.17：PiecewiseLinearSpectrum 使用一组采样值定义光谱分布。然后通过在它们之间线性插值来定义连续分布。

**BlackbodySpectrum** gives the spectral distribution of a blackbody emitter at a specified temperature. It represents a normalized blackbody spectral distribution where the maximum value at any wavelength is 1. Wien's displacement law gives the wavelength where radiance is at its maximum.

> **BlackbodySpectrum** 给出指定温度下黑体发射体的光谱分布。它表示归一化的黑体光谱分布，其中任何波长的最大值为 1。维恩位移定律给出辐射亮度最大的波长。

### 4.5.3 Embedded Spectral Data

pbrt's scene description format provides multiple ways to specify spectral data, ranging from blackbody temperatures to arrays of (λ, value) pairs. A variety of useful spectral distributions are also embedded directly in the pbrt binary, including ones that describe the emission profiles of various types of light source, the scattering properties of various conductors, and the wavelength-dependent indices of refraction of various types of glass.

> pbrt 的场景描述格式提供了多种指定光谱数据的方式，从黑体温度到 (λ, value) 对的数组。各种有用的光谱分布也直接嵌入在 pbrt 二进制文件中，包括描述各种类型光源发射轮廓、各种导体散射属性以及各种类型玻璃的波长相关折射率的光谱分布。

### 4.5.4 Sampled Spectral Distributions

pbrt avoids the complexity of operating on full spectral distributions by performing spectral calculations at a set of discrete wavelengths as part of the Monte Carlo integration that is already being performed for image synthesis. Thus, we only need to be able to evaluate the integrand at specified discrete wavelengths to estimate irradiance and other quantities.

> pbrt 通过在一组离散波长处执行光谱计算来避免操作完整光谱分布的复杂性，这是已经为图像合成执行的蒙特卡洛积分的一部分。因此，我们只需要能够在指定的离散波长处求值被积函数来估计辐照度和其他量。

pbrt uses 4 wavelength samples by default; this value can easily be changed, though doing so requires recompiling the system.

> pbrt 默认使用 4 个波长样本；该值可以轻松更改，但需要重新编译系统。

```
static constexpr int NSpectrumSamples = 4;
```

#### SampledSpectrum

The SampledSpectrum class stores an array of NSpectrumSamples values that represent values of the spectral distribution at discrete wavelengths. It provides all the standard arithmetic operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division), as well as Lerp(), Sqrt(), Clamp(), Pow(), Exp() functions that operate component-wise. It is often useful to know if all the values in a SampledSpectrum are zero—for example, if a surface has zero reflectance, the light transport routines can avoid the cost of casting reflection rays.

> SampledSpectrum 类存储一个包含 NSpectrumSamples 个值的数组，表示离散波长处光谱分布的值。它提供所有标准算术运算（加、减、乘、除），以及逐分量操作的 Lerp()、Sqrt()、Clamp()、Pow()、Exp() 函数。知道 SampledSpectrum 中的所有值是否为零通常很有用——例如，如果表面的反射率为零，光传输例程可以避免投射反射光线的成本。

#### SampledWavelengths

A separate class, SampledWavelengths, stores the wavelengths for which a SampledSpectrum stores samples. It is important not only to keep careful track of the SampledWavelengths represented by an individual SampledSpectrum but also to not perform any operations that combine SampledSpectrums that have samples at different wavelengths.

> 一个单独的类 SampledWavelengths 存储 SampledSpectrum 为其存储样本的波长。不仅需要仔细跟踪单个 SampledSpectrum 所表示的 SampledWavelengths，还要避免执行任何组合在不同波长处有样本的 SampledSpectrum 的操作。

The easiest way to sample wavelengths is uniformly over a given range. The `SampleUniform()` method takes a single uniform sample u and a range of wavelengths. The first wavelength is chosen uniformly, and remaining wavelengths are chosen by taking uniform steps, wrapping around if λ_max is passed. This results in a set of stratified wavelength samples generated using a single random number.

> 采样波长最简单的方式是在给定范围内均匀采样。`SampleUniform()` 方法接受单个均匀样本 u 和波长范围。第一个波长均匀选取，其余波长通过均匀步进选取，如果超过 λ_max 则回绕。这产生了一组使用单个随机数生成的分层波长样本。

In some cases, different wavelengths of light may follow different paths after a scattering event. The most common example is when light undergoes dispersion and different wavelengths refract to different directions. When this happens, `TerminateSecondary()` terminates all but one of the wavelengths; subsequent computations can then consider the single surviving wavelength exclusively. The surviving wavelength's PDF is updated to account for the termination event by multiplying it by 1/NSpectrumSamples.

> 在某些情况下，散射事件后不同波长的光可能遵循不同的路径。最常见的例子是光发生色散，不同波长折射到不同方向。发生这种情况时，`TerminateSecondary()` 终止除一个以外的所有波长；后续计算可以只考虑存活的单个波长。存活波长的 PDF 通过乘以 1/NSpectrumSamples 来更新以考虑终止事件。

#### Discussion

Why are SampledWavelengths and SampledSpectrum separate classes, rather than a single class? In practice many SampledSpectrum objects are created during rendering, many as temporary values. It is therefore worthwhile to minimize the object's size. Our experience has been that bugs from mixing computations at different wavelengths have been rare.

> 为什么 SampledWavelengths 和 SampledSpectrum 是分开的类而不是一个类？在实践中，渲染过程中创建了许多 SampledSpectrum 对象，很多作为临时值。因此值得最小化对象的大小。我们的经验是，混合不同波长计算的 bug 很少见。
