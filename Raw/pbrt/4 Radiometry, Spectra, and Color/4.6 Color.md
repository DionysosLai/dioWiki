## 4.6 Color

"Spectral distribution" and "color" might seem like two names for the same thing, but they are distinct. A spectral distribution is a purely physical concept, while color describes the human perception of a spectrum. Color is thus closely connected to the physiology of the human visual system and the brain's processing of visual stimulus.

> "光谱分布"和"颜色"看似同一事物的两个名称，但它们是不同的。光谱分布是纯粹的物理概念，而颜色描述人类对光谱的感知。因此颜色与人类视觉系统的生理学和大脑对视觉刺激的处理密切相关。

The *tristimulus theory* of color perception says that all visible spectral distributions can be accurately represented for human observers using three scalar values. Its basis is that there are three types of photoreceptive cone cells in the eye, each sensitive to different wavelengths of light. Integrating the product of a spectral distribution with three tristimulus matching functions gives three *tristimulus values*. The matching functions thus define a *color space*.

> 颜色感知的*三刺激值理论*表明，对于人类观察者，所有可见光谱分布都可以使用三个标量值准确表示。其基础是眼睛中有三种光感受锥细胞，每种对不同波长的光敏感。将光谱分布与三个三刺激值匹配函数的乘积积分得到三个*三刺激值*。匹配函数因此定义了一个*色彩空间*。

Note that the tristimulus values for the product of two spectral distributions are *not* given by the product of their tristimulus values. This nit is why using tristimulus color like RGB for rendering may not give accurate results.

> 注意两个光谱分布乘积的三刺激值*不等于*它们各自三刺激值的乘积。这个细节就是为什么使用 RGB 等三刺激值颜色进行渲染可能无法给出准确结果的原因。

### 4.6.1 XYZ Color

An important set of color matching functions were determined by the CIE standards body. They define the *XYZ color space* (Figure 4.18). XYZ is a *device-independent* color space, meaning it does not describe the characteristics of a particular display or color measurement device.

> 一组重要的颜色匹配函数由 CIE 标准机构确定。它们定义了 *XYZ 色彩空间*（图 4.18）。XYZ 是*设备无关的*色彩空间，这意味着它不描述特定显示器或颜色测量设备的特性。

Given a spectral distribution S(λ), its XYZ color space coordinates are computed by integrating its product with the X̄(λ), Ȳ(λ), and Z̄(λ) spectral matching curves (Equation 4.22). The CIE Ȳ curve was chosen to be proportional to the spectral response curve V(λ) used to define luminance.

> 给定光谱分布 S(λ)，其 XYZ 色彩空间坐标通过将其与 X̄(λ)、Ȳ(λ) 和 Z̄(λ) 光谱匹配曲线的乘积积分来计算（方程 4.22）。CIE Ȳ 曲线被选择为与用于定义亮度的光谱响应曲线 V(λ) 成正比。

Remarkably, spectra with substantially different distributions may have very similar X, Y, and Z values. To the human observer, such spectra appear the same. Pairs of such spectra are called *metamers*.

> 令人惊奇的是，分布截然不同的光谱可能具有非常相似的 X、Y 和 Z 值。对人类观察者来说，这样的光谱看起来相同。这样的光谱对被称为*同色异谱*。

Figure 4.19: Plot of XYZ color coefficients for the wavelengths of light in the visible range. The curve is shaded with the RGB color associated with each wavelength.

> 图 4.19：可见光波长范围内 XYZ 颜色系数的曲线图。曲线用每个波长相关的 RGB 颜色着色。

#### Chromaticity and xyY Color

Color can be separated into *lightness* and *chroma*. The *chromaticity coordinates* are defined as x = X/(X+Y+Z), y = Y/(X+Y+Z). Considering just x and y, we can plot a *chromaticity diagram* (Figure 4.20). Spectra with light at just a single wavelength—the pure spectral colors—lie along the curved part. All valid colors lie inside the upside-down horseshoe shape.

> 颜色可以分为*明度*和*色度*。*色度坐标*定义为 x = X/(X+Y+Z)，y = Y/(X+Y+Z)。仅考虑 x 和 y，我们可以绘制*色度图*（图 4.20）。仅具有单一波长光的光谱——纯光谱色——沿曲线部分分布。所有有效颜色都位于倒马蹄形内部。

Figure 4.20: Chromaticity Diagram. All valid colors lie inside the shaded region.

> 图 4.20：色度图。所有有效颜色都位于着色区域内。

The xyY color space separates a color's chromaticity from its lightness. It uses the x and y chromaticity coordinates and Y from XYZ.

> xyY 色彩空间将颜色的色度与明度分离。它使用 x 和 y 色度坐标以及来自 XYZ 的 Y。

### 4.6.2 RGB Color

RGB color is used more commonly than XYZ in rendering applications. In RGB color spaces, colors are represented by a triplet of values corresponding to red, green, and blue. However, an RGB triplet on its own is meaningless; it must be defined with respect to a specific RGB color space.

> RGB 颜色在渲染应用中比 XYZ 使用得更广泛。在 RGB 色彩空间中，颜色由对应红、绿、蓝的三元组值表示。然而，单独的 RGB 三元组是无意义的；它必须相对于特定的 RGB 色彩空间来定义。

When an RGB color is shown on a display, the spectrum displayed is the weighted sum of three spectral emission curves. Figure 4.21 plots the RGB distributions emitted by an LCD and an LED display—they are remarkably different. The resulting spectral distributions from displaying the same RGB color on those displays are thus quite different as well (Figure 4.22).

> 当 RGB 颜色显示在显示器上时，显示的光谱是三条光谱发射曲线的加权和。图 4.21 绘制了 LCD 和 LED 显示器发射的 RGB 分布——它们截然不同。因此，在这些显示器上显示相同 RGB 颜色所产生的光谱分布也截然不同（图 4.22）。

It is possible to convert XYZ coefficients directly to corresponding RGB coefficients using a matrix, and pbrt frequently uses this approach to efficiently convert colors between color spaces.

> 可以使用矩阵将 XYZ 系数直接转换为对应的 RGB 系数，pbrt 经常使用这种方法高效地在色彩空间之间转换颜色。

### 4.6.3 RGB Color Spaces

A color space can be defined using chromaticity coordinates to specify three *color primaries*. The primaries define the *gamut* of the color space—the set of colors it can represent with RGB values between 0 and 1. In addition to the primaries, it is necessary to specify the color space's *whitepoint*, which is the color displayed when all three primaries are activated to their maximum. The D65 illuminant is a common choice for specifying whitepoints.

> 色彩空间可以使用色度坐标来指定三个*基色*来定义。基色定义了色彩空间的*色域*——它可以用 0 到 1 之间的 RGB 值表示的颜色集。除了基色外，还需要指定色彩空间的*白点*，即所有三个基色激活到最大时显示的颜色。D65 光源是指定白点的常见选择。

The RGBColorSpace class uses this approach, storing chromaticity coordinates of the primaries and whitepoint, plus XYZ/RGB conversion matrices.

> RGBColorSpace 类使用这种方法，存储基色和白点的色度坐标，以及 XYZ/RGB 转换矩阵。

#### Standard Color Spaces

There are a number of widely used standard color spaces:

> 有许多广泛使用的标准色彩空间：

- **sRGB**, developed in the 1990s, widely used for monitors. One of the original motivations was to standardize color on the web.

> - **sRGB**，于 1990 年代开发，广泛用于显示器。最初的动机之一是标准化网络上的颜色。

- **DCI-P3**, developed for digital film projection, covers a wider gamut than sRGB. Increasingly being adopted for computer displays and mobile phones.

> - **DCI-P3**，为数字电影投影开发，色域比 sRGB 更广。越来越多地被计算机显示器和手机采用。

- **Rec2020**, covers an even wider gamut, used in the UHDTV television standard.

> - **Rec2020**，色域更广，用于 UHDTV 电视标准。

- **ACES2065-1**, has primaries outside of the representable colors, so that all colors can be represented by it. Suitable for long-term archival storage.

> - **ACES2065-1**，基色位于可表示颜色之外，因此所有颜色都可以由它表示。适合长期存档存储。

> 图 4.23：使用色度图可视化的 sRGB、DCI-P3、Rec2020 和 ACES2065-1 色彩空间的色域。

### 4.6.4 Why Spectral Rendering?

The ability to multiply spectral values is crucial for evaluating the interaction of materials and light sources. However, component-wise multiplication of RGB values is in some sense arbitrary, because its behavior heavily depends on the chosen color space. The same RGB color can have very different values in different color spaces, and multiplication can even push colors outside the CIE horseshoe shape of physically realizable colors (Figure 4.24).

> 乘以光谱值的能力对于评估材料和光源的交互至关重要。然而，RGB 值的逐分量乘法在某种意义上是任意的，因为其行为严重依赖于所选择的色彩空间。相同的 RGB 颜色在不同的色彩空间中可能有非常不同的值，乘法甚至可能将颜色推到 CIE 马蹄形物理可实现颜色之外（图 4.24）。

Thus, rendering using a spectral model is preferable even in situations where RGB output is ultimately desired. Additional benefits include easily modeling dispersion and advanced reflectance models with natural wavelength dependence.

> 因此，即使最终需要 RGB 输出，使用光谱模型进行渲染也是更可取的。额外的好处包括轻松建模色散和具有自然波长依赖性的高级反射率模型。

### 4.6.5 Choosing the Number of Wavelength Samples

Error in the Monte Carlo estimates of color matching function integrals leads to *color noise* in images (Figure 4.25). Increasing the number of pixel samples can reduce this error, though it is more effective to associate multiple wavelength samples with each ray. The path that a ray takes through the scene is usually independent of wavelength and the incremental cost to compute lighting at multiple wavelengths is generally small.

> 颜色匹配函数积分的蒙特卡洛估计中的误差会导致图像中的*颜色噪声*（图 4.25）。增加像素样本数可以减少这种误差，但将多个波长样本与每条光线关联更有效。光线在场景中的路径通常与波长无关，在多个波长处计算光照的增量成本通常很小。

For the simple scene in Figure 4.25, 32 stratified wavelength samples is over a million times more efficient than one sample. However, for complex scenes (Figure 4.27), after eight wavelength samples, the incremental cost is not beneficial—other sources of Monte Carlo error dominate. This is why pbrt uses 4 wavelength samples by default.

> 对于图 4.25 中的简单场景，32 个分层波长样本比一个样本高效超过一百万倍。然而，对于复杂场景（图 4.27），在八个波长样本之后，增量成本没有益处——其他蒙特卡洛误差源占主导。这就是为什么 pbrt 默认使用 4 个波长样本。

### 4.6.6 From RGB to Spectra

Converting RGB colors to spectral distributions is an underspecified problem due to the existence of metamers. The approach in pbrt accounts for three fundamentally different types of spectral distributions:

> 由于同色异谱的存在，将 RGB 颜色转换为光谱分布是一个欠定问题。pbrt 中的方法考虑了三种根本不同类型的光谱分布：

- *Illuminant spectra*: nonnegative and unbounded; can be smooth or spiky.
- *Reflectance spectra*: values in [0, 1]; typically smooth in the visible range.
- *Unbounded spectra*: nonnegative and unbounded but not describing emission.

> - *光源光谱*：非负且无界；可以是平滑的或尖锐的。
> - *反射率光谱*：值在 [0, 1] 范围内；通常在可见光范围内平滑。
> - *无界光谱*：非负且无界，但不描述发射。

The conversion uses a sigmoid function applied to a quadratic polynomial s(c₀λ² + c₁λ + c₂) (Equation 4.26), which ensures energy conservation by squashing the output to [0, 1]. The three coefficients are determined by a precomputed optimization that minimizes round-trip error.

> 转换使用应用于二次多项式的 sigmoid 函数 s(c₀λ² + c₁λ + c₂)（方程 4.26），通过将输出压缩到 [0, 1] 来确保能量守恒。三个系数通过预计算优化确定，该优化最小化往返误差。

The **RGBSigmoidPolynomial** class encapsulates the coefficients and evaluates the spectral function. The **RGBToSpectrumTable** stores precomputed coefficient tables spanning the RGB color cube for each supported color space (sRGB, DCI-P3, Rec2020, ACES2065-1).

> **RGBSigmoidPolynomial** 类封装系数并求值光谱函数。**RGBToSpectrumTable** 为每个支持的色彩空间（sRGB、DCI-P3、Rec2020、ACES2065-1）存储跨越 RGB 颜色立方体的预计算系数表。

Figure 4.30: Spectra Computed from RGB Values. Plots of reflectance spectra represented by RGBSigmoidPolynomial for several RGB colors.

> 图 4.30：从 RGB 值计算的光谱。几个 RGB 颜色由 RGBSigmoidPolynomial 表示的反射率光谱曲线。

Three Spectrum classes use this machinery:

> 三个 Spectrum 类使用此机制：

- **RGBAlbedoSpectrum**: for reflectance spectra with RGB values in [0, 1]. Directly evaluates the sigmoid polynomial.

> - **RGBAlbedoSpectrum**：用于 RGB 值在 [0, 1] 范围内的反射率光谱。直接求值 sigmoid 多项式。

- **RGBUnboundedSpectrum**: for positive-valued RGB. Adds a scale factor that remaps the sigmoid polynomial's [0, 1] range. Normalizes RGB to have a maximum value of 1/2 rather than 1 for better spectral fitting.

> - **RGBUnboundedSpectrum**：用于正值 RGB。添加一个缩放因子，将 sigmoid 多项式的 [0, 1] 范围重新映射。将 RGB 归一化为最大值 1/2 而非 1，以获得更好的光谱拟合。

- **RGBIlluminantSpectrum**: multiplies the sigmoid polynomial by the color space's standard illuminant spectrum. This ensures that white objects lit by white lights lead to white pixel values. Conceptually, this resembles viewing a white reference light source through a colored film.

> - **RGBIlluminantSpectrum**：将 sigmoid 多项式乘以色彩空间的标准光源光谱。这确保了被白光照射的白色物体产生白色像素值。概念上，这类似于通过彩色滤片观看白色参考光源。
