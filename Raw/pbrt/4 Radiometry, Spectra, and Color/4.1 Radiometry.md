## 4.1 Radiometry

Radiometry provides a set of ideas and mathematical tools to describe light propagation and reflection. It forms the basis of the derivation of the rendering algorithms that will be used throughout the rest of this book. Interestingly enough, radiometry was not originally derived from first principles using the physics of light but was built on an abstraction of light based on particles flowing through space. As such, effects like polarization of light do not naturally fit into this framework, although connections have since been made between radiometry and Maxwell's equations, giving radiometry a solid basis in physics.

> 辐射度量学提供了一组描述光传播和反射的思想和数学工具。它构成了本书其余部分将使用的渲染算法推导的基础。有趣的是，辐射度量学最初并非从光的物理学第一性原理推导而来，而是建立在基于粒子在空间中流动的光的抽象之上。因此，光的偏振等效应并不自然地适合这个框架，不过后来辐射度量学与麦克斯韦方程组之间建立了联系，为辐射度量学提供了坚实的物理基础。

*Radiative transfer* is the phenomenological study of the transfer of radiant energy. It is based on radiometric principles and operates at the *geometric optics* level, where macroscopic properties of light suffice to describe how light interacts with objects much larger than the light's wavelength. It is not uncommon to incorporate phenomena from wave optics models of light, but these results need to be expressed in the language of radiative transfer's basic abstractions.

> *辐射传输*是对辐射能量传输的现象学研究。它基于辐射度量学原理，在*几何光学*层面运作，其中光的宏观性质足以描述光如何与远大于光波长的物体相互作用。将波动光学模型中的现象纳入其中并不少见，但这些结果需要用辐射传输基本抽象的语言来表达。

In this manner, it is possible to describe interactions of light with objects of approximately the same size as the wavelength of the light, and thereby model effects like dispersion and interference. At an even finer level of detail, quantum mechanics is needed to describe light's interaction with atoms. Fortunately, direct simulation of quantum mechanical principles is unnecessary for solving rendering problems in computer graphics, so the intractability of such an approach is avoided.

> 通过这种方式，可以描述光与大约相同大小的物体的相互作用，从而模拟色散和干涉等效应。在更精细的层面上，需要量子力学来描述光与原子的相互作用。幸运的是，直接模拟量子力学原理对于解决计算机图形学中的渲染问题是不必要的，因此避免了这种方法的难以处理性。

In pbrt, we will assume that geometric optics is an adequate model for the description of light and light scattering. This leads to a few basic assumptions about the behavior of light that will be used implicitly throughout the system:

> 在 pbrt 中，我们将假设几何光学是描述光和光散射的足够模型。这导致了一些关于光行为的基本假设，这些假设将在整个系统中隐式使用：

- *Linearity:* The combined effect of two inputs to an optical system is always equal to the sum of the effects of each of the inputs individually. Nonlinear scattering behavior is only observed in physical experiments involving extremely high energies, so this is generally a reasonable assumption.

> - *线性：*光学系统中两个输入的组合效果总是等于每个输入单独效果之和。非线性散射行为仅在涉及极高能量的物理实验中观察到，因此这通常是一个合理的假设。

- *Energy conservation:* When light scatters from a surface or from participating media, the scattering events can never produce more energy than they started with.

> - *能量守恒：*当光从表面或参与介质散射时，散射事件永远不会产生比初始更多的能量。

- *No polarization:* Electromagnetic radiation including visible light is *polarized*. In pbrt, we will make the common assumption that light is unpolarized—that is, a superposition of waves with many different polarizations so that only their average behavior is perceived. Therefore, the only relevant property of light is its distribution by wavelength (or, equivalently, frequency).

> - *无偏振：*包括可见光在内的电磁辐射是*偏振的*。在 pbrt 中，我们将采用常见的假设，即光是非偏振的——也就是说，是具有许多不同偏振的波的叠加，因此只能感知到它们的平均行为。因此，光唯一相关的属性是其按波长（或等价地，按频率）的分布。

- *No fluorescence or phosphorescence:* The behavior of light at one wavelength is completely independent of light's behavior at other wavelengths or times. As with polarization, it is not too difficult to include these effects if they are required.

> - *无荧光或磷光：*光在一个波长处的行为完全独立于光在其他波长或时间的行为。与偏振一样，如果需要，纳入这些效应并不太困难。

- *Steady state:* Light in the environment is assumed to have reached equilibrium, so its radiance distribution is not changing over time. This happens nearly instantaneously with light in realistic scenes, so it is not a limitation in practice.

> - *稳态：*假设环境中的光已达到平衡，因此其辐射亮度分布不随时间变化。在现实场景中，光几乎瞬间达到这一状态，因此在实践中这不是一个限制。

The most significant loss from adopting a geometric optics model is the incompatibility with diffraction and interference effects. Even though this incompatibility can be circumvented—for example, by replacing radiance with the concept of a *Wigner distribution function*—such extensions are beyond the scope of this book.

> 采用几何光学模型最大的损失是与衍射和干涉效应的不兼容。尽管这种不兼容可以被规避——例如，通过用*维格纳分布函数*的概念替代辐射亮度——但此类扩展超出了本书的范围。

### 4.1.1 Basic Quantities

There are four radiometric quantities that are central to rendering: flux, irradiance / radiant exitance, intensity, and radiance. They can each be derived from energy by successively taking limits over time, area, and directions. All of these radiometric quantities are in general wavelength dependent, though we will defer that topic until Section 4.1.3.

> 有四个对渲染至关重要的辐射度量量：通量、辐照度/辐射出射度、强度和辐射亮度。它们都可以通过对能量依次在时间、面积和方向上取极限来推导。所有这些辐射度量量通常都依赖于波长，不过我们将把这个话题推迟到第 4.1.3 节。

#### Energy

Our starting point is energy, which is measured in joules (J). Sources of illumination emit photons, each of which is at a particular wavelength and carries a particular amount of energy. All the basic radiometric quantities are effectively different ways of measuring photons. A photon at wavelength λ carries energy Q = hc/λ, where c is the speed of light and h is Planck's constant.

> 我们的出发点是能量，以焦耳 (J) 为单位。光源发射光子，每个光子都处于特定波长并携带特定数量的能量。所有基本辐射度量量实际上都是测量光子的不同方式。波长为 λ 的光子携带能量 Q = hc/λ，其中 c 是光速，h 是普朗克常数。

#### Flux

Energy measures work over some period of time, though under the steady-state assumption generally used in rendering, we are mostly interested in measuring light at an instant. *Radiant flux*, also known as *power*, is the total amount of energy passing through a surface or region of space per unit time. Its units are joules/second (J/s), or more commonly, watts (W).

> 能量衡量一段时间内的功，但在渲染中通常使用的稳态假设下，我们主要感兴趣的是在某一瞬间测量光。*辐射通量*，也称为*功率*，是每单位时间通过表面或空间区域的总能量。其单位是焦耳/秒 (J/s)，或更常用的瓦特 (W)。

Total emission from light sources is generally described in terms of flux. Figure 4.1 shows flux from a point light source measured by the total amount of energy passing through imaginary spheres around the light. Note that the total amount of flux measured on either of the two spheres is the same—although less energy is passing through any local part of the large sphere than the small sphere, the greater area of the large sphere means that the total flux is the same.

> 光源的总发射通常用通量来描述。图 4.1 显示了点光源的通量，通过围绕光源的假想球体通过的总能量来测量。注意，在两个球体上测量的总通量是相同的——虽然通过大球体任何局部部分的能量比小球体少，但大球体更大的面积意味着总通量相同。

Figure 4.1: Radiant flux, Φ, measures energy passing through a surface or region of space. Here, flux from a point light source is measured at spheres that surround the light.

> 图 4.1：辐射通量 Φ 衡量通过表面或空间区域的能量。这里，点光源的通量在围绕光源的球体上测量。

#### Irradiance and Radiant Exitance

Any measurement of flux requires an area over which photons per time is being measured. Given a finite area A, we can define the average density of power over the area by E = Φ/A. This quantity is either *irradiance* (E), the area density of flux arriving at a surface, or *radiant exitance* (M), the area density of flux leaving a surface. These measurements have units of W/m².

> 任何通量测量都需要一个面积来测量每单位时间的光子数。给定有限面积 A，我们可以定义面积上的平均功率密度 E = Φ/A。这个量要么是*辐照度* (E)，即到达表面的通量面积密度，要么是*辐射出射度* (M)，即离开表面的通量面积密度。这些量的单位是 W/m²。

For the point light source example in Figure 4.1, irradiance at a point on the outer sphere is less than the irradiance at a point on the inner sphere, since the surface area of the outer sphere is larger. In particular, if the point source is emitting the same amount of illumination in all directions, then for a sphere of radius r, E = Φ/(4πr²). This fact explains why the amount of energy received from a light at a point falls off with the squared distance from the light.

> 对于图 4.1 中的点光源示例，外球体上某点的辐照度小于内球体上某点的辐照度，因为外球体的表面积更大。特别是，如果点光源在所有方向上发射相同数量的照明，那么对于半径为 r 的球体，E = Φ/(4πr²)。这个事实解释了为什么从光源到某点接收的能量随距离的平方衰减。

The irradiance equation can also help us understand the origin of *Lambert's law*, which says that the amount of light energy arriving at a surface is proportional to the cosine of the angle between the light direction and the surface normal (Figure 4.2).

> 辐照度方程还可以帮助我们理解*朗伯定律*的起源，该定律表明到达表面的光能量与光方向和表面法线之间角度的余弦成正比（图 4.2）。

Figure 4.2: Lambert's Law. Irradiance arriving at a surface varies according to the cosine of the angle of incidence of illumination, since illumination is over a larger area at larger incident angles.

> 图 4.2：朗伯定律。到达表面的辐照度根据照明入射角的余弦变化，因为在较大的入射角下照明覆盖更大的面积。

#### Intensity

Consider now an infinitesimal light source emitting photons. If we center this light source within the unit sphere, we can compute the angular density of emitted power. *Intensity*, denoted by I, is this quantity; it has units W/sr. Intensity describes the directional distribution of light, but it is only meaningful for point light sources.

> 现在考虑一个无穷小光源发射光子。如果我们将此光源置于单位球的中心，可以计算发射功率的角密度。*强度*，用 I 表示，就是这个量；其单位是 W/sr。强度描述光的方向分布，但它仅对点光源有意义。

#### Radiance

The final, and most important, radiometric quantity is *radiance*, L. Irradiance and radiant exitance give us differential power per differential area at a point, but they do not distinguish the directional distribution of power. Radiance takes this last step and measures irradiance or radiant exitance with respect to solid angles. Radiance is the flux density per unit area, per unit solid angle.

> 最后一个，也是最重要的辐射度量量是*辐射亮度* L。辐照度和辐射出射度给出了某点处每单位微分面积的微分功率，但它们不区分功率的方向分布。辐射亮度迈出了最后一步，相对于立体角测量辐照度或辐射出射度。辐射亮度是每单位面积、每单位立体角的通量密度。

Figure 4.3: Radiance is defined as flux per unit solid angle per unit projected area.

> 图 4.3：辐射亮度定义为每单位立体角每单位投影面积的通量。

Of all of these radiometric quantities, radiance will be the one used most frequently throughout the rest of the book. An intuitive reason for this is that in some sense it is the most fundamental of all the radiometric quantities; if radiance is given, then all the other values can be computed in terms of integrals of radiance over areas and directions. Another nice property of radiance is that it remains constant along rays through empty space. It is thus a natural quantity to compute with ray tracing.

> 在所有这些辐射度量量中，辐射亮度将是本书其余部分最常使用的。一个直觉的原因是，在某种意义上它是所有辐射度量量中最基本的；如果给定辐射亮度，则所有其他值都可以通过辐射亮度在面积和方向上的积分来计算。辐射亮度的另一个好特性是它沿穿过空旷空间的光线保持恒定。因此它是用光线追踪计算的自然量。

### 4.1.2 Incident and Exitant Radiance Functions

When light interacts with surfaces in the scene, the radiance function is generally not continuous across the surface boundaries. In the most extreme case of a fully opaque surface (e.g., a mirror), the radiance function slightly above and slightly below a surface could be completely unrelated.

> 当光与场景中的表面交互时，辐射亮度函数通常在表面边界上不连续。在完全不透明表面（例如镜子）的最极端情况下，表面略上方和略下方的辐射亮度函数可能完全不相关。

We prefer to solve this ambiguity by making a distinction between radiance arriving at the point (e.g., due to illumination from a light source) and radiance leaving that point (e.g., due to reflection from a surface). The function that describes the arriving radiance is denoted by Lᵢ, and the outgoing reflected radiance is denoted by Lₒ. Note that in both cases the direction vector ω is oriented to point away from the surface point p.

> 我们倾向于通过区分到达该点的辐射亮度（例如，由于光源的照明）和离开该点的辐射亮度（例如，由于表面的反射）来解决这种歧义。描述到达辐射亮度的函数用 Lᵢ 表示，出射反射辐射亮度用 Lₒ 表示。注意，在两种情况下，方向向量 ω 都朝向远离表面点 p 的方向。

Figure 4.4: (a) The incident radiance function Lᵢ describes the distribution of radiance arriving at a point as a function of position and direction. (b) The exitant radiance function Lₒ gives the distribution of radiance leaving the point.

> 图 4.4：(a) 入射辐射亮度函数 Lᵢ 描述了作为位置和方向函数到达某点的辐射亮度分布。(b) 出射辐射亮度函数 Lₒ 给出了离开该点的辐射亮度分布。

Another property to keep in mind is that at a point in space where there is no surface (i.e., in free space), the radiance is continuous, so Lᵢ and Lₒ only differ by a direction reversal.

> 另一个需要记住的属性是，在空间中没有表面的点（即在自由空间中），辐射亮度是连续的，因此 Lᵢ 和 Lₒ 仅相差一个方向反转。

### 4.1.3 Radiometric Spectral Distributions

Thus far, all the radiometric quantities have been defined without considering variation in their distribution over wavelengths. They have therefore effectively been the integrals of wavelength-dependent quantities over an (unspecified) range of wavelengths of interest. Just as we were able to define the various radiometric quantities in terms of limits of other quantities, we can also define their spectral variants by taking their limits over small wavelength ranges.

> 到目前为止，所有辐射度量量都是在不考虑其波长分布变化的情况下定义的。因此，它们实际上是波长相关量在（未指定的）感兴趣波长范围上的积分。正如我们能够用其他量的极限来定义各种辐射度量量一样，我们也可以通过在小波长范围上取极限来定义它们的光谱变体。

For example, we can define *spectral radiance* as the limit of radiance over an infinitesimal interval of wavelengths. In turn, radiance can be found by integrating spectral radiance over a range of wavelengths. Definitions for the other radiometric quantities follow similarly. All of these spectral variants have an additional factor of 1/m in their units.

> 例如，我们可以将*光谱辐射亮度*定义为辐射亮度在无穷小波长区间上的极限。反过来，辐射亮度可以通过在波长范围上积分光谱辐射亮度来获得。其他辐射度量量的定义类似。所有这些光谱变体的单位中都有额外的 1/m 因子。

### 4.1.4 Luminance and Photometry

All the radiometric measurements like flux, radiance, and so forth have corresponding photometric measurements. *Photometry* is the study of visible electromagnetic radiation in terms of its perception by the human visual system. Each spectral radiometric quantity can be converted to its corresponding photometric quantity by integrating against the spectral response curve V(λ), which describes the relative sensitivity of the human eye to various wavelengths.

> 所有辐射度量测量（如通量、辐射亮度等）都有对应的光度测量。*光度学*是根据人类视觉系统的感知来研究可见电磁辐射的学科。每个光谱辐射度量量都可以通过与光谱响应曲线 V(λ) 积分来转换为其对应的光度量，V(λ) 描述了人眼对各种波长的相对灵敏度。

*Luminance* measures how bright a spectral power distribution appears to a human observer. For example, luminance accounts for the fact that a spectral distribution with a particular amount of energy in the green wavelengths will appear brighter to a human than a spectral distribution with the same amount of energy in blue.

> *亮度*测量光谱功率分布对人类观察者来说有多亮。例如，亮度考虑了这样一个事实：在绿色波长处具有特定能量的光谱分布对人类来说会比在蓝色波长处具有相同能量的光谱分布看起来更亮。

Luminance and the spectral response curve are closely related to the XYZ representation of color, which will be introduced in Section 4.6.1.

> 亮度和光谱响应曲线与颜色的 XYZ 表示密切相关，这将在第 4.6.1 节中介绍。
