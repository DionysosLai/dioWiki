## 4.2 Working with Radiometric Integrals

A frequent task in rendering is the evaluation of integrals of radiometric quantities. In this section, we will present some tricks that can make it easier to do this. To illustrate the use of these techniques, we will take the computation of irradiance at a point as an example. Irradiance at a point p with surface normal n due to radiance over a set of directions Ω is given by Equation (4.7), where the cosθ factor in the integrand is due to the cosθ factor in the definition of radiance.

> 渲染中的一项常见任务是计算辐射度量量的积分。在本节中，我们将介绍一些可以使这项工作更容易的技巧。为了说明这些技术的使用，我们将以计算某点处辐照度为例。由方向集合 Ω 上的辐射亮度引起的、表面法线为 n 的点 p 处的辐照度由方程 (4.7) 给出，其中被积函数中的 cosθ 因子来自辐射亮度定义中的 cosθ 因子。

> 图 4.5：某点处的辐照度由辐射亮度乘以入射方向余弦在该点上方整个上半球上的积分给出。

### 4.2.1 Integrals over Projected Solid Angle

The various cosine factors in the integrals for radiometric quantities can often distract from what is being expressed in the integral. This problem can be avoided using *projected solid angle* rather than solid angle to measure areas subtended by objects being integrated over. The projected solid angle subtended by an object is determined by projecting the object onto the unit sphere, as was done for the solid angle, but then projecting the resulting shape down onto the unit disk that is perpendicular to the surface normal (Figure 4.6).

> 辐射度量量积分中的各种余弦因子经常会分散对积分所表达内容的注意力。这个问题可以通过使用*投影立体角*而不是立体角来测量被积分对象所张的面积来避免。一个物体所张的投影立体角通过将物体投影到单位球上来确定（如同立体角一样），但随后将所得形状向下投影到垂直于表面法线的单位圆盘上（图 4.6）。

Figure 4.6: The projected solid angle subtended by an object is the cosine-weighted solid angle that it subtends. It can be computed by finding the object's solid angle, projecting it down to the plane perpendicular to the surface normal, and measuring its area there.

> 图 4.6：一个物体所张的投影立体角是其所张的余弦加权立体角。它可以通过求出物体的立体角，将其投影到垂直于表面法线的平面上，然后测量其面积来计算。

The projected solid angle measure is related to the solid angle measure by dω⊥ = |cosθ| dω, so the irradiance integral over the hemisphere can be written more simply. For the rest of this book, we will write integrals over directions in terms of solid angle, rather than projected solid angle.

> 投影立体角测度与立体角测度的关系为 dω⊥ = |cosθ| dω，因此半球上的辐照度积分可以更简洁地书写。在本书的其余部分，我们将以立体角而非投影立体角来书写方向上的积分。

### 4.2.2 Integrals over Spherical Coordinates

It is often convenient to transform integrals over solid angle into integrals over spherical coordinates (θ, φ). The differential area on the unit sphere is the product of the differential lengths of its sides, sinθ dθ and dφ. Therefore, dω = sinθ dθ dφ (Equation 4.8).

> 将立体角上的积分转换为球面坐标 (θ, φ) 上的积分通常很方便。单位球上的微分面积是其两边微分长度 sinθ dθ 和 dφ 的乘积。因此，dω = sinθ dθ dφ（方程 4.8）。

Figure 4.7: The differential area subtended by a differential solid angle is the product of the differential lengths of the two edges, sinθ dθ and dφ. The resulting relationship, dω = sinθ dθ dφ, is the key to converting between integrals over solid angles and integrals over spherical angles.

> 图 4.7：微分立体角所张的微分面积是两条边的微分长度 sinθ dθ 和 dφ 的乘积。由此得到的关系 dω = sinθ dθ dφ 是在立体角积分和球面角积分之间转换的关键。

If the radiance is the same from all directions, the irradiance integral simplifies to E = πL.

> 如果所有方向的辐射亮度相同，辐照度积分简化为 E = πL。

### 4.2.3 Integrals over Area

One last useful transformation is to turn integrals over directions into integrals over area. Differential area dA on a surface is related to differential solid angle dω as viewed from a point p by dω = dA cosθ' / r² (Equation 4.9), where θ' is the angle between the surface normal of dA and the vector to p, and r is the distance from p to dA.

> 最后一种有用的变换是将方向上的积分转化为面积上的积分。从点 p 观察时，表面上的微分面积 dA 与微分立体角 dω 的关系为 dω = dA cosθ' / r²（方程 4.9），其中 θ' 是 dA 的表面法线与指向 p 的向量之间的角度，r 是 p 到 dA 的距离。

Figure 4.8: The differential solid angle subtended by a differential area dA is equal to dA cosθ / r², where θ is the angle between dA's surface normal and the vector to the point p, and r is the distance from p to dA.

> 图 4.8：微分面积 dA 所张的微分立体角等于 dA cosθ / r²，其中 θ 是 dA 的表面法线与指向点 p 的向量之间的角度，r 是 p 到 dA 的距离。

Therefore, for a quadrilateral light source, the irradiance integral can be rewritten as an integral over the area of the quadrilateral, where Lₑ is the emitted radiance from the surface of the quadrilateral, θ is the angle between the surface normal at p and the direction from p to the point on the light, and θ' is the angle between the surface normal on the light and the direction from the light to p (Figure 4.9).

> 因此，对于一个四边形光源，辐照度积分可以重写为在四边形面积上的积分，其中 Lₑ 是四边形表面的发射辐射亮度，θ 是 p 处表面法线与从 p 指向光源上某点方向之间的角度，θ' 是光源上的表面法线与从光源指向 p 方向之间的角度（图 4.9）。

Figure 4.9: To compute irradiance at a point p from a quadrilateral source, it is easier to integrate over the surface area of the source than to integrate over the irregular set of directions that it subtends. The relationship between solid angles and areas given by Equation (4.9) lets us go back and forth between the two approaches.

> 图 4.9：要计算四边形光源在点 p 处产生的辐照度，在光源的表面积上积分比在其所张的不规则方向集上积分更容易。方程 (4.9) 给出的立体角与面积之间的关系让我们可以在两种方法之间来回转换。
