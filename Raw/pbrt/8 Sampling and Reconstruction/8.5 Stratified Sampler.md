## 8.5 Stratified Sampler

The IndependentSampler's weakness is that it makes no effort to ensure good coverage of the sampling domain. The StratifiedSampler applies stratification: subdividing the sampling domain into regions and generating a single sample inside each one. Because a sample is taken in each region, important features in the integrand are less likely to be missed.

> IndependentSampler 的弱点在于它不努力确保采样域的良好覆盖。StratifiedSampler 应用分层策略：将采样域细分为区域，并在每个区域内生成一个样本。由于在每个区域都取了样本，被积函数中的重要特征不太可能被遗漏。

The StratifiedSampler places each sample at a random point inside each stratum by jittering the center point with a uniform random offset. The nonuniformity from jittering helps turn aliasing into noise (Section 8.1.6). It also offers an unjittered mode for comparisons.

> StratifiedSampler 通过用均匀随机偏移抖动中心点，将每个样本放置在每个层内的随机位置。抖动产生的非均匀性有助于将走样转化为噪声（8.1.6 节）。它还提供非抖动模式用于比较。

Direct application of stratification to high-dimensional sampling quickly leads to an intractable number of samples—for example, 4 strata in each of 5 dimensions gives 4^5 = 1024 samples per pixel. This is the *curse of dimensionality*. The solution is *padding*: computing lower-dimensional stratified patterns for subsets of dimensions and randomly associating samples from each set.

> 直接将分层应用于高维采样会迅速导致不可处理的采样数量——例如 5 个维度各 4 层产生 4^5 = 1024 个每像素样本。这就是**维数灾难**。解决方案是**填充**：为维度子集计算低维分层模式，然后随机关联每组的采样。

The StratifiedSampler constructor takes x and y pixel sample counts, a jitter flag, and a random seed. The total samples per pixel is xPixelSamples × yPixelSamples. To generate stratified samples without explicitly representing all values, it uses `PermutationElement()` to perform random permutation of the sample index to determine which stratum to sample—a shuffling operation essential for padding to prevent correlation between dimensions.

> StratifiedSampler 构造函数接受 x 和 y 像素采样数、抖动标志和随机种子。每像素总采样数为 xPixelSamples × yPixelSamples。为了在不显式表示所有值的情况下生成分层样本，它使用 `PermutationElement()` 对采样索引执行随机排列以确定要采样的层——这是填充所必需的打乱操作，防止维度间的相关性。

For 1D samples: the permuted stratum index plus a random jitter offset (or 0.5 if unjittered), divided by total strata count, gives the sample value. For 2D samples: the stratum index is decomposed into x and y stratum coordinates, each jittered and divided by the respective dimension's strata count.

> 对于 1D 采样：排列后的层索引加上随机抖动偏移（或非抖动时为 0.5），除以总层数，给出采样值。对于 2D 采样：层索引被分解为 x 和 y 层坐标，各自抖动并除以相应维度的层数。

With n×n-dimensional stratification, the star discrepancy of jittered points is O(N^{-1/2-1/(2d)}) (Equation 8.17), which means stratified samples do not qualify as low discrepancy. The PSD of 2D stratified samples has low power at low frequencies and fairly constant power at higher frequencies, making it effective at transforming aliasing into high-frequency noise.

> 对于 n×n 维分层，抖动点的星偏差为 O(N^{-1/2-1/(2d)})（公式 8.17），这意味着分层采样不符合低偏差标准。二维分层采样的 PSD 在低频处功率低，在高频处功率相当恒定，使其能有效地将走样转化为高频噪声。
