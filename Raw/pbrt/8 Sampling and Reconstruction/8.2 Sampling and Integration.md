## 8.2 Sampling and Integration

The lighting integration algorithms used throughout pbrt are based on Monte Carlo integration, yet Section 8.1 focused on sampling and reconstruction. That topic is important for understanding aliasing and image reconstruction filters, but it is a different one than minimizing Monte Carlo integration error. There are a number of connections between Monte Carlo and both Fourier analysis and other approaches to analyzing point-sampling algorithms, however. For example, jittered sampling is a form of stratified sampling, a variance reduction technique introduced in Section 2.2.1.

> pbrt 中使用的光照积分算法基于蒙特卡洛积分，但 8.1 节侧重于采样和重建。该主题对理解走样和图像重建滤波器很重要，但与最小化蒙特卡洛积分误差是不同的问题。然而，蒙特卡洛与傅里叶分析及其他分析点采样算法的方法之间存在许多联系。例如，抖动采样是分层采样的一种形式——2.2.1 节介绍的方差缩减技术。

This chapter presents 6 classes implementing the Sampler interface, offering a total of 17 different techniques. The question of which sampling technique is best is not easily answered—not only do the various mathematical approaches often disagree, but the human visual system adds another dimension: sampling patterns with blue noise error characteristics are visually preferable, yet may not have lower numeric error.

> 本章介绍了 6 个实现 Sampler 接口的类，共提供 17 种不同技术。哪种采样技术最好的问题不容易回答——不仅各种数学方法经常存在分歧，人类视觉系统还增加了另一个维度：具有蓝噪声误差特征的采样模式在视觉上更受欢迎，但数值误差不一定更低。

### 8.2.1 Fourier Analysis of Variance

Fourier analysis can be applied to evaluate sampling patterns in the context of Monte Carlo integration, leading to insights about both variance and convergence rates. The key simplifications assumed here: (1) uniformly distributed, equally weighted sample points, (2) unbiased estimator, (3) homogeneous properties with respect to toroidal translation.

> 傅里叶分析可以应用于在蒙特卡洛积分的背景下评估采样模式，从而洞察方差和收敛率。此处假设的关键简化：(1) 均匀分布的等权采样点，(2) 无偏估计量，(3) 关于环面平移的齐次性质。

Using the *Fourier series* representation (Equations 8.8–8.9) instead of the continuous Fourier transform (to avoid boundary discontinuity issues from the Gibbs phenomenon), the Monte Carlo estimator can be rewritten as a continuous integral (Equation 8.10). The error in the estimate is given by Equation (8.11): it is a sum over all nonzero frequency products of the function's and sampling pattern's Fourier coefficients.

> 使用**傅里叶级数**表示（公式 8.8–8.9）而非连续傅里叶变换（以避免吉布斯现象导致的边界不连续问题），蒙特卡洛估计量可以重写为连续积分（公式 8.10）。估计误差由公式 (8.11) 给出：它是函数与采样模式的傅里叶系数在所有非零频率处的乘积之和。

Equation (8.11) is the key result. If f is band limited and the sampling rate equals f's highest frequency, then a zero variance estimator results—only half the sampling rate is necessary for perfect integration compared to perfect reconstruction!

> 公式 (8.11) 是关键结果。如果 f 是带限的，且采样率等于 f 的最高频率，则会产生零方差估计量——与完美重建相比，完美积分只需要一半的采样率！

The variance (Equation 8.12) is the sum of products of f's and the sampling pattern's PSDs. This gives a clear direction: the power spectrum of the sampling pattern should be low where the function's power spectrum is high. Assuming the function has most energy in lower frequencies, this argues for a sampling pattern with energy concentrated in higher frequencies—precisely the blue noise criterion.

> 方差（公式 8.12）是 f 的 PSD 与采样模式的 PSD 的乘积之和。这给出了明确的方向：采样模式的功率谱应该在函数功率谱较高的地方较低。假设函数的大部分能量在低频，这要求采样模式的能量集中在高频——恰好是蓝噪声准则。

With uniform random sampling (white noise), the PSD is constant 1/N, leading to the standard Monte Carlo variance σ²[f]/N. More generally, if the PSD can be asymptotically bounded, higher rates of variance reduction are achievable—for example, jittered sampling in 2D can achieve O(1/N^(1+2/d)) variance for smooth integrands.

> 对于均匀随机采样（白噪声），PSD 为常数 1/N，导致标准蒙特卡洛方差 σ²[f]/N。更一般地，如果 PSD 可以渐近有界，则可以实现更高的方差缩减率——例如，二维抖动采样对于光滑被积函数可以实现 O(1/N^(1+2/d)) 方差。

Fourier analysis has revealed that *Poisson disk* sampling patterns (where no two points can be closer than some minimum distance) have unexpectedly bad asymptotic convergence—never better than O(1/N), worse than jittered points. Although their PSD has low energy for a larger range of frequencies, it retains a small amount of energy all the way to 0, unlike jittered patterns.

> 傅里叶分析揭示了 **Poisson disk** 采样模式（任意两点间距不小于某最小距离）具有出乎意料的糟糕渐近收敛——永远不会好于 O(1/N)，比抖动点更差。虽然其 PSD 在更大的频率范围内能量较低，但一直到 0 频率都保留了少量能量，与抖动模式不同。

### 8.2.2 Low Discrepancy and Quasi Monte Carlo

Another approach for evaluating sample quality is based on *discrepancy*. The basic idea is that a given fraction of the volume should have roughly the same fraction of total sample points inside it. The *star discrepancy* D*_N uses boxes with one corner at the origin (Equation 8.13).

> 评估采样质量的另一种方法基于**偏差**。基本思想是给定体积的比例应当大致包含总采样点数的相同比例。**星偏差** D*_N 使用一角在原点的盒子（公式 8.13）。

The optimal 1D shifted regular sequence (Equation 8.14) has discrepancy D*_N = O(1/N), the lowest possible for a 1D sequence. An interesting note: by the discrepancy measure, the uniform 1D sequence is optimal, yet Fourier analysis indicated jittering was superior—a different perspective on the same problem.

> 最优的一维平移规则序列（公式 8.14）的偏差 D*_N = O(1/N)，是一维序列可能的最低值。有趣的是：按偏差度量，均匀一维序列是最优的，但傅里叶分析表明抖动更优——这是对同一问题的不同视角。

A d-dimensional sequence has *low discrepancy* if its discrepancy is O((log N)^d / N) (Equation 8.15). Low-discrepancy point sets used for integration lead to *quasi–Monte Carlo* (QMC) methods. The *Koksma–Hlawka inequality* (Equation 8.16) relates discrepancy to integration error: the error bound involves both the discrepancy and the *total variation* of the function.

> 如果 d 维序列的偏差为 O((log N)^d / N)（公式 8.15），则称其具有**低偏差**。用于积分的低偏差点集引出**准蒙特卡洛** (QMC) 方法。**Koksma–Hlawka 不等式**（公式 8.16）将偏差与积分误差联系起来：误差界涉及偏差和函数的**全变差**。

As dimensionality increases, the integration error with low discrepancy approaches O((log N)^d / N), asymptotically much better than O(1/√N) from Monte Carlo. In practice, QMC usually has even better convergence. Randomized sample points that preserve low discrepancy lead to *randomized quasi–Monte Carlo* (RQMC) methods, which allow the use of variance. RQMC is the foundation of most of pbrt's Monte Carlo integration algorithms.

> 随着维数增加，低偏差的积分误差趋近于 O((log N)^d / N)，渐近地远好于蒙特卡洛的 O(1/√N)。实际上，QMC 通常具有更好的收敛性。保持低偏差的随机化采样点产生**随机化准蒙特卡洛** (RQMC) 方法，允许使用方差。RQMC 是 pbrt 大多数蒙特卡洛积分算法的基础。
