## 8.1 Sampling Theory

A digital image is represented as a set of pixel values, typically aligned on a rectangular grid. When a digital image is displayed on a physical device, these values are used to determine the spectral power emitted by pixels on the display. When thinking about digital images, it is important to differentiate between image pixels, which represent the value of a function at a particular sample location, and display pixels, which are physical objects that emit light with some spatial and directional distribution. Displays use the image pixel values to construct a new image function over the display surface. This function is defined at all points on the display, not just the infinitesimal points of the digital image's pixels. This process of taking a collection of sample values and converting them back to a continuous function is called *reconstruction*.

> 数字图像表示为一组像素值，通常排列在矩形网格上。当数字图像在物理设备上显示时，这些值用于确定显示器像素发出的光谱功率。思考数字图像时，区分图像像素和显示像素很重要：图像像素表示函数在特定采样位置的值，而显示像素是发出具有一定空间和方向分布光线的物理对象。显示器使用图像像素值在显示表面上构建新的图像函数。该函数定义在显示器的所有点上，而不仅是数字图像像素的无穷小点。将一组采样值转换回连续函数的过程称为**重建**。

In order to compute the discrete pixel values in the digital image, it is necessary to sample the original continuously defined image function. In pbrt, like most other ray-tracing renderers, the only way to get information about the image function is to sample it by tracing rays. While an image could be generated by just sampling the function precisely at the pixel positions, a better result can be obtained by taking more samples at different positions and incorporating this additional information into the final pixel values. Indeed, for the best quality result, the pixel values should be computed such that the reconstructed image on the display device is as close as possible to the original image of the scene on the virtual camera's film plane.

> 为了计算数字图像中的离散像素值，必须对原始连续定义的图像函数进行采样。在 pbrt 中，与大多数其他光线追踪渲染器一样，获取图像函数信息的唯一方法是通过追踪光线来采样它。虽然可以仅在像素位置精确采样来生成图像，但在不同位置取更多样本并将这些额外信息纳入最终像素值，可以得到更好的结果。实际上，为获得最佳质量，像素值的计算应使显示设备上重建的图像尽可能接近虚拟相机胶片平面上的场景原始图像。

Because the sampling and reconstruction process involves approximation, it introduces error known as *aliasing*, which can manifest itself in many ways, including jagged edges or flickering in animations. These errors occur because the sampling process is not able to capture all the information from the continuously defined image function.

> 由于采样和重建过程涉及近似，它会引入称为**走样**的误差，表现形式多样，包括锯齿状边缘或动画中的闪烁。这些误差的产生是因为采样过程无法捕获连续定义的图像函数的全部信息。

As an example, consider a 1D function where we can evaluate at any desired location. Each such location is called a *sample position*, and the value is the *sample value*. Figure 8.1 shows a set of samples of a smooth 1D function, along with a reconstructed signal that approximates the original function using piecewise linear interpolation (reconstruction with a hat function).

> 例如，考虑一个一维函数，我们可以在任意所需位置对其求值。每个这样的位置称为**采样位置**，其值称为**采样值**。图 8.1 展示了一个光滑一维函数的一组采样，以及使用分段线性插值（用帽函数重建）来近似原始函数的重建信号。

*Fourier analysis* can be used to evaluate the quality of the match between the reconstructed function and the original. This section will introduce the main ideas of Fourier analysis with enough detail to work through some parts of the sampling and reconstruction processes.

> **傅里叶分析**可用于评估重建函数与原始函数之间的匹配质量。本节将介绍傅里叶分析的主要思想，提供足够的细节来处理采样和重建过程的某些部分。

### 8.1.1 The Frequency Domain and the Fourier Transform

One of the foundations of Fourier analysis is the Fourier transform, which represents a function in the *frequency domain*. (We will say that functions are normally expressed in the *spatial domain*.) A more slowly varying function is said to have lower-frequency content.

> 傅里叶分析的基础之一是傅里叶变换，它在**频域**中表示函数。（我们说函数通常在**空间域**中表达。）变化较慢的函数被认为具有较低的频率内容。

Most functions can be decomposed into a weighted sum of shifted sinusoids. This remarkable fact was first described by Joseph Fourier, and the Fourier transform converts a function into this representation. The frequency space representation gives insight into some of a function's characteristics—the distribution of frequencies in the sine functions corresponds to the distribution of frequencies in the original function.

> 大多数函数可以分解为平移正弦波的加权和。这一非凡事实由 Joseph Fourier 首先描述，傅里叶变换将函数转换为这种表示。频域表示能洞察函数的某些特征——正弦函数中的频率分布对应于原始函数中的频率分布。

The Fourier transform of a 1D function is given by Equation (8.1), involving complex exponentials. For even functions, the Fourier transform has no imaginary terms. The new function is a function of frequency ω. The Fourier transform is a linear operator and generalizes to multidimensional functions.

### 8.1.2 Ideal Sampling and Reconstruction

The sampling process formally corresponds to multiplying the function by a "shah" (impulse train) function—an infinite sum of equally spaced delta functions with period T defining the *sampling rate*. The multiplication yields an infinite sequence of values of the function at equally spaced points.

> 采样过程在形式上对应于将函数乘以"shah"（脉冲列）函数——一个等间距 delta 函数的无限求和，其周期 T 定义了**采样率**。乘法产生函数在等间距点上的无限值序列。

The sampled values can be used to define a reconstructed function by choosing a reconstruction filter and computing the *convolution*. For reconstruction, convolution gives a weighted sum of scaled instances of the reconstruction filter centered at the sample points.

> 采样值可用于通过选择重建滤波器并计算**卷积**来定义重建函数。对于重建，卷积给出以采样点为中心的重建滤波器缩放实例的加权和。

In the frequency domain, the product of two functions corresponds to the convolution of their Fourier transforms (Equation 8.3), and convolution in the spatial domain is equivalent to multiplication in the frequency domain (Equation 8.4). The Fourier transform of a shah function with period T is another shah function with period 1/T. This reciprocal relationship is important: if samples are farther apart in the spatial domain, they are closer together in the frequency domain.

> 在频域中，两个函数的乘积对应于它们傅里叶变换的卷积（公式 8.3），空间域中的卷积等价于频域中的乘法（公式 8.4）。周期为 T 的 shah 函数的傅里叶变换是另一个周期为 1/T 的 shah 函数。这种倒数关系很重要：如果采样在空间域中间距更大，它们在频域中就更密集。

Convolving a function with a shah yields an infinite sequence of copies of the original function's spectrum. To reconstruct the original function, we just discard all spectrum copies except the one centered at the origin by multiplying with a box function of appropriate width. In the spatial domain, this corresponds to convolution with the sinc function (Equation 8.5). This is ideal sampling and reconstruction—we can determine the exact frequency space representation purely by sampling at regularly spaced points.

> 将函数与 shah 卷积会产生原始函数频谱的无限个副本序列。要重建原始函数，只需通过乘以适当宽度的 box 函数来丢弃除原点处以外的所有频谱副本。在空间域中，这对应于与 sinc 函数的卷积（公式 8.5）。这就是理想的采样与重建——我们可以仅通过在规则间距点上采样来确定精确的频域表示。

Unfortunately, because the sinc function has infinite extent, it is necessary to use all sample values to compute any particular reconstructed value. Filters with finite spatial extent are preferable for practical implementations. A commonly used alternative—the box function for reconstruction—is a poor choice, as it does a bad job of isolating the central copy of the function's spectrum.

> 不幸的是，由于 sinc 函数具有无限支撑，计算任何特定重建值都需要使用所有采样值。具有有限空间支撑的滤波器在实际实现中更为可取。常用的替代方案——用 box 函数进行重建——是一个糟糕的选择，因为它在隔离函数频谱的中心副本方面表现很差。

### 8.1.3 Aliasing

One of the most serious practical problems with ideal sampling and reconstruction is the assumption that the signal is band limited. For signals that are not band limited or not sampled at a sufficiently high rate, the spectrum copies overlap and can no longer be cleanly separated. The resulting high-frequency details leaking into lower-frequency regions are called *aliases*, and the signal is said to be *aliased*. Sampling artifacts are called *prealiasing* and reconstruction artifacts *postaliasing*; any attempt to fix these errors is *antialiasing*.

> 理想采样与重建最严重的实际问题之一是假设信号是带限的。对于非带限信号或采样率不够高的信号，频谱副本会重叠，无法被干净地分离。由此产生的高频细节泄漏到低频区域被称为**走样**，信号被称为**混叠的**。采样伪影称为**预走样**，重建伪影称为**后走样**；修复这些误差的任何尝试都是**反走样**。

The *sampling theorem* says that as long as the frequency of uniformly spaced sample points is greater than twice the maximum frequency present in the signal, it is possible to reconstruct the original signal perfectly. This minimum sampling frequency is called the *Nyquist frequency*. However, increasing the sampling rate is expensive in a ray tracer, and for non-band-limited signals, perfect reconstruction is impossible at any sampling rate.

> **采样定理**指出，只要均匀间隔采样点的频率大于信号中最高频率的两倍，就可以完美重建原始信号。这个最小采样频率称为**奈奎斯特频率**。然而，在光线追踪器中提高采样率代价高昂，而对于非带限信号，无论采样率多高都不可能完美重建。

Few of the interesting functions in computer graphics are band limited. Any function containing a discontinuity cannot be band limited, and therefore we cannot perfectly sample and reconstruct it. Thus, it is necessary to apply different methods besides just increasing the sampling rate.

> 计算机图形学中很少有有趣的函数是带限的。任何包含不连续的函数都不可能是带限的，因此我们无法完美地采样和重建它。因此，有必要采用除提高采样率之外的其他方法。

### 8.1.4 Understanding Pixels

The word "pixel" is used to refer to two different things: physical elements in displays/cameras and regular samples of an image function. The pixels that constitute an image are defined to be point samples at discrete points on the image plane; there is no "area" associated with an image pixel. As Alvy Ray Smith (1995) has emphatically pointed out, thinking of image pixels as small squares with finite area is an incorrect mental model.

> "像素"一词用于指代两种不同的东西：显示器/相机中的物理元件和图像函数的规则采样。构成图像的像素被定义为图像平面上离散点处的点采样；图像像素没有关联的"面积"。正如 Alvy Ray Smith (1995) 强调指出的，将图像像素视为具有有限面积的小方块是一种错误的心智模型。

pbrt adopts the convention of truncating continuous coordinates to discrete coordinates, so the range of continuous coordinates for the discrete range [0, n) is naturally [0, n), and the resulting code is much simpler (Heckbert 1990a).

> pbrt 采用截断连续坐标到离散坐标的约定，因此离散范围 [0, n) 的连续坐标范围自然为 [0, n)，由此产生的代码更简洁 (Heckbert 1990a)。

### 8.1.5 Sampling and Aliasing in Rendering

The image function can be generalized to a higher-dimensional function that also depends on the time and lens position at which it is sampled. Even more generally, because integrators use Monte Carlo integration, we have an even higher-dimensional image function including sample values used by the integrator. Sampling all of these dimensions well is important for generating high-quality imagery efficiently.

> 图像函数可以推广为一个更高维的函数，还取决于采样的时间和镜头位置。更一般地，由于积分器使用蒙特卡洛积分，我们有一个更高维的图像函数，包括积分器使用的采样值。良好地采样所有这些维度对于高效生成高质量图像非常重要。

**Sources of Aliasing**: Geometry is one of the most common causes—object boundaries introduce step functions with infinite frequency content. The perfect sinc reconstruction filter causes *Gibbs phenomenon* ringing artifacts with aliased samples. Very small objects can cause geometric aliasing by falling between samples. Texture and material highlights cause *shading aliasing*, and sharp shadows introduce step functions. We can never remove all aliasing sources, so we must develop mitigation techniques.

> **走样来源**：几何是最常见的原因之一——物体边界引入具有无限频率内容的阶跃函数。完美的 sinc 重建滤波器在混叠采样时产生**吉布斯现象**振铃伪影。非常小的物体可能因落在采样之间而导致几何走样。纹理和材质高光导致**着色走样**，尖锐阴影引入阶跃函数。我们永远无法消除所有走样来源，因此必须开发缓解技术。

**Adaptive Sampling**: One approach is adaptive supersampling—take additional samples in regions with high-frequency content. However, adjacent sample values cannot tell us with certainty what is happening between them, making this difficult to implement well.

> **自适应采样**：一种方法是自适应超采样——在高频内容区域取更多样本。然而，相邻采样值无法确定地告诉我们它们之间发生了什么，因此很难实现好。

**Prefiltering**: Another approach is to blur the original function so that no high frequencies remain that cannot be captured at the sampling rate. This corresponds to convolving with a sinc-like filter in the spatial domain. While this changes the function by removing information, blurring is generally less objectionable than aliasing.

> **预滤波**：另一种方法是模糊原始函数，使采样率无法捕获的高频不再存在。这对应于在空间域与类 sinc 滤波器进行卷积。虽然这通过移除信息改变了函数，但模糊通常比走样更不令人反感。

### 8.1.6 Spectral Analysis of Sampling Patterns

For stochastic sampling methods where sample positions are specified by random variables, the *power spectral density* (PSD) is helpful for analysis. The PSD of the product of two functions in the spatial domain is given by the convolution of their PSDs in the Fourier domain.

> 对于采样位置由随机变量指定的随机采样方法，**功率谱密度** (PSD) 对分析很有帮助。空间域中两个函数乘积的 PSD 由它们在傅里叶域中 PSD 的卷积给出。

The ideal sampling pattern's PSD would have a single delta spike at the origin and be zero everywhere else, requiring infinite sampling density. With *jittered sampling* (adding uniform random offsets to regularly spaced points, Equation 8.6), the analytic PSD (Equation 8.7) has a spike at the origin, near-zero values at low frequencies, and settles to around 1 at higher frequencies.

> 理想采样模式的 PSD 在原点处有一个单一 delta 尖峰，其他处为零，这需要无限采样密度。对于**抖动采样**（向规则间隔点添加均匀随机偏移，公式 8.6），解析 PSD（公式 8.7）在原点有尖峰，低频处接近零值，在高频处趋近于 1。

Jittered sampling converts aliasing into high-frequency noise by spreading high-frequency energy over many frequencies—more visually pleasing than lower-frequency aliasing. In general, aliasing is reduced most effectively if there is minimal energy in the PSD of the sampling function at low frequencies.

> 抖动采样通过将高频能量分散到许多频率上，将走样转化为高频噪声——比低频走样更令人视觉愉悦。一般来说，如果采样函数的 PSD 在低频处的能量最小，走样的减少效果最好。

PSDs are sometimes described by their color. *White noise* has equal power at all frequencies. *Blue noise* has power concentrated at higher frequencies and less at low frequencies. pbrt includes precomputed 2D tables with blue noise characteristics via the `BlueNoise()` function—nearby pixels differ in value, corresponding to higher-frequency variation, whereas white noise has visible clumps of similar-valued pixels.

> PSD 有时用颜色来描述。**白噪声**在所有频率上具有相等的功率。**蓝噪声**的功率集中在高频，低频较少。pbrt 通过 `BlueNoise()` 函数包含具有蓝噪声特性的预计算二维表——相邻像素的值不同，对应更高频的变化，而白噪声则有可见的相似值像素聚集。
