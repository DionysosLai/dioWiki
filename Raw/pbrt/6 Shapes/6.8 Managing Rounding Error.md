## 6.8 Managing Rounding Error

Thus far, we have been discussing ray–shape intersection algorithms with respect to idealized arithmetic operations based on the real numbers. This approach has gotten us far, although the fact that computers can only represent finite quantities and therefore cannot actually represent all the real numbers is important. In place of real numbers, computers use floating-point numbers, which have fixed storage requirements. However, error may be introduced each time a floating-point operation is performed, since the result may not be representable in the designated amount of memory.

> 到目前为止，我们一直在讨论基于实数理想化算术运算的光线-形状求交算法。这种方法使我们走了很远，尽管计算机只能表示有限数量因而实际上无法表示所有实数这一事实很重要。代替实数，计算机使用浮点数，它们具有固定的存储需求。然而，每次执行浮点运算时都可能引入误差，因为结果可能无法在指定的内存量中表示。

The accumulation of this error has several implications for the accuracy of intersection tests. First, it is possible that it will cause valid intersections to be missed completely—for example, if a computed intersection's t value is negative even though the precise value is positive. Furthermore, computed ray–shape intersection points may be above or below the actual surface of the shape. This leads to a problem: when new rays are traced starting from computed intersection points for shadow rays and reflection rays, if the ray origin is below the actual surface, we may find an incorrect reintersection with the surface. Conversely, if the origin is too far above the surface, shadows and reflections may appear detached. (See Figure 6.38.)

> 这种误差的累积对求交测试的精度有几个影响。首先，它可能导致有效的交点被完全遗漏——例如，如果计算的交点 t 值为负，即使精确值为正。此外，计算的光线-形状交点可能在形状实际表面的上方或下方。这导致一个问题：当从计算的交点开始追踪阴影光线和反射光线时，如果光线原点在实际表面下方，我们可能会发现与表面的错误重新相交。相反，如果原点在表面上方太远，阴影和反射可能看起来是分离的。（见图 6.38。）

Figure 6.38: Geometric Settings for Rounding-Error Issues That Can Cause Visible Errors in Images. The incident ray on the left intersects the surface. On the left, the computed intersection point (black circle) is slightly below the surface and a too-low "epsilon" offsetting the origin of the shadow ray leads to an incorrect self-intersection. On the right, a too-high "epsilon" causes a valid intersection to be missed as the ray's origin is past the occluding surface.

> 图 6.38：可导致图像中可见错误的舍入误差问题的几何设置。左侧的入射光线与表面相交。在左侧，计算的交点（黑色圆圈）略低于表面，而过低的"epsilon"偏移阴影光线原点导致了错误的自相交。在右侧，过高的"epsilon"导致有效交点被遗漏，因为光线原点已超过遮挡表面。

Typical practice to address this issue in ray tracing is to offset spawned rays by a fixed "ray epsilon" value, ignoring any intersections along the ray closer than some t_min value. Figure 6.39 shows why this approach requires fairly high epsilon values to work effectively: if the spawned ray is oblique to the surface, incorrect ray intersections may occur quite some distance from the ray origin. Unfortunately, large epsilon values cause ray origins to be relatively far from the original intersection points, which in turn can cause valid nearby intersections to be missed, leading to loss of fine detail in shadows and reflections.

> 光线追踪中解决此问题的典型做法是通过固定的"ray epsilon"值偏移衍生光线，忽略沿光线比某个 t_min 值更近的任何交点。图 6.39 展示了为什么这种方法需要相当高的 epsilon 值才能有效工作：如果衍生光线相对于表面是斜的，错误的光线交点可能发生在距光线原点相当远的地方。不幸的是，较大的 epsilon 值导致光线原点与原始交点相对较远，这反过来可能导致附近有效交点被遗漏，导致阴影和反射中精细细节的丢失。

Figure 6.39: If the computed intersection point (filled circle) is below the surface and the spawned ray is oblique, incorrect reintersections may occur some distance from the ray origin (open circle). If a minimum t value along the ray is used to discard nearby intersections, a relatively large t_min is needed to handle oblique rays well.

> 图 6.39：如果计算的交点（实心圆）在表面下方且衍生光线是斜的，错误的重新相交可能发生在距光线原点（空心圆）一定距离处。如果使用沿光线的最小 t 值来丢弃附近的交点，需要相对较大的 t_min 来很好地处理斜射光线。

In this section, we will introduce the ideas underlying floating-point arithmetic and describe techniques for analyzing the error in floating-point computations. We will then apply these methods to the ray–shape algorithms introduced earlier in this chapter and show how to compute ray intersection points with bounded error. This will allow us to conservatively position ray origins so that incorrect self-intersections are never found, while keeping ray origins extremely close to the actual intersection point so that incorrect misses are minimized. In turn, no additional "ray epsilon" values are needed.

> 在本节中，我们将介绍浮点算术的基础思想，并描述分析浮点计算中误差的技术。然后我们将这些方法应用于本章前面介绍的光线-形状算法，并展示如何计算具有有界误差的光线交点。这将允许我们保守地定位光线原点，使错误的自相交永远不会被发现，同时保持光线原点极其接近实际交点，从而最大限度地减少错误的遗漏。因此，不需要额外的"ray epsilon"值。

### 6.8.1 Floating-Point Arithmetic

### 6.8.1 浮点算术

Computation must be performed on a finite representation of numbers that fits in a finite amount of memory; the infinite set of real numbers cannot be represented on a computer. One such finite representation is fixed point, where given a 16-bit integer, for example, one might map it to positive real numbers by dividing by 256. Fixed-point numbers can be implemented efficiently using integer arithmetic operations (a property that made them popular on early PCs that did not support floating-point computation), but they suffer from a number of shortcomings; among them, the maximum number they can represent is limited, and they are not able to accurately represent very small numbers near zero.

> 计算必须在适合有限内存量的数字有限表示上执行；无限的实数集合无法在计算机上表示。一种这样的有限表示是定点数，例如给定一个 16 位整数，可以通过除以 256 将其映射到正实数。定点数可以使用整数算术运算高效实现（这一特性使它们在不支持浮点计算的早期 PC 上很流行），但它们有许多缺点；其中，它们能表示的最大数字有限，并且无法准确表示接近零的非常小的数字。

An alternative representation for real numbers on computers is floating-point numbers. These are based on representing numbers with a sign, a significand, and an exponent: essentially, the same representation as scientific notation but with a fixed number of digits devoted to significand and exponent. This representation makes it possible to represent and perform computations on numbers with a wide range of magnitudes while using a fixed amount of storage.

> 计算机上实数的另一种表示是浮点数。它们基于用符号、有效数字和指数来表示数字：本质上与科学记数法相同的表示，但有固定数量的数字用于有效数字和指数。这种表示使得能够在使用固定存储量的同时，表示和执行具有广泛量级范围的数字的计算。

Programmers using floating-point arithmetic are generally aware that floating-point values may be inaccurate; this understanding sometimes leads to a belief that floating-point arithmetic is unpredictable. In this section we will see that floating-point arithmetic has a carefully designed foundation that in turn makes it possible to compute conservative bounds on the error introduced in a particular computation. For ray-tracing calculations, this error is often surprisingly small.

> 使用浮点算术的程序员通常知道浮点值可能不准确；这种理解有时导致人们认为浮点算术是不可预测的。在本节中我们将看到浮点算术有一个精心设计的基础，这反过来使得能够计算特定计算中引入的误差的保守界限。对于光线追踪计算，这种误差通常出人意料地小。

Modern CPUs and GPUs nearly ubiquitously implement a model of floating-point arithmetic based on a standard promulgated by the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE 754). The IEEE 754 technical standard specifies the format of floating-point numbers in memory as well as specific rules for precision and rounding of floating-point computations; it is these rules that make it possible to reason rigorously about the error present in a computed floating-point value.

> 现代 CPU 和 GPU 几乎普遍实现了基于电气和电子工程师协会颁布的标准（IEEE 754）的浮点算术模型。IEEE 754 技术标准规定了内存中浮点数的格式以及浮点计算精度和舍入的具体规则；正是这些规则使得能够严格推理计算浮点值中存在的误差。

#### Floating-Point Representation

#### 浮点表示

The IEEE standard specifies that 32-bit floats are represented with a sign bit, 8 bits for the exponent, and 23 bits for the significand. The significand actually has 24 bits of precision when a *normalized* floating-point value is stored, since in binary the leading digit must be one and need not be stored explicitly. Thus, the implicit leading 1 digit with the 23 digits encoding the fractional part gives a total of 24 bits of precision.

> IEEE 标准规定 32 位浮点数用 1 个符号位、8 位指数和 23 位有效数字表示。当存储*规格化*浮点值时，有效数字实际上有 24 位精度，因为在二进制中前导数字必须是 1 且不需要显式存储。因此，隐含的前导 1 位加上编码小数部分的 23 位，总共给出 24 位精度。

An implication of this representation is that the spacing between representable floats between two adjacent powers of two is uniform throughout the range. In a range [2^e, 2^(e+1)], the spacing is 2^(e-23). This spacing is also referred to as the magnitude of a *unit in last place* ("ulp"); note that the magnitude of an ulp is determined by the floating-point value that it is with respect to.

> 这种表示的一个含义是，在两个相邻的 2 的幂之间，可表示浮点数之间的间距在整个范围内是均匀的。在范围 [2^e, 2^(e+1)] 中，间距为 2^(e-23)。这个间距也被称为*最后位置单元*（"ulp"）的大小；注意 ulp 的大小由它所相对的浮点值决定。

The minimum exponent e_b=0 is set aside for special treatment. With this exponent, the floating-point value is interpreted as not having the implicit leading 1 bit in the significand, allowing representation of zero and *denormalized* numbers. The maximum exponent e_b=255 is also reserved: with all-zero significand bits it represents positive or negative infinity, and with nonzero significand bits it represents "not a number" (NaN) values.

> 最小指数 e_b=0 被保留用于特殊处理。使用此指数，浮点值被解释为有效数字中没有隐含的前导 1 位，允许表示零和*非规格化*数。最大指数 e_b=255 也被保留：全零有效数字位表示正或负无穷，非零有效数字位表示"非数字"（NaN）值。

NaNs propagate through computations: any arithmetic operation where one of the operands is a NaN always returns NaN. Thus, if a NaN emerges from a long chain of computations, we know that something went awry somewhere along the way. Any comparison with a NaN value returns false.

> NaN 在计算中传播：任何操作数之一为 NaN 的算术运算始终返回 NaN。因此，如果 NaN 从长链计算中出现，我们知道某处出了问题。与 NaN 值的任何比较都返回 false。

By default, the majority of floating-point computation in pbrt uses 32-bit floats. However, it is possible to configure it to use 64-bit double-precision values instead. Doubles allocate 11 bits to the exponent and 52 to the significand. pbrt also supports 16-bit halfs (5 bits for the exponent and 10 for the significand) as an in-memory representation for pixel values in images.

> 默认情况下，pbrt 中的大部分浮点计算使用 32 位浮点数。然而，可以配置它使用 64 位双精度值。双精度分配 11 位给指数和 52 位给有效数字。pbrt 还支持 16 位半精度（5 位给指数和 10 位给有效数字）作为图像像素值的内存表示。

#### Arithmetic Operations

#### 算术运算

IEEE 754 provides important guarantees about the properties of floating-point arithmetic: specifically, it guarantees that addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and square root give the same results given the same inputs and that these results are the floating-point number that is closest to the result of the underlying computation if it had been performed in infinite-precision arithmetic.

> IEEE 754 提供了关于浮点算术性质的重要保证：具体来说，它保证加法、减法、乘法、除法和平方根在相同输入下给出相同的结果，并且这些结果是最接近于底层计算结果（如果以无限精度算术执行）的浮点数。

The rounding error can be bounded: the amount of error introduced from rounding can be no more than half the floating-point spacing at the result value. For 32-bit floats, this bound is the *machine epsilon*, ε_m = 2^(-24) ≈ 5.96 × 10^(-8). Thus we have the fundamental bound: the result of a floating-point operation equals the exact result times (1±ε_m). The fused multiply add (FMA) operation only rounds once and thus gives better accuracy than computing the multiplication and addition separately.

> 舍入误差可以被界定：由舍入引入的误差量不超过结果值处浮点间距的一半。对于 32 位浮点数，此界是*机器精度*，ε_m = 2^(-24) ≈ 5.96 × 10^(-8)。因此我们有基本界：浮点运算的结果等于精确结果乘以 (1±ε_m)。融合乘加（FMA）运算只舍入一次，因此比分别计算乘法和加法给出更好的精度。

A number of useful properties follow directly: fl(1×a) = a, fl(0+a) = a, fl(a/a) = 1, fl(a-a) = 0. Multiplying or dividing by powers of 2 is exact (assuming no overflow or underflow). All of these properties follow from the principle that the result must be the nearest floating-point value to the actual result.

> 许多有用的性质直接得出：fl(1×a) = a，fl(0+a) = a，fl(a/a) = 1，fl(a-a) = 0。乘以或除以 2 的幂是精确的（假设没有上溢或下溢）。所有这些性质都来自以下原则：结果必须是最接近实际结果的浮点值。

#### Utility Routines

#### 实用工具例程

A few basic utility routines will be useful: IsNaN(), IsInf(), and IsFinite() functions for checking special values. The FloatToBits() and BitsToFloat() conversion functions allow interpreting floating-point values in terms of their constituent bits, using pstd::bit_cast. Given these conversions, it is easy to extract the Exponent, Significand, and SignBit from a float.

> 一些基本的实用工具例程将很有用：IsNaN()、IsInf() 和 IsFinite() 函数用于检查特殊值。FloatToBits() 和 BitsToFloat() 转换函数允许根据组成位解释浮点值，使用 pstd::bit_cast。给定这些转换，从浮点数中提取指数、有效数字和符号位很容易。

These conversions can be used to implement NextFloatUp() and NextFloatDown() functions that bump a floating-point value to the next greater or smaller representable value. Thanks to the specifics of the in-memory representation of floats—the exponent lies at the high bits above the significand—adding one to the integer representation advances to the next higher float (with carry propagating into the exponent if the significand overflows). For negative values, subtracting one similarly advances to the next higher value.

> 这些转换可用于实现 NextFloatUp() 和 NextFloatDown() 函数，将浮点值调整到下一个更大或更小的可表示值。由于浮点数内存表示的具体特性——指数位于有效数字上方的高位——将整数表示加一就推进到下一个更高的浮点数（如果有效数字溢出，进位传播到指数）。对于负值，减一类似地推进到下一个更高的值。

#### Error Propagation

#### 误差传播

Using the guarantees of IEEE floating-point arithmetic, it is possible to develop methods to analyze and bound the error in a given floating-point computation. Two measurements of error are useful: *absolute error* δ_a is the magnitude of the difference between the computed and exact result, and *relative error* δ_r is the ratio of the absolute error to the precise result.

> 使用 IEEE 浮点算术的保证，可以开发分析和界定给定浮点计算中误差的方法。两种误差度量是有用的：*绝对误差* δ_a 是计算结果和精确结果之差的大小，*相对误差* δ_r 是绝对误差与精确结果的比率。

As a first application, consider computing the sum of four numbers a, b, c, and d. If we compute this sum as r = (((a + b) + c) + d), the absolute error is bounded by (3ε_m)(|a|+|b|) + (2ε_m)|c| + ε_m|d| approximately. Changing the expression to r = (a + b) + (c + d) gives different bounds that may be better or worse depending on the relative magnitudes of the terms. This approach is known as *forward error analysis*.

> 作为第一个应用，考虑计算四个数 a、b、c 和 d 的总和。如果我们将此总和计算为 r = (((a + b) + c) + d)，绝对误差大约被限制为 (3ε_m)(|a|+|b|) + (2ε_m)|c| + ε_m|d|。将表达式改为 r = (a + b) + (c + d) 给出不同的界，取决于各项的相对大小可能更好或更差。这种方法被称为*前向误差分析*。

Higham (2002, Section 3.1) gives an approach to more tightly bound products of error terms. If we have n terms of (1±ε_m), their product is bounded by (1±γ(n)), where γ(n) = nε_m / (1 - nε_m). The function gamma() computes this value. Using this notation, we can express error bounds more concisely. Quotients of (1±ε_m) terms can also be bounded with the γ function.

> Higham (2002, 第 3.1 节) 给出了更紧密地界定误差项乘积的方法。如果我们有 n 个 (1±ε_m) 项，它们的乘积被限制为 (1±γ(n))，其中 γ(n) = nε_m / (1 - nε_m)。gamma() 函数计算此值。使用此记法，我们可以更简洁地表达误差界。(1±ε_m) 项的商也可以用 γ 函数来界定。

For multiplication with inputs carrying accumulated error, the relative error in the result is bounded by roughly the sum of the relative errors plus one more ulp—about as good as we might hope for. However, with addition and subtraction, if the signs of the operands differ (or equivalently, they are the same but subtraction is performed), the relative error can be quite high when the result is small compared to the operands. This substantial increase in relative error is called *catastrophic cancellation*.

> 对于带有累积误差的输入的乘法，结果中的相对误差大约被限制为相对误差之和加一个 ulp——大约是我们所能期望的最好结果。然而，对于加法和减法，如果操作数的符号不同（或等价地，它们相同但执行减法），当结果与操作数相比很小时，相对误差可能非常高。这种相对误差的大幅增加被称为*灾难性消去*。

#### Running Error Analysis

#### 运行误差分析

In addition to working out error bounds algebraically, we can also have the computer do this work for us as some computation is being performed. This approach is known as *running error analysis*. The Interval class tracks rounding errors in floating-point arithmetic and is useful even if none of the initial values are intervals. While computing error bounds in this way has higher runtime overhead than using derived expressions, it can be convenient when derivations become unwieldy.

> 除了代数地推导误差界外，我们还可以让计算机在执行某些计算时为我们做这项工作。这种方法被称为*运行误差分析*。Interval 类跟踪浮点算术中的舍入误差，即使初始值都不是区间也很有用。虽然以这种方式计算误差界比使用推导的表达式有更高的运行时开销，但当推导变得笨重时它可能很方便。

### 6.8.2 Conservative Ray–Bounds Intersections

### 6.8.2 保守的光线-包围盒求交

Floating-point round-off error can cause the ray–bounding box intersection test to miss cases where a ray actually does intersect the box. While it is acceptable to have occasional false positives from ray–box intersection tests, we would like to never miss an intersection—getting this right is important for the correctness of the BVHAggregate acceleration data structure so that valid ray–shape intersections are not missed.

> 浮点舍入误差可能导致光线-包围盒求交测试遗漏光线实际上确实与盒相交的情况。虽然光线-盒求交测试偶尔的误报是可以接受的，但我们希望永远不要遗漏交点——正确处理这一点对 BVH 加速数据结构的正确性很重要，以免遗漏有效的光线-形状交点。

The computation to find the t value for a ray intersection with a plane perpendicular to an axis introduces error bounded by γ(3)|t|. If the intervals around the computed t_near and t_far values overlap, it is impossible to know their true ordering. Increasing t_far by twice the error bound, 2γ(3), before performing the comparison ensures that we conservatively return true in this case. This is implemented as: tFar *= 1 + 2 * gamma(3).

> 找到光线与垂直于某轴的平面交点的 t 值的计算引入的误差被限制为 γ(3)|t|。如果计算的 t_near 和 t_far 值周围的区间重叠，就不可能知道它们的真实顺序。在执行比较之前将 t_far 增加两倍的误差界 2γ(3)，确保在这种情况下我们保守地返回 true。实现为：tFar *= 1 + 2 * gamma(3)。

### 6.8.3 Accurate Quadratic Discriminants

### 6.8.3 精确的二次判别式

Recall that intersecting a ray with a sphere or cylinder involves finding the zeros of a quadratic equation, which requires calculating its discriminant b²-4ac. If the discriminant is computed as written, catastrophic cancellation occurs when the sphere is far from the ray origin, since b² and 4ac are both large and nearly equal. This issue is made worse since the magnitudes of the terms are related to the *squared* distance.

> 回忆一下，光线与球或圆柱的求交涉及找二次方程的零点，这需要计算其判别式 b²-4ac。如果判别式按原样计算，当球远离光线原点时会发生灾难性消去，因为 b² 和 4ac 都很大且几乎相等。由于各项的大小与*平方*距离有关，这个问题更加严重。

Algebraically rewriting the discriminant computation makes it possible to compute it with more accuracy. By decomposing the vector from the sphere center to the ray origin into components parallel and perpendicular to the ray direction, and using the Pythagorean theorem, we can eliminate the b² term from the discriminant. Further, using the difference-of-squares identity (a+b)(a-b) = a²-b² reduces the magnitudes of intermediate values, which further reduces error.

> 代数地重写判别式计算使得能够更准确地计算它。通过将从球心到光线原点的向量分解为平行和垂直于光线方向的分量，并使用勾股定理，我们可以从判别式中消除 b² 项。进一步地，使用平方差恒等式 (a+b)(a-b) = a²-b² 减少了中间值的大小，从而进一步减少误差。

One might ask, why not use DifferenceOfProducts() to compute the discriminant with low error? The reason is that the values a, b, and c already suffer from rounding error. In particular, b is the difference of two positive values, so it is susceptible to catastrophic cancellation. A similar derivation gives a more accurate discriminant for the cylinder.

> 有人可能会问，为什么不使用 DifferenceOfProducts() 来以低误差计算判别式？原因是 a、b 和 c 的值已经存在舍入误差。特别是，b 是两个正值的差，因此容易受到灾难性消去的影响。类似的推导给出了圆柱体更精确的判别式。

### 6.8.4 Robust Triangle Intersections

### 6.8.4 鲁棒的三角形求交

The details of the ray–triangle intersection algorithm in Section 6.5.3 were carefully designed to avoid cases where rays could incorrectly pass through an edge or vertex shared by two adjacent triangles without generating an intersection. An intersection algorithm with this guarantee is referred to as being *watertight*.

> 第 6.5.3 节中光线-三角形求交算法的细节被精心设计以避免光线可能错误地穿过两个相邻三角形共享的边或顶点而不产生交点的情况。具有此保证的求交算法被称为*防水的*。

The algorithm is based on transforming triangle vertices into a coordinate system with the ray's origin at its origin and the ray's direction along the +z axis. Although round-off error may be introduced by this transformation, it does not affect watertightness since the same transformation is applied to all triangles.

> 该算法基于将三角形顶点变换到一个坐标系中，其中光线的原点在坐标系原点，光线方向沿 +z 轴。虽然此变换可能引入舍入误差，但它不影响防水性，因为相同的变换应用于所有三角形。

The key to the robustness is that the edge function evaluations are guaranteed to have the correct sign with floating-point arithmetic. If the value of the edge function is zero, the fragment <<Fall back to double-precision test at triangle edges>> reevaluates it with double precision; it can be shown that doubling the precision suffices to accurately distinguish these cases. The overhead is minimal: in a benchmark with 88 million ray intersection tests, the double-precision fallback had to be used in less than 0.0000023% of the cases.

> 鲁棒性的关键在于边缘函数求值保证在浮点算术下具有正确的符号。如果边缘函数的值为零，<<在三角形边缘回退到双精度测试>>片段用双精度重新求值；可以证明将精度加倍足以准确区分这些情况。开销很小：在一个有 8800 万次光线求交测试的基准测试中，双精度回退只在不到 0.0000023% 的情况下使用。

### 6.8.5 Bounding Intersection Point Error

### 6.8.5 界定交点误差

We can apply the error analysis machinery to derive conservative bounds on the absolute error in computed ray–shape intersection points, which allows us to construct bounding boxes that are guaranteed to include an intersection point on the actual surface (Figure 6.43).

> 我们可以应用误差分析机制来推导计算的光线-形状交点中绝对误差的保守界，这允许我们构造保证包含实际表面上交点的包围盒（图 6.43）。

Figure 6.43: Shape intersection algorithms in pbrt compute an intersection point (filled circle). The absolute error in this point is bounded by δ_x and δ_y, giving a small box around the point. Because these bounds are conservative, we know that the actual intersection point on the surface (open circle) must lie somewhere within the box.

> 图 6.43：pbrt 中的形状求交算法计算一个交点（实心圆）。此点的绝对误差被 δ_x 和 δ_y 界定，给出点周围的一个小盒子。因为这些界是保守的，我们知道表面上的实际交点（空心圆）必须在盒子内的某处。

Computing intersection points using the parametric ray equation p = o + t·d can introduce significant error, since the magnitudes of o, t, and d may be quite different from the magnitude of the intersection point, leading to catastrophic cancellation. We introduce better approaches in the following.

> 使用参数光线方程 p = o + t·d 计算交点可能引入显著误差，因为 o、t 和 d 的大小可能与交点的大小相当不同，导致灾难性消去。我们在下面介绍更好的方法。

#### Reprojection: Quadrics

#### 重投影：二次曲面

Rather than using the parametric ray equation, an effective approach is to refine computed intersection points by reprojecting them to the surface. For a sphere at the origin with radius r, the point is reprojected by scaling with the ratio r/distance. The resulting absolute error is bounded by γ(5) times the magnitude in each dimension—no more than about 2.5 ulps from a point on the surface.

> 与其使用参数光线方程，一种有效的方法是通过将计算的交点重投影到表面来精化它们。对于原点处半径为 r 的球，通过 r/distance 比率缩放来重投影该点。结果的绝对误差在每个维度上被限制为 γ(5) 乘以量级——距表面上的点不超过大约 2.5 ulp。

For a cylinder along the z axis, only the x and y coordinates need to be reprojected, and the error bounds turn out to be only γ(3) times their magnitudes. The disk shape is particularly easy: setting the z coordinate to lie exactly on the plane of the disk gives zero error.

> 对于沿 z 轴的圆柱体，只需要重投影 x 和 y 坐标，误差界仅为 γ(3) 乘以其量级。圆盘形状特别简单：将 z 坐标精确设置在圆盘平面上给出零误差。

#### Parametric Evaluation: Triangles

#### 参数求值：三角形

Another effective approach uses the shape's parametric representation. For triangles, the edge function values are used to find barycentric coordinates, and the vertex positions are interpolated. The absolute error in the interpolated point is bounded by γ(7) times the sum of absolute values of the barycentric-weighted vertex positions.

> 另一种有效的方法使用形状的参数表示。对于三角形，边缘函数值用于找到重心坐标，并对顶点位置进行插值。插值点的绝对误差被限制为 γ(7) 乘以重心加权顶点位置的绝对值之和。

#### Parametric Evaluation: Bilinear Patches

#### 参数求值：双线性面片

Bilinear patch intersection points are found by evaluating the bilinear function. The error is bounded by γ(6) times the sum of absolute values of the four corner vertex positions. The same bounds apply for sampled points.

> 双线性面片交点通过求值双线性函数找到。误差被限制为 γ(6) 乘以四个角顶点位置的绝对值之和。相同的界适用于采样点。

#### Parametric Evaluation: Curves

#### 参数求值：曲线

Because the Curve shape orients itself to face incident rays, rays leaving it must be offset by the curve's width in order to not incorrectly reintersect it. For wide curves, this bound is significant and may lead to visible errors; in that case, the Curve shape should probably be replaced with bilinear patches.

> 因为 Curve 形状朝向入射光线定向自己，离开它的光线必须偏移曲线的宽度以避免错误地重新与之相交。对于宽曲线，此界是显著的，可能导致可见错误；在这种情况下，Curve 形状可能应该用双线性面片替换。

#### Effect of Transformations

#### 变换的影响

The last detail to attend to in order to bound the error in computed intersection points is the effect of transformations, which introduce additional rounding error when applied. If possible, it is best to try to avoid coordinate-system transformations of rays and intersection points. For example, it is better to transform triangle vertices to rendering space and intersect rendering-space rays with them. Transformations are still useful for quadrics and object instancing.

> 为了界定计算交点中的误差需要注意的最后一个细节是变换的影响，变换在应用时会引入额外的舍入误差。如果可能，最好尝试避免光线和交点的坐标系变换。例如，将三角形顶点变换到渲染空间并与渲染空间光线求交更好。变换对于二次曲面和物体实例化仍然有用。

For a point with no accumulated error, the absolute error in the transformed x coordinate is bounded by γ(3) times the sum of absolute values of the matrix-vector product terms. For a point that already carries error bounds, the expression includes additional terms accounting for the incoming error, scaled by (γ(3)+1). The derivation of these bounds was presented in detail, along with the observation that using the Interval class would give bounds 3–6× wider and be six times slower.

> 对于没有累积误差的点，变换后 x 坐标的绝对误差被限制为 γ(3) 乘以矩阵-向量乘积项的绝对值之和。对于已经携带误差界的点，表达式包括额外的项来考虑传入的误差，按 (γ(3)+1) 缩放。这些界的推导已详细给出，同时观察到使用 Interval 类将给出宽 3-6 倍的界且速度慢 6 倍。

### 6.8.6 Robust Spawned Ray Origins

### 6.8.6 鲁棒的衍生光线原点

Computed intersection points and their error bounds give us a small 3D box that bounds a region of space. We know that the precise intersection point must be somewhere inside this box and that the surface must pass through it. Having these boxes makes it possible to position the origins of spawned rays so that they are always on the right side of the surface and do not incorrectly reintersect it.

> 计算的交点及其误差界给我们一个界定空间区域的小三维盒子。我们知道精确的交点必须在此盒子内的某处，且表面必须穿过它。有了这些盒子，就有可能定位衍生光线的原点，使它们始终在表面的正确一侧且不会错误地重新与之相交。

In order to ensure that the spawned ray origin is on the right side of the surface, we move far enough along the normal so that the plane perpendicular to the normal is outside the error bounding box. The maximum offset d is computed as d = Dot(Abs(n), pi.Error()). The offset is applied in the direction of the normal (or opposite, based on the outgoing ray direction), and then rounded away from the original point using NextFloatUp/NextFloatDown.

> 为了确保衍生光线原点在表面的正确一侧，我们沿法线移动足够远，使垂直于法线的平面在误差包围盒之外。最大偏移 d 计算为 d = Dot(Abs(n), pi.Error())。偏移沿法线方向（或反方向，取决于出射光线方向）应用，然后使用 NextFloatUp/NextFloatDown 从原始点向外舍入。

The OffsetRayOrigin() function implements this computation. Interaction provides convenience methods SpawnRay() and SpawnRayTo() that use it. The ShadowEpsilon constant (0.0001f) is used to stop shadow rays slightly before reaching light source surfaces, addressing the related issue of avoiding incorrect intersections with area light surfaces.

> OffsetRayOrigin() 函数实现此计算。Interaction 提供了使用它的便捷方法 SpawnRay() 和 SpawnRayTo()。ShadowEpsilon 常量 (0.0001f) 用于使阴影光线在到达光源表面前略微停止，解决了避免与面光源表面错误相交的相关问题。

One last issue: when transforming a ray whose origin was carefully computed, rounding error may cause the transformed origin to end up on the wrong side of the surface. Therefore, whenever a ray is transformed, its origin is advanced to the edge of the error bounds introduced by the transformation.

> 最后一个问题：当变换一条原点经过仔细计算的光线时，舍入误差可能导致变换后的原点落在表面的错误一侧。因此，每当光线被变换时，其原点被推进到由变换引入的误差界的边缘。

### 6.8.7 Avoiding Intersections behind Ray Origins

### 6.8.7 避免光线原点后方的交点

Bounding the error in computed intersection points allows us to compute ray origins that are guaranteed to be on the right side of the surface. However, a second source of rounding error must also be addressed: the error in parametric t values computed for ray–shape intersections. Rounding error can lead to a computed positive t value even though the actual value is negative.

> 界定计算交点中的误差允许我们计算保证在表面正确一侧的光线原点。然而，还必须处理第二个舍入误差来源：光线-形状求交计算的参数 t 值中的误差。舍入误差可能导致计算出正的 t 值，即使实际值为负。

For the ray–axis-aligned slab computation (t = (p-o)/d), IEEE floating-point guarantees that the sign of the computed t is always correct. For intersection routines based on the Interval class, the computed t has an associated error bound. For triangles, detailed error bounds on the t value are derived using the δ_z, δ_x, δ_y, δ_e, and δ_t terms, and t is checked against deltaT to ensure it is conservatively greater than zero. Similar epsilon-based approaches are used for bilinear patches.

> 对于光线-轴对齐平板计算 (t = (p-o)/d)，IEEE 浮点保证计算的 t 的符号始终正确。对于基于 Interval 类的求交例程，计算的 t 有关联的误差界。对于三角形，使用 δ_z、δ_x、δ_y、δ_e 和 δ_t 项推导了 t 值的详细误差界，并将 t 与 deltaT 进行比较以确保其保守地大于零。双线性面片使用类似的基于 epsilon 的方法。

### 6.8.8 Discussion

### 6.8.8 讨论

Minimizing and bounding numerical error in other geometric computations (e.g., partial derivatives of surface positions, interpolated texture coordinates, etc.) are much less important than they are for the positions of ray intersections. The computations involving color and light in physically based rendering generally do not present trouble with respect to round-off error, as they involve sums of products of positive numbers with reasonably close magnitudes.

> 最小化和界定其他几何计算（例如表面位置的偏导数、插值纹理坐标等）中的数值误差远不如光线交点位置的重要。物理渲染中涉及颜色和光照的计算通常不会在舍入误差方面造成问题，因为它们涉及量级合理接近的正数的乘积之和。

Interestingly enough, we saw an increase of roughly 20% in overall ray-tracing execution time after replacing pbrt's old *ad hoc* method to avoid incorrect self-intersections with the method described in this section. (In comparison, rendering with double-precision floating point causes an increase of roughly 30%.) Profiling showed that very little of the additional time was due to the additional computation to find error bounds.

> 有趣的是，在用本节描述的方法替换 pbrt 旧的*临时*方法来避免错误的自相交后，我们看到整体光线追踪执行时间增加了大约 20%。（相比之下，使用双精度浮点渲染导致大约 30% 的增加。）性能分析表明，额外时间中很少是由于计算误差界的额外计算。

The majority of this slowdown is due to an increase in ray–object intersection tests. The reason was first identified by Wächter (2008, p. 30): when ray origins are very close to shape surfaces, more nodes of intersection acceleration hierarchies must be visited when tracing spawned rays than if overly loose offsets are used. While this reduction in performance is unfortunate, it is a direct result of the greater accuracy of the method; it is the price to be paid for more accurate resolution of valid nearby intersections.

> 这种减速的大部分是由于光线-物体求交测试的增加。原因首先由 Wächter (2008, p. 30) 确定：当光线原点非常接近形状表面时，追踪衍生光线时必须访问求交加速层次结构的更多节点，而如果使用过于宽松的偏移则不需要。虽然这种性能降低是不幸的，但它是该方法更高精度的直接结果；这是为更准确地解析有效的附近交点所付出的代价。
