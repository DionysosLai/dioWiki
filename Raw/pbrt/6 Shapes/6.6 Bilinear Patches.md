## 6.6 Bilinear Patches

It is useful to have a shape defined by four vertices. One option would be a planar quadrilateral, though not requiring all four vertices to be coplanar is preferable, as it is less restrictive. Such a shape is the bilinear patch, which is a parametric surface defined by four vertices p00, p10, p01, and p11. Each vertex gives the position associated with a corner of the parametric domain and points on the surface are defined via bilinear interpolation. The bilinear patch is a *doubly ruled surface*: there are two straight lines through every point on it. Not only can bilinear patches be used to represent planar quadrilaterals, but they can also represent simple curved surfaces. They are a useful target for converting higher-order parametric surfaces to simpler shapes that are amenable to direct ray intersection.

> 用四个顶点定义的形状非常实用。一个选择是平面四边形，但不要求四点共面更好，因为限制更少。这种形状就是双线性面片（bilinear patch），它是由四个顶点 p00、p10、p01、p11 定义的参数曲面，表面点通过双线性插值确定。双线性面片是一种*双直纹曲面*：其上每一点都有两条直线穿过。它既可以表示平面四边形，也可以表示简单曲面，是将高阶参数曲面转换为可直接求交的简单形状的理想目标。

pbrt allows the specification of bilinear patch meshes for the same reasons that triangle meshes can be specified: to allow per-vertex attributes like position and surface normal to be shared by multiple patches and to allow mesh-wide properties to be stored just once. To this end, BilinearPatchMesh plays the equivalent role to the TriangleMesh. The BilinearPatchMesh constructor mirrors the TriangleMesh's, transforming positions and normals to rendering space and using the BufferCache to avoid storing redundant buffers in memory.

> pbrt 允许指定双线性面片网格，理由与三角形网格相同：让位置和法线等逐顶点属性可被多个面片共享，并且网格级属性只需存储一次。BilinearPatchMesh 扮演与 TriangleMesh 等价的角色，其构造函数将位置和法线变换到渲染空间，并使用 BufferCache 避免冗余存储。

The BilinearPatch class implements the Shape interface and represents a single patch in a bilinear patch mesh. Also similar to triangles, each BilinearPatch stores the index of the mesh that it is a part of as well as its own index in the mesh's patches. The GetMesh() method makes it easy for a BilinearPatch to get the pointer to its associated mesh. There is a subtlety with the use of a vector to store the meshes: pbrt's scene initialization code parallelizes reading binary mesh files from disk, and a mutex protects adding meshes to this vector. As the vector grows, it is periodically reallocated. Consequently, the BilinearPatch constructor must not call GetMesh() since that accesses allMeshes without mutual exclusion; instead, the mesh is passed to the constructor as a parameter.

> BilinearPatch 类实现了 Shape 接口，代表双线性面片网格中的单个面片。与三角形类似，每个 BilinearPatch 存储所属网格的索引和自身在网格中的面片索引。GetMesh() 方法方便地获取关联的网格指针。这里有一个微妙之处：pbrt 的场景初始化代码会并行从磁盘读取二进制网格文件，使用互斥锁保护向存储网格的向量中添加元素。随着向量增长会周期性重新分配内存，因此 BilinearPatch 构造函数不能调用 GetMesh()（因为它不加锁地访问 allMeshes），而是将网格作为参数直接传入。

### Area and Rectangle Detection

The area of a parametric surface defined over [0,1]² is given by the integral of the magnitude of the cross product of the partial derivatives ∂p/∂u and ∂p/∂v. The partial derivatives of a bilinear patch are easily derived. However, it is not generally possible to evaluate the area integral in closed form with these partial derivatives. Therefore, the BilinearPatch constructor caches the patch's surface area in a member variable, using numerical integration to compute its value if necessary.

> 定义在 [0,1]² 上的参数曲面面积等于偏导 ∂p/∂u 和 ∂p/∂v 叉积模的积分。双线性面片的偏导容易求出，但通常无法用这些偏导对面积积分求出闭式解。因此 BilinearPatch 构造函数将面积缓存在成员变量中，必要时使用数值积分（3×3 Riemann 和）来计算。

Because bilinear patches are often used to represent rectangles, the constructor checks for that case and takes the product of the lengths of the sides to compute the area when appropriate. The IsRectangle() method first tests if any two neighboring vertices are coincident (not a rectangle). Then it checks if the four vertices are coplanar by computing the surface normal of the plane formed by three vertices and testing whether the fourth vertex's direction is nearly perpendicular to it. Finally, four coplanar vertices form a rectangle if they all have the same squared distance from their centroid — using relative error makes this test independent of absolute patch size.

> 因为双线性面片常用于表示矩形，构造函数会检测此情况并用边长之积直接计算面积。IsRectangle() 方法首先测试是否有两个相邻顶点重合（则不是矩形），然后检查四顶点是否共面（用三个顶点构成的平面法线与第四个顶点方向的点积判断），最后检查四个共面顶点到中心点的距离平方是否相等——使用相对误差使测试不受面片绝对大小影响。

The bounds of a bilinear patch are given by the bounding box that bounds its four corner vertices. As with Triangles, the mesh vertices are already in rendering space, so no further transformation is necessary.

> 双线性面片的包围盒由四个角顶点的包围盒给出。与三角形一样，网格顶点已在渲染空间中，无需进一步变换。

### Normal Bounds

Although a planar patch has a single surface normal, the surface normal of a nonplanar patch varies across its surface. If the bilinear patch is actually a triangle (two vertices coincide), the NormalBounds() method evaluates a single surface normal and returns the corresponding DirectionCone. Otherwise, normals are computed at the four corners. At each corner, the partial derivatives simplify to differences with adjacent vertices. Care is necessary with orientation: if per-vertex shading normals were specified, they determine which side the geometric normal lies on; otherwise the normal may need flipping based on reverseOrientation and the handedness of the transformation. A bounding cone is found by averaging the four corner normals and computing the maximum angle any of them makes with that average.

> 平面面片有单一法线，而非平面面片的法线在表面上变化。若双线性面片实际上是三角形（两个顶点重合），NormalBounds() 直接计算唯一法线并返回 DirectionCone。否则在四个角计算法线——角点处偏导简化为与相邻顶点的差值。法线朝向需谨慎处理：若指定了逐顶点着色法线，则由其决定几何法线的朝向；否则可能需根据 reverseOrientation 和变换的手性翻转法线。包围锥通过四个角法线的平均值及其中最大夹角来确定。

### 6.6.1 Intersection Tests

Unlike triangles (but like spheres and cylinders), a ray may intersect a bilinear patch twice, in which case the closest of the two intersections is returned. The algorithm first determines the parametric (u,v) coordinates of any intersections; only if any are found within [0,1] are the corresponding v and t values computed.

> 与三角形不同（但与球面和圆柱体类似），光线可能与双线性面片相交两次，此时返回较近的交点。算法首先确定所有交点的参数坐标 (u,v)；只有当 u 在 [0,1] 范围内时才计算对应的 v 和 t 值。

Going back to the bilinear surface definition, fixing one of u or v gives a line. For example, with u fixed, we get a line f(u) parameterized by v connecting two opposite edges. The intersection test considers the set of all such lines. For any intersection, the minimum distance from a point along the ray to a point along one of these lines will be zero.

> 回顾双线性曲面定义，固定 u 或 v 之一得到一条直线。例如固定 u 时，得到一条由 v 参数化的线段 f(u)，连接面片的两条对边。求交测试考虑所有这样的线段集合：在任何交点处，光线上某点到某条线段的最小距离为零。

Given two infinite non-parallel lines, the minimum distance between them can be found by determining the pair of parallel planes that each contain one line and finding the distance between them. The cross product of the two line directions gives a vector perpendicular to both, providing the first three plane equation coefficients. Because we only care about zeros of the distance function, we can neglect the denominator. After equating the difference to zero and collecting terms, the distance turns out to be a quadratic equation in u (reassuringly quadratic since a ray can intersect a bilinear patch up to two times).

> 给定两条不平行的无穷直线，它们之间的最小距离可通过确定各包含一条线的平行平面对来求得。两条线方向的叉积给出垂直于两者的向量，提供平面方程的前三个系数。因为只关心距离为零的情况，可以忽略分母。令差值为零并合并同类项后，距离是关于 u 的二次方程（这符合预期，因为光线最多与双线性面片相交两次）。

The two u values are handled in turn. For each valid u ∈ [0,1], the v and t values are computed by finding the parametric values along the ray and the line where the distance between them is minimized. Although this distance should be zero since we determined there is an intersection, there may be round-off error, so formulating the computation as a distance minimization makes the best of available values. The v and t numerators are computed via 3×3 matrix determinants using the SquareMatrix class. Due to round-off error, t is tested against an epsilon value (from Section 6.8.7) to avoid reporting incorrect intersections behind the ray's origin. The second root is handled with equivalent code plus logic to keep the closer intersection.

> 依次处理两个 u 值。对于每个有效的 u ∈ [0,1]，通过找到光线和线段上距离最小化的参数值来计算 v 和 t。虽然距离理论上为零（因为确定了有交点），但可能存在舍入误差，因此将计算表述为距离最小化是利用现有值的合理方式。v 和 t 的分子通过 SquareMatrix 类计算 3×3 矩阵行列式得到。由于舍入误差，t 需要对照 epsilon 值（来自 6.8.7 节）测试，以避免报告光线原点后方的错误交点。第二个根用等价代码处理，加上保留较近交点的逻辑。

The BilinearIntersection structure stores the (u,v) coordinates and ray parametric value t, sufficient to encapsulate the intersection for later computation of geometric properties. The InteractionFromIntersection() static method computes all the geometric information to return the SurfaceInteraction corresponding to a specified point on a bilinear patch.

> BilinearIntersection 结构存储 (u,v) 坐标和光线参数值 t，足以封装交点信息以便后续计算几何属性。InteractionFromIntersection() 静态方法计算返回 SurfaceInteraction 所需的全部几何信息。

### InteractionFromIntersection

Given the parametric coordinates of an intersection point, the corresponding point on the bilinear patch is easily computed via the bilinear interpolation equation and its partial derivatives. If per-vertex texture coordinates have been specified, they too are interpolated at the intersection point; otherwise the parametric coordinates are used for texture mapping. The texture coordinates are denoted (s,t) to distinguish from the patch's (u,v) parameterization.

> 给定交点的参数坐标，面片上对应的点容易通过双线性插值方程及其偏导计算。若指定了逐顶点纹理坐标，同样在交点处插值；否则用参数坐标做纹理映射。纹理坐标记为 (s,t) 以区别于面片的 (u,v) 参数化。

When per-vertex texture coordinates are specified, the partial derivatives ∂p/∂u and ∂p/∂v in the SurfaceInteraction must be updated to be in terms of (s,t). First, the partial derivatives ∂(s,t)/∂u and ∂(s,t)/∂v are computed by bilinearly interpolating the per-vertex texture coordinates and differentiating; the desired ∂u/∂s etc. are found by taking reciprocals. Then the chain rule gives the updated partial derivatives ∂p/∂s and ∂p/∂t. If the provided texture coordinates specify a degenerate mapping, dpdu and dpdv are left unchanged (their cross product still gives a correct normal). A dot product check ensures the normal lies in the same hemisphere as before, flipping if necessary.

> 当指定了逐顶点纹理坐标时，SurfaceInteraction 中的偏导 ∂p/∂u 和 ∂p/∂v 必须更新为关于 (s,t) 的版本。首先通过双线性插值逐顶点纹理坐标并求导得到 ∂(s,t)/∂u 和 ∂(s,t)/∂v，取倒数得到所需的 ∂u/∂s 等。然后用链式法则计算更新后的 ∂p/∂s 和 ∂p/∂t。若纹理坐标映射退化，dpdu 和 dpdv 保持不变（其叉积仍给出正确法线）。点积检查确保法线与之前在同一半球，必要时翻转。

The second partial derivatives of a bilinear patch are easily found: ∂²p/∂u² and ∂²p/∂v² are both zero vectors, while ∂²p/∂u∂v = (p00 - p01) + (p11 - p10). From these, the partial derivatives of the surface normal ∂n/∂u and ∂n/∂v are computed using the first and second fundamental forms (the Weingarten equations), then adjusted for the (s,t) parameterization. The intersection point error pError is computed as γ(6) times the sum of absolute vertex positions. The SurfaceInteraction is initialized with all these geometric quantities.

> 双线性面片的二阶偏导容易求出：∂²p/∂u² 和 ∂²p/∂v² 都是零向量，而 ∂²p/∂u∂v = (p00 - p01) + (p11 - p10)。由此用第一和第二基本形式（Weingarten 方程）计算法线偏导 ∂n/∂u 和 ∂n/∂v，再根据 (s,t) 参数化进行调整。交点误差 pError 计算为 γ(6) 乘以顶点绝对位置之和。用所有这些几何量初始化 SurfaceInteraction。

If per-vertex shading normals are present, they are bilinearly interpolated and, if non-degenerate, the shading geometry is set. The shading normal partial derivatives are computed similarly, and a rotation matrix from the geometric normal to the shading normal is applied to dpdu and dpdv to produce the shading tangent vectors.

> 如果存在逐顶点着色法线，对其进行双线性插值，若结果非退化，则设置着色几何。着色法线偏导的计算类似，并将从几何法线到着色法线的旋转矩阵应用于 dpdu 和 dpdv，产生着色切线向量。

### 6.6.2 Sampling

The sampling routines for bilinear patches select between algorithms depending on patch characteristics. For area sampling, both rectangular patches and patches with image-based emission distribution receive special treatment. For solid angle sampling from a reference point, rectangular patches are projected onto the sphere and sampled as spherical rectangles. General-purpose sampling routines are used otherwise.

> 双线性面片的采样例程根据面片特性选择不同算法。对于面积采样，矩形面片和具有基于图像的发射分布的面片都有特殊处理。从参考点按立体角采样时，矩形面片被投影到球面上作为球面矩形采样。其他情况使用通用采样例程。

**Area Sampling.** The area sampling method first samples parametric (u,v) coordinates. If an emission image distribution exists (for DiffuseAreaLight with spatially varying emission), the sample is drawn from it. Otherwise, if the patch is not a rectangle, an approximation to uniform area sampling is used: the differential surface area at each of the four corners is computed (as the magnitude of the cross product of differences with adjacent vertices) and used as weights for bilinear sampling via SampleBilinear/BilinearPDF. If the patch is a rectangle, uniform area sampling is trivial. An exact equal-area sampling algorithm cannot be derived for bilinear patches because the area integral of Equation (6.12) cannot be evaluated in closed form. Approximating the differential area with a bilinear function of corner values works well in practice.

> **面积采样。** 面积采样方法首先采样参数坐标 (u,v)。若存在发射图像分布（用于 DiffuseAreaLight 的空间变化发射），则从中抽样。否则，若面片不是矩形，使用近似均匀面积采样：计算四个角的微分面积（相邻顶点差的叉积模），用作 SampleBilinear/BilinearPDF 的权重进行双线性采样。若面片是矩形，均匀面积采样是平凡的。双线性面片无法推导精确的等面积采样算法，因为方程 (6.12) 的面积积分无法求闭式解。用角点值的双线性函数近似微分面积在实践中效果良好。

The PDF value stored is with respect to the parametric domain. To return a PDF with respect to surface area, it is necessary to account for the change of variables, dividing by the magnitude of the cross product of dpdu and dpdv. The corresponding PDF() method computes the parametric (u,v) from the interaction (using InvertBilinear if per-vertex texture coordinates exist), evaluates the parametric PDF, then transforms to surface area measure.

> 存储的 PDF 值相对于参数域。要返回相对于表面面积的 PDF，需要进行变量替换，除以 dpdu 和 dpdv 叉积的模。对应的 PDF() 方法从交互信息计算参数 (u,v)（若存在逐顶点纹理坐标则使用 InvertBilinear），评估参数 PDF，然后转换为面积度量。

**Solid Angle Sampling.** For non-rectangular patches, patches with image emission distributions, or patches subtending very small solid angles (below MinSphericalSampleArea = 1e-4), the standard approach is used: sample by area and convert the PDF to solid angle measure. For rectangular patches, a specialized technique projects the rectangle onto the sphere via SampleSphericalRectangle(), which is an analog of SampleSphericalTriangle() for rectangles. The implementation follows spherical trigonometry similar to the triangle case, but its derivation is not included in the text.

> **立体角采样。** 对于非矩形面片、具有图像发射分布的面片或所张立体角很小（低于 MinSphericalSampleArea = 1e-4）的面片，使用标准方法：按面积采样并将 PDF 转换为立体角度量。对于矩形面片，使用专门技术通过 SampleSphericalRectangle() 将矩形投影到球面上——它是 SampleSphericalTriangle() 的矩形版本。实现涉及与三角形情况类似的球面三角学，但文中未包含其推导。

Also as with triangles, an approximation to the cos θ factor at the reference point is incorporated by warping the uniform sample u using a bilinear function. The weights are computed as |cos θ| for each corner direction relative to the reference surface normal, clamped to a minimum of 0.01. The spherical rectangle sampling algorithm maintains the correspondence between each corner of the sampling space and the corresponding corner of the patch in parametric space. The sampled point's (u,v) coordinates on the rectangle are found by projecting onto the edge vectors, and texture coordinates are interpolated if available.

> 与三角形一样，通过用双线性函数扭曲均匀样本 u 来近似参考点处的 cos θ 因子。权重计算为每个角方向相对于参考表面法线的 |cos θ|，下限钳制为 0.01。球面矩形采样算法保持采样空间各角与面片参数空间各角的对应关系。矩形上采样点的 (u,v) 坐标通过投影到边向量上求得，若有纹理坐标则进行插值。

The associated PDF() method for solid angle sampling first performs a ray-patch intersection to find the SurfaceInteraction. For area-sampled patches, the area PDF is converted to solid angle. For spherically sampled rectangles, the uniform solid angle PDF is 1/SphericalQuadArea. If the cos θ approximation was applied, InvertSphericalRectangleSample() recovers the sample value u that maps to the intersection point, and BilinearPDF provides the corresponding bilinear warp factor.

> 立体角采样的关联 PDF() 方法首先执行光线-面片求交以找到 SurfaceInteraction。对于面积采样的面片，将面积 PDF 转换为立体角。对于球面采样的矩形，均匀立体角 PDF 为 1/SphericalQuadArea。若应用了 cos θ 近似，InvertSphericalRectangleSample() 恢复映射到交点的样本值 u，BilinearPDF 提供对应的双线性扭曲因子。
