## 5.4 Film and Imaging

After the camera's projection or lens system forms an image of the scene on the film, it is necessary to model how the film measures light to create the final image generated by the renderer. This section starts with an overview of the radiometry of how light is measured on the film and then continues with the topic of how spectral energy is converted to tristimulus colors (typically, RGB). This leads to the PixelSensor class, which models that process as well as further processing that is generally performed by cameras. After next considering how image samples on the film are accumulated into the pixels of the final image, we introduce the Film interface and then two implementations of it that put this model into practice.

> 在相机的投影或透镜系统在胶片上形成场景图像之后，需要建模胶片如何测量光线以创建渲染器生成的最终图像。本节从概述胶片上如何测量光线的辐射度量开始，然后继续讨论如何将光谱能量转换为三刺激值颜色（通常是 RGB）。这引出了 PixelSensor 类，它对该过程以及相机通常执行的进一步处理进行建模。在接下来考虑如何将胶片上的图像样本累积到最终图像的像素之后，我们介绍 Film 接口，然后介绍将该模型付诸实践的两种实现。

### 5.4.1 The Camera Measurement Equation

Given a simulation of the process of real image formation, it is also worthwhile to more carefully define the radiometry of the measurement made by a film or a camera sensor. Rays from the rear of the lens to the film carry radiance from the scene. As considered from a point on the film plane, there is thus a set of directions from which radiance is incident.

> 给定真实成像过程的模拟，更仔细地定义胶片或相机传感器所做测量的辐射度量也是值得的。从透镜后方到胶片的光线携带来自场景的辐射。从胶片平面上的一个点来看，因此存在一组辐射入射的方向。

Given the incident radiance function, we can define the irradiance at a point on the film plane. Starting with the definition of irradiance in terms of radiance (Equation 4.7), we can convert from an integral over solid angle to an integral over area (the area of the plane tangent to the rear lens element) using Equation 4.9. Because the film plane is parallel to the lens's plane, the cosine terms simplify. The distance between film point and lens point equals the axial distance z divided by cos θ. Putting this together gives Equation 5.3.

> 给定入射辐射函数，我们可以定义胶片平面上一点的辐照度。从辐射定义辐照度出发（公式 4.7），我们可以使用公式 4.9 将立体角上的积分转换为面积上的积分（与后镜片元件相切的平面面积）。因为胶片平面与透镜平面平行，余弦项得以简化。胶片点与透镜点之间的距离等于轴向距离 z 除以 cos θ。综合这些得到公式 5.3。

For cameras where the extent of the film is relatively large with respect to the distance z, the cos⁴θ term can meaningfully reduce the incident irradiance—this factor also contributes to vignetting. Most modern digital cameras correct for this effect with preset correction factors.

> 对于胶片范围相对于距离 z 较大的相机，cos⁴θ 项可以显著降低入射辐照度——这个因素也导致暗角。大多数现代数码相机通过预设校正因子来补偿这种效应。

Integrating irradiance at a point on the film over the time that the shutter is open gives *radiant exposure* (also known as *fluence*), which is the radiometric unit for energy per area, J/m² (Equation 5.4). Taking that and also integrating over sensor pixel area A_p, we have the Joules arriving at a pixel (Equation 5.5)—this is called the *camera measurement equation*.

> 将胶片上一点的辐照度对快门打开时间积分得到*辐射曝光*（也称为*通量*），是能量每面积的辐射度量单位 J/m²（公式 5.4）。在此基础上再对传感器像素面积 A_p 积分，得到到达一个像素的焦耳数（公式 5.5）——这被称为*相机测量方程*。

Although these factors apply to all of the camera models, they are only included in the implementation of the RealisticCamera. The reason is purely pragmatic: most renderers do not model this effect, so omitting it from the simpler camera models makes it easier to compare images rendered by pbrt with those rendered by other systems.

> 虽然这些因素适用于所有相机模型，但它们仅包含在 RealisticCamera 的实现中。原因纯粹是实用的：大多数渲染器不建模这种效应，所以从较简单的相机模型中省略它使得比较 pbrt 渲染的图像与其他系统渲染的图像更容易。

### 5.4.2 Modeling Sensor Response

Traditional film is based on a chemical process where silver halide crystals produce silver bromide when exposed to light. Modern digital cameras use CCD or CMOS sensors where each pixel effectively counts the number of photons it is exposed to by transforming photons into electrical charge.

> 传统胶片基于化学过程，银卤化物晶体在暴露于光线时产生溴化银。现代数码相机使用 CCD 或 CMOS 传感器，每个像素通过将光子转换为电荷来有效计数其暴露的光子数量。

For both film and digital sensors, color measurements at pixels can be modeled using spectral response curves. These functions are defined such that, given an incident spectral distribution L(λ), a pixel's red component is given by:

> 对于胶片和数字传感器，像素处的颜色测量可以使用光谱响应曲线来建模。这些函数被定义为，给定入射光谱分布 L(λ)，像素的红色分量由以下公式给出：

r = ∫ L(λ) r̄(λ) dλ (Equation 5.6)

> r = ∫ L(λ) r̄(λ) dλ（公式 5.6）

Digital sensor pixels are typically arranged in a *mosaic*, with twice as many green pixels as red and blue, due to the human visual system's greater sensitivity to green. A *demosaicing* algorithm must be used to convert these sensor pixels to image pixels where the RGB components are colocated.

> 数字传感器像素通常排列成*马赛克*模式，绿色像素数量是红色和蓝色的两倍，因为人类视觉系统对绿色更敏感。必须使用*去马赛克*算法将这些传感器像素转换为 RGB 分量共存的图像像素。

Each pixel must receive a sufficient amount of light to capture an accurate image. The energy captured at a pixel depends on the incident radiance, the pixel area, the exit pupil area, and the exposure time. Sensors and film therefore provide an additional control in the form of an *ISO setting*. For physical film, ISO encodes its responsiveness to light. In digital cameras, ISO controls the *gain*—a scaling factor applied to pixel values. Because pbrt does not model sensor noise, the ISO value can be set arbitrarily to achieve a desired exposure.

> 每个像素必须接收足够的光量才能捕获准确的图像。像素捕获的能量取决于入射辐射、像素面积、出射瞳面积和曝光时间。因此传感器和胶片以 *ISO 设置*的形式提供额外的控制。对于物理胶片，ISO 编码其对光的响应能力。在数码相机中，ISO 控制*增益*——应用于像素值的缩放因子。因为 pbrt 不建模传感器噪声，ISO 值可以任意设置以实现所需的曝光。

In pbrt's sensor model, we model neither mosaicing nor noise, nor other effects like blooming or rolling shutter. The PixelSensor class implements pbrt's semi-idealized model of pixel color measurement.

> 在 pbrt 的传感器模型中，我们不建模马赛克、噪声，也不建模溢出或卷帘快门等其他效果。PixelSensor 类实现了 pbrt 的半理想化像素颜色测量模型。

PixelSensor models three components of sensor pixels' operation:

> PixelSensor 对传感器像素操作的三个组件进行建模：

1. *Exposure controls:* These are the user-settable parameters that control how bright or dark the image is.
2. *RGB response:* PixelSensor uses spectral response curves based on measurements of physical camera sensors to model the conversion of spectral radiance to tristimulus colors.
3. *White balance:* Cameras generally process images to adjust initial RGB values according to the color of illumination to model chromatic adaptation.

> 1. *曝光控制*：这些是用户可设置的参数，控制图像的明暗程度。
> 2. *RGB 响应*：PixelSensor 使用基于物理相机传感器测量的光谱响应曲线来建模从光谱辐射到三刺激值颜色的转换。
> 3. *白平衡*：相机通常处理图像以根据照明颜色调整初始 RGB 值，从而建模色度适应。

The RGB color space in which a sensor pixel records light is generally not the same as the RGB color space that the user has specified for the final image. Therefore, the PixelSensor constructor computes a matrix that converts from its RGB space to XYZ. From there, it is easy to convert to a particular output color space.

> 传感器像素记录光线的 RGB 色彩空间通常与用户为最终图像指定的 RGB 色彩空间不同。因此，PixelSensor 构造函数计算一个从其 RGB 空间转换到 XYZ 的矩阵。从 XYZ 可以很容易地转换到特定的输出色彩空间。

This matrix is found by solving an optimization problem: starting with over twenty spectral distributions representing the reflectance of patches from a standardized color chart. The constructor computes the RGB colors of those patches under the camera's illuminant in the camera's color space as well as their XYZ colors under the illuminant of the output color space. This is an over-constrained problem that can be solved using linear least squares (Equation 5.7).

> 该矩阵通过求解优化问题找到：从二十多个代表标准色卡色块反射率的光谱分布开始。构造函数在相机光照下计算这些色块在相机色彩空间中的 RGB 颜色，以及在输出色彩空间光照下的 XYZ 颜色。这是一个过约束问题，可以使用线性最小二乘法求解（公式 5.7）。

The main functionality provided by the PixelSensor is the ToSensorRGB() method, which converts a point-sampled spectral distribution in a SampledSpectrum to RGB coefficients in the sensor's color space. It does so via Monte Carlo evaluation of the sensor response integral (Equation 5.8).

> PixelSensor 提供的主要功能是 ToSensorRGB() 方法，它将 SampledSpectrum 中的点采样光谱分布转换为传感器色彩空间中的 RGB 系数。它通过蒙特卡罗评估传感器响应积分来实现（公式 5.8）。

#### Chromatic Adaptation and White Balance

One of the remarkable properties of the human visual system is that the color of objects is generally seen as the same, even under different lighting conditions; this effect is called *chromatic adaptation*. Cameras perform a similar function called *white balancing*.

> 人类视觉系统的一个显著特性是，即使在不同的照明条件下，物体的颜色通常看起来也是相同的；这种效应称为*色度适应*。相机执行类似的功能称为*白平衡*。

pbrt provides a WhiteBalance() function that implements the *von Kries transform*. White balance with the von Kries transform is performed in the *LMS* color space, where the responsivity of the three matching functions is specified to match the three types of cone in the human eye. By performing white balancing in the LMS space, we can model the effect of modulating the contribution of each type of cone, which is believed to be how chromatic adaptation is implemented in humans.

> pbrt 提供了一个实现 *von Kries 变换*的 WhiteBalance() 函数。von Kries 变换的白平衡在 *LMS* 色彩空间中执行，其中三个匹配函数的响应性被指定为匹配人眼中三种锥体细胞。通过在 LMS 空间中执行白平衡，我们可以建模调制每种锥体贡献的效果，这被认为是色度适应在人类中实现的方式。

Given a color in LMS space, white balancing is performed by dividing out the color of the scene's illuminant and then multiplying by the color of the desired illuminant, which can be represented by a diagonal matrix. The complete white balance matrix that operates on XYZ colors follows directly.

> 给定 LMS 空间中的颜色，白平衡通过除去场景照明光源的颜色然后乘以所需照明光源的颜色来执行，这可以用对角矩阵表示。操作 XYZ 颜色的完整白平衡矩阵直接导出。

#### Sampling Sensor Response

Because the sensor response functions describe the sensor's wavelength-dependent response to radiance, it is worth approximately accounting for their variation when sampling the wavelengths of light that a ray is to carry. A parametric probability distribution function that balances concerns of visual importance and sampling efficiency is:

> 因为传感器响应函数描述了传感器对辐射的波长依赖响应，在采样光线携带的光波长时大致考虑它们的变化是值得的。一个平衡视觉重要性和采样效率的参数化概率分布函数为：

p(λ) ∝ 1/cosh²(0.0072(λ − 538)) (Equation 5.9)

> p(λ) ∝ 1/cosh²(0.0072(λ − 538))（公式 5.9）

The SampledWavelengths class provides a SampleVisible() method that uses this technique, sampling over the wavelength range from 360 to 830 nm. Using this distribution in place of a uniform distribution greatly reduces color noise with only a 1% increase in runtime.

> SampledWavelengths 类提供了一个使用此技术的 SampleVisible() 方法，在 360 到 830 nm 的波长范围内采样。使用此分布代替均匀分布大大减少了颜色噪声，运行时间仅增加 1%。

### 5.4.3 Filtering Image Samples

The main responsibility of Film implementations is to aggregate multiple spectral samples at each pixel in order to compute a final value for it. A filter function is used to define the spatial variation in sensor response around each image pixel. The filtered red value at a position p is given by:

> Film 实现的主要职责是聚合每个像素处的多个光谱样本以计算其最终值。滤波函数用于定义每个图像像素周围传感器响应的空间变化。位置 p 处的滤波后红色值由以下公式给出：

I(p) = ∫ f(p − p') r(p') dp' / ∫ f(p − p') dp' (Equation 5.10)

> I(p) = ∫ f(p − p') r(p') dp' / ∫ f(p − p') dp'（公式 5.10）

Two approaches have been used in graphics to sample the integrand. The first (used in previous versions of pbrt) is to sample the image uniformly, where each sample may contribute to multiple pixels. The second approach (used in this version of pbrt) is *filter importance sampling*, where Equation 5.10 is estimated independently at each pixel using a distribution based on the filter function.

> 图形学中使用了两种方法来采样被积函数。第一种（在以前版本的 pbrt 中使用）是均匀采样图像，每个样本可能贡献给多个像素。第二种方法（在此版本 pbrt 中使用）是*滤波器重要性采样*，使用基于滤波函数的分布在每个像素独立估计公式 5.10。

This version of pbrt uses the weighted importance sampling estimator, computing pixel values as:

> 此版本的 pbrt 使用加权重要性采样估计器，像素值计算为：

I(p) ≈ Σᵢ sign(f(pᵢ)) r(pᵢ) / Σᵢ sign(f(pᵢ)) (Equation 5.13)

> I(p) ≈ Σᵢ sign(f(pᵢ)) r(pᵢ) / Σᵢ sign(f(pᵢ))（公式 5.13）

Filter importance sampling has several advantages: it makes parallel rendering easier (no concurrent pixel modification), and if there are variance spike samples ("fireflies"), they only affect a single pixel rather than being smeared over multiple pixels.

> 滤波器重要性采样有几个优势：它使并行渲染更容易（不会并发修改同一像素），而且如果存在方差尖峰样本（"萤火虫"），它们只影响单个像素而不会扩散到多个像素。

### 5.4.4 The Film Interface

The Film interface is defined in the file base/film.h. Samples can be provided to the film in two ways:

> Film 接口定义在文件 base/film.h 中。样本可以通过两种方式提供给胶片：

1. **AddSample()**: from the Sampler selecting points on the film at which the Integrator estimates the radiance. Film implementations can assume that multiple threads will not call AddSample() concurrently with the same pFilm location.

> 1. **AddSample()**：由 Sampler 选择胶片上的点，Integrator 在这些点估计辐射。Film 实现可以假设多个线程不会同时用相同的 pFilm 位置调用 AddSample()。

2. **AddSplat()**: for light transport algorithms that sample paths starting from the light sources and need to "splat" contributions to arbitrary pixels. Rather than computing a weighted average, splats are simply summed. This method may be called concurrently by multiple threads, so Film implementations must use mutual exclusion or atomic operations.

> 2. **AddSplat()**：用于从光源开始采样路径并需要向任意像素"溅射"贡献的光传输算法。溅射值不计算加权平均，而是简单地累加。此方法可能被多个线程并发调用，因此 Film 实现必须使用互斥锁或原子操作。

Film implementations must also provide methods for: SampleWavelengths(), FullResolution(), PixelBounds(), Diagonal() (sensor diagonal in meters), WriteImage(), ToOutputRGB(), GetImage(), GetPixelRGB(), GetFilter(), GetPixelSensor(), and GetFilename().

> Film 实现还必须提供以下方法：SampleWavelengths()、FullResolution()、PixelBounds()、Diagonal()（以米为单位的传感器对角线）、WriteImage()、ToOutputRGB()、GetImage()、GetPixelRGB()、GetFilter()、GetPixelSensor() 和 GetFilename()。

### 5.4.5 Common Film Functionality

A FilmBase class collects commonly used member variables and provides a number of the methods required by the Film interface. The FilmBase constructor takes: the overall resolution of the image in pixels, a bounding box for a possible crop region, a filter function, a PixelSensor, the length of the diagonal of the film's physical area, and the filename for the output image.

> FilmBase 类收集常用的成员变量并提供 Film 接口所需的许多方法。FilmBase 构造函数接受：以像素为单位的图像总分辨率、可能的裁剪区域的包围盒、滤波函数、PixelSensor、胶片物理面积对角线长度以及输出图像的文件名。

An implementation of SampleWavelengths() samples according to the distribution in Equation 5.9. The SampleBounds() method computes the sample bounds by expanding by the filter radius and accounting for half-pixel offsets.

> SampleWavelengths() 的实现根据公式 5.9 中的分布进行采样。SampleBounds() 方法通过按滤波半径扩展并考虑半像素偏移来计算采样边界。

### 5.4.6 RGBFilm

RGBFilm records an image represented by RGB color. In addition to the parameters passed to FilmBase, it takes a color space for the output image, a parameter for the maximum value of an RGB component (for clamping), and a parameter controlling floating-point precision in the output image.

> RGBFilm 记录由 RGB 颜色表示的图像。除了传递给 FilmBase 的参数外，它还接受输出图像的色彩空间、RGB 分量最大值参数（用于钳制）以及控制输出图像浮点精度的参数。

The running weighted sums of pixel contributions are represented using RGB colors in the rgbSum member variable. weightSum holds the sum of filter weight values. These correspond to the numerator and denominator in Equation 5.13. rgbSplat holds an unweighted sum of sample splats.

> 像素贡献的运行加权和使用 rgbSum 成员变量中的 RGB 颜色表示。weightSum 保存滤波权重值的和。这些分别对应公式 5.13 中的分子和分母。rgbSplat 保存样本溅射的未加权和。

Double-precision floating point is used for all of these quantities. Single-precision floats are almost always sufficient, but when used for reference images rendered with high sample counts they may have insufficient precision.

> 所有这些量都使用双精度浮点。单精度浮点几乎总是足够的，但当用于高采样数渲染的参考图像时可能精度不足。

AddSample() converts spectral radiance to sensor RGB, optionally clamps it (to suppress "firefly" variance spikes), then updates the pixel's running sums. The clamping approach scales the RGB value so its maximum component equals maxComponentValue.

> AddSample() 将光谱辐射转换为传感器 RGB，可选地进行钳制（以抑制"萤火虫"方差尖峰），然后更新像素的运行和。钳制方法缩放 RGB 值使其最大分量等于 maxComponentValue。

AddSplat() uses the filter function to distribute splatted contributions to multiple pixels within the filter's radius, using atomic operations for thread safety.

> AddSplat() 使用滤波函数将溅射贡献分配到滤波半径内的多个像素，使用原子操作保证线程安全。

GetPixelRGB() returns the final RGB value: it computes the weighted average from rgbSum/weightSum, adds the splat contribution normalized by the filter integral, and applies the color space conversion matrix.

> GetPixelRGB() 返回最终 RGB 值：它从 rgbSum/weightSum 计算加权平均值，加上由滤波积分归一化的溅射贡献，并应用色彩空间转换矩阵。

### 5.4.7 GBufferFilm

The GBufferFilm stores not only RGB at each pixel, but also additional information about the geometry at the first visible intersection point. This additional information is useful for image denoising algorithms, providing training data for machine learning, etc.

> GBufferFilm 不仅在每个像素存储 RGB，还存储关于第一个可见交点处几何信息的附加数据。这些附加信息对图像去噪算法、为机器学习提供训练数据等很有用。

Its Pixel structure augments the one used in RGBFilm with additional fields: position, normals (geometric and shading), UV coordinates, depth derivatives (dzdx, dzdy), albedo, and per-channel variance estimates using the VarianceEstimator class.

> 其 Pixel 结构在 RGBFilm 使用的结构基础上增加了额外字段：位置、法线（几何和着色）、UV 坐标、深度导数（dzdx、dzdy）、反照率以及使用 VarianceEstimator 类的逐通道方差估计。
