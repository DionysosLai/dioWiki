## 5.3 Spherical Camera

One advantage of ray tracing compared to scan line or rasterization-based rendering methods is that it is easy to employ unusual image projections. We have great freedom in how the image sample positions are mapped into ray directions, since the rendering algorithm does not depend on properties such as straight lines in the scene always projecting to straight lines in the image.

> 与扫描线或光栅化渲染方法相比，光线追踪的一个优势是可以轻松使用非常规的图像投影方式。我们在如何将图像采样位置映射到光线方向方面拥有很大的自由度，因为渲染算法不依赖于诸如"场景中的直线总是投影为图像中的直线"这样的属性。

In this section, we will describe a camera model that traces rays in all directions around a point in the scene, giving a view of everything that is visible from that point. The SphericalCamera supports two spherical parameterizations from Section 3.8 to map points in the image to associated directions. Figure 5.16 shows this camera in action with the *San Miguel* model.

> 在本节中，我们将描述一种相机模型，它从场景中的一个点向所有方向发射光线，提供从该点可见的一切事物的视图。SphericalCamera 支持第 3.8 节中的两种球面参数化方法，将图像中的点映射到相应的方向。图 5.16 展示了该相机在 *San Miguel* 模型中的使用效果。

SphericalCamera does not derive from ProjectiveCamera since the projections that it uses are nonlinear and cannot be captured by a single matrix.

> SphericalCamera 不继承自 ProjectiveCamera，因为它使用的投影是非线性的，无法用单个矩阵来表示。

The first mapping that SphericalCamera supports is the **equirectangular mapping** that was defined in Section 3.8.3. In the implementation here, θ values range from 0 at the top of the image to π at the bottom of the image, and φ values range from 0 to 2π, moving from left to right across the image.

> SphericalCamera 支持的第一种映射是第 3.8.3 节中定义的**等距矩形映射**（equirectangular mapping）。在这里的实现中，θ 值从图像顶部的 0 到底部的 π，φ 值从 0 到 2π，从图像的左侧到右侧。

The equirectangular mapping is easy to evaluate and has the advantage that lines of constant latitude and longitude on the sphere remain straight. However, it preserves neither area nor angles between curves on the sphere (i.e., it is not *conformal*). These issues are especially evident at the top and bottom of the image in Figure 5.16 (a).

> 等距矩形映射易于计算，且优点是球面上的等纬线和等经线保持为直线。然而，它既不保面积也不保角（即不是*保角映射*）。这些问题在图 5.16 (a) 图像的顶部和底部尤为明显。

Therefore, the SphericalCamera also supports the **equal-area mapping** from Section 3.8.3. With this mapping, any finite solid angle of directions on the sphere maps to the same area in the image, regardless of where it is on the sphere. (This mapping is also used by the ImageInfiniteLight, which is described in Section 12.5.2, and so images rendered using this camera can be used as light sources.) The equal-area mapping's use with the SphericalCamera is shown in Figure 5.16 (b).

> 因此，SphericalCamera 还支持第 3.8.3 节中的**等面积映射**（equal-area mapping）。使用这种映射，球面上任何有限立体角的方向都映射到图像中相同大小的面积，无论它位于球面的哪个位置。（此映射也被 ImageInfiniteLight 使用，该光源在第 12.5.2 节中描述，因此使用此相机渲染的图像可以用作光源。）SphericalCamera 使用等面积映射的效果如图 5.16 (b) 所示。

An enumeration reflects which mapping should be used.

> 一个枚举值指示应使用哪种映射方式。

The main task of the GenerateRay() method is to apply the requested mapping. For the use of both mappings, coordinates in NDC space are found by dividing the raster space sample location by the image's overall resolution. Then, after the mapping is applied, the y and z coordinates are swapped to account for the fact that both mappings are defined with y as the "up" direction, while z is "up" in camera space.

> GenerateRay() 方法的主要任务是应用所请求的映射。对于两种映射的使用，NDC 空间中的坐标通过将光栅空间采样位置除以图像的整体分辨率来获得。然后在应用映射后，y 和 z 坐标被交换，因为两种映射都以 y 作为"上"方向，而在相机空间中 z 才是"上"方向。

For the equirectangular mapping, the coordinates are scaled to cover the range [0, π] × [0, 2π] and the spherical coordinate formula is used to compute the ray direction.

> 对于等距矩形映射，坐标被缩放到覆盖 [0, π] × [0, 2π] 的范围，然后使用球坐标公式计算光线方向。

The values for the CameraSample may be slightly outside of the [0, 1] range, due to the pixel sample filter function. A call to WrapEqualAreaSquare() takes care of handling the boundary conditions before EqualAreaSquareToSphere() performs the actual mapping.

> CameraSample 的值可能由于像素采样滤波函数而略微超出 [0, 1] 范围。在 EqualAreaSquareToSphere() 执行实际映射之前，调用 WrapEqualAreaSquare() 来处理边界条件。
