## 11.2 Transmittance

The scattering processes in Section [11.1](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Volume_Scattering/Volume_Scattering_Processes.html#sec:volume-scattering-processes) are all specified in terms of their local effect at points in space. However, in rendering, we are usually interested in their aggregate effects on radiance along a ray, which usually requires transforming the differential equations to integral equations that can be solved using Monte Carlo. The reduction in radiance between two points on a ray due to extinction is a quantity that will often be useful; for example, we will need to estimate this value to compute the attenuated radiance from a light source that is incident at a point on a surface in scenes with participating media.

> 第 11.1 节中的散射过程都是根据它们在空间中各点的局部效果来规定的。然而，在渲染中，我们通常对它们沿光线对辐射亮度的累积效果感兴趣，这通常需要将微分方程转换为可以使用蒙特卡洛求解的积分方程。光线上两点之间由于消光而导致的辐射亮度减少是一个经常有用的量；例如，在具有参与介质的场景中，我们需要估计这个值来计算入射到表面某点的光源的衰减辐射亮度。

### 11.2.1 Null Scattering

> ### 11.2.1 零散射

The key idea that makes it possible to derive a more easily sampled transmittance integral is an approach known as *null scattering*. Null scattering is a mathematical formalism that can be interpreted as introducing an additional type of scattering that does not correspond to any type of physical scattering process but is specified so that it has no effect on the distribution of light. In doing so, null scattering makes it possible to treat inhomogeneous media as if they were homogeneous, which makes it easier to apply sampling algorithms to inhomogeneous media. (In Chapter [14](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Transport_II_Volume_Rendering.html#chap:volume-integration), we will see that it is a key foundation for volumetric light transport algorithms beyond transmittance estimation.)

> 使得推导更容易采样的透射率积分成为可能的关键思想是一种称为*零散射*的方法。零散射是一种数学形式，可以解释为引入一种额外的散射类型，它不对应任何物理散射过程，但被设定为对光的分布没有影响。通过这样做，零散射使得可以将非均匀介质当作均匀介质来处理，从而更容易对非均匀介质应用采样算法。（在第 14 章中，我们将看到它是超越透射率估计的体积光传输算法的关键基础。）

Thus, recursive estimation of transmittance continues either until termination due to Russian roulette or until the sampled point is past the endpoint. This approach is the *track-length transmittance estimator*, also known as *delta tracking*.

A physical interpretation of delta tracking is that it randomly decides whether the ray interacts with a true particle or a fictitious particle at each scattering event. Interactions with fictitious particles (corresponding to null scattering) are ignored and the algorithm continues, restarting from the sampled point. Interactions with true particles cause extinction, in which case 0 is returned. If a ray makes it through the medium without extinction, the value 1 is returned.

> delta 追踪的物理解释是，它在每个散射事件处随机决定光线是与真实粒子还是虚拟粒子交互。与虚拟粒子（对应零散射）的交互被忽略，算法继续从采样点重新开始。与真实粒子的交互导致消光，在这种情况下返回 0。如果光线穿过介质而没有被消光，则返回值 1。
