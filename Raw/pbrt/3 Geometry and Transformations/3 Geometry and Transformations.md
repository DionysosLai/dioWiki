## 3 Geometry and Transformations

Almost all nontrivial graphics programs are built on a foundation of geometric classes that represent mathematical constructs like points, vectors, and rays. Because these classes are ubiquitous throughout the system, good abstractions and efficient implementations are critical. This chapter presents the interface to and implementation of pbrt 's geometric foundation. Note that these are not the classes that represent the actual scene geometry (triangles, spheres, etc.); those classes are the topic of Chapter [6](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Shapes.html#chap:shapes).

> 几乎所有非平凡的图形程序都建立在表示点、向量和光线等数学构造的几何类基础之上。由于这些类在系统中无处不在，良好的抽象和高效的实现至关重要。本章介绍 pbrt 几何基础的接口与实现。需要注意的是，这些并非表示实际场景几何体（三角形、球体等）的类；那些类是第 6 章的主题。
