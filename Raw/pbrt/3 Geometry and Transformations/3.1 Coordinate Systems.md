## 3.1 Coordinate Systems

As is typical in computer graphics, pbrt represents three-dimensional points, vectors, and normal vectors with three coordinate values:,, and. These values are meaningless without a *coordinate system* that defines the origin of the space and gives three linearly independent vectors that define the,, and axes of the space. Together, the origin and three vectors are called the *frame* that defines the coordinate system. Given an arbitrary point or direction in 3D, its coordinate values depend on its relationship to the frame. Figure [3.1](#fig:point-wrt-coordsys) shows an example that illustrates this idea in 2D.

> 与计算机图形学中的惯例一样，pbrt 使用三个坐标值 x、y 和 z 来表示三维点、向量和法向量。这些值如果没有一个定义了空间原点并给出三个线性无关向量（定义空间 x、y、z 轴）的*坐标系*，就毫无意义。原点和三个向量一起被称为定义坐标系的*框架*（frame）。给定 3D 中的任意点或方向，其坐标值取决于它与框架的关系。图 3.1 展示了一个二维中说明这一思想的例子。

[![](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Geometry_and_Transformations/pha03f01.svg)](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Geometry_and_Transformations/pha03f01.svg)

Figure 3.1: In 2D, the coordinates of a point are defined by the relationship of the point to a particular 2D coordinate system. Here, two coordinate systems are shown; the point might have coordinates with respect to the coordinate system with its coordinate axes drawn in solid lines but have coordinates with respect to the coordinate system with dashed axes. In either case, the 2D point is at the same absolute position in space.

> 图 3.1：在二维中，一个点的坐标由该点与特定二维坐标系的关系定义。这里展示了两个坐标系；该点相对于实线坐标轴的坐标系可能有一组坐标，但相对于虚线坐标轴的坐标系则有另一组坐标。无论哪种情况，该二维点在空间中的绝对位置是相同的。

In the general -dimensional case, a frame's origin and its linearly independent basis vectors define an -dimensional *affine space*. All vectors in the space can be expressed as a linear combination of the basis vectors. Given a vector and the basis vectors, there is a unique set of scalar values such that

> 在一般的 n 维情形中，框架的原点及其线性无关的基向量定义了一个 n 维*仿射空间*。空间中所有向量都可以表示为基向量的线性组合。给定一个向量和基向量，存在唯一的一组标量值使得该向量可以表示为基向量的线性组合。

The scalars are the *representation* of with respect to the basis and are the coordinate values that we store with the vector. Similarly, for all points, there are unique scalars such that the point can be expressed in terms of the origin and the basis vectors

> 这些标量是向量相对于基的*表示*，也是我们与向量一起存储的坐标值。类似地，对于所有的点，存在唯一的标量使得该点可以用原点和基向量来表示。

Thus, although points and vectors are both represented by,, and coordinates in 3D, they are distinct mathematical entities and are not freely interchangeable.

> 因此，虽然点和向量在三维中都由 x、y、z 坐标表示，但它们是不同的数学实体，不能自由互换。

This definition of points and vectors in terms of coordinate systems reveals a paradox: to define a frame we need a point and a set of vectors, but we can only meaningfully talk about points and vectors with respect to a particular frame. Therefore, in three dimensions we need a *standard frame* with origin and basis vectors,, and. All other frames will be defined with respect to this canonical coordinate system, which we call *world space*.

> 用坐标系来定义点和向量揭示了一个悖论：要定义一个框架我们需要一个点和一组向量，但我们只能相对于某个特定框架来有意义地谈论点和向量。因此，在三维中我们需要一个具有原点和基向量的*标准框架*。所有其他框架都将相对于这个规范坐标系来定义，我们称之为*世界空间*。

### 3.1.1 Coordinate System Handedness

There are two different ways that the three coordinate axes can be arranged, as shown in Figure [3.2](#fig:handedness). Given perpendicular and coordinate axes, the axis can point in one of two directions. These two choices are called *left-handed* and *right-handed*. The choice between the two is arbitrary but has a number of implications for how some of the geometric operations throughout the system are implemented. pbrt uses a left-handed coordinate system.

> 三个坐标轴有两种不同的排列方式，如图 3.2 所示。给定相互垂直的 x 和 y 坐标轴，z 轴可以指向两个方向之一。这两种选择分别称为*左手坐标系*和*右手坐标系*。两者之间的选择是任意的，但对系统中某些几何运算的实现方式有若干影响。pbrt 使用左手坐标系。

[![](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Geometry_and_Transformations/pha03f02.svg)](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Geometry_and_Transformations/pha03f02.svg)

Figure 3.2: (a) In a left-handed coordinate system, the axis points into the page when the and axes are oriented with pointing to the right and pointing up. (b) In a right-handed system, the axis points out of the page.

> 图 3.2：(a) 在左手坐标系中，当 x 轴朝右、y 轴朝上时，z 轴指向页面内部。(b) 在右手坐标系中，z 轴指向页面外部。
