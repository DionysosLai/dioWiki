## 3.8 Spherical Geometry

Geometry on the unit sphere is also frequently useful in rendering. 3D unit direction vectors can equivalently be represented as points on the unit sphere, and sets of directions can be represented as areas on the unit sphere. Useful operations such as bounding a set of directions can often be cleanly expressed as bounds on the unit sphere. We will therefore introduce some useful principles of spherical geometry and related classes and functions in this section.

> 单位球上的几何在渲染中也经常有用。三维单位方向向量可以等价地表示为单位球上的点，方向集合可以表示为单位球上的面积。诸如约束方向集合之类的有用操作通常可以简洁地表示为单位球上的界限。因此，我们将在本节中介绍球面几何的一些有用原理以及相关的类和函数。

### 3.8.1 Solid Angles

In 2D, the *planar angle* is the total angle subtended by some object with respect to some position (Figure [3.12](#fig:plane-angle)). Consider the unit circle around the point; if we project the shaded object onto that circle, some length of the circle will be covered by its projection. The arc length of (which is the same as the angle ) is the angle subtended by the object. Planar angles are measured in *radians* and the entire unit circle covers radians.

> 在二维中，*平面角*是某个物体相对于某个位置所张的总角度（图 3.12）。考虑围绕该点的单位圆；如果我们将阴影物体投影到该圆上，圆的某段长度将被其投影覆盖。弧长（与角度相同）就是物体所张的角。平面角以*弧度*为单位测量，整个单位圆覆盖 2π 弧度。

The solid angle extends the 2D unit circle to a 3D unit sphere (Figure [3.13](#fig:solid-angle)). The total area is the solid angle subtended by the object. Solid angles are measured in *steradians* (sr). The entire sphere subtends a solid angle of, and a hemisphere subtends.

> 立体角将二维单位圆扩展到三维单位球（图 3.13）。总面积就是物体所张的立体角。立体角以*球面度*（sr）为单位测量。整个球体张开 4π 的立体角，半球张开 2π。

By providing a way to measure area on the unit sphere (and thus over the unit directions), the solid angle also provides the foundation for a measure for integrating spherical functions; the *differential solid angle* corresponds to the differential area measure on the unit sphere.

> 通过提供在单位球上（因此在单位方向上）测量面积的方式，立体角也为积分球面函数的测度提供了基础；*微分立体角*对应于单位球上的微分面积测度。

### 3.8.2 Spherical Polygons

We will sometimes find it useful to consider the set of directions from a point to the surface of a polygon. If a regular planar polygon is projected onto the unit sphere, it forms a *spherical polygon*.

> 我们有时会发现考虑从一个点到多边形表面的方向集合很有用。如果将一个正规平面多边形投影到单位球上，它形成一个*球面多边形*。

The angle at each vertex is given by the angle between the planes corresponding to the two edges that meet at the vertex (the *dihedral angle*). Given the angles, it follows that the area of a spherical triangle can be computed using *Girard's theorem*, which says that a triangle's surface area on the unit sphere is given by the "excess angle":

### 3.8.3 Spherical Parameterizations

The 3D Cartesian coordinates of a point on the unit sphere are not always the most convenient representation of a direction. For example, if we are tabulating a function over the unit sphere, a 2D parameterization that takes advantage of the fact that the sphere's surface is two-dimensional is preferable.

> 单位球上一点的三维笛卡尔坐标并不总是方向的最方便表示。例如，如果我们要在单位球上列表化一个函数，利用球面是二维的这一事实的二维参数化更可取。

There are a variety of mappings between 2D and the sphere. It can be shown that any mapping from the plane to the sphere introduces some form of distortion; the task then is to choose a mapping that best fulfills the requirements for a particular application. pbrt thus uses three different spherical parameterizations, each with different advantages and disadvantages.

> 二维和球面之间有多种映射。可以证明，从平面到球面的任何映射都会引入某种形式的失真；那么任务就是选择最能满足特定应用需求的映射。因此 pbrt 使用三种不同的球面参数化，各有不同的优缺点。

Parameterizing the sphere with spherical coordinates corresponds to the *equirectangular* mapping of the sphere. It is not a particularly good parameterization for representing regularly sampled data on the sphere due to substantial distortion at the sphere's poles.

> 用球面坐标参数化球面对应于球面的*等距矩形*映射。由于在球面极点处存在大量失真，它不是表示球面上规则采样数据的特别好的参数化方式。

#### Octahedral Encoding

While [Vector3f](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Geometry_and_Transformations/Vectors.html#Vector3f) is a convenient representation for computation using unit vectors, it does not use storage efficiently: it uses 12 bytes of memory but is capable of representing 3D direction vectors of arbitrary length. [OctahedralVector](#OctahedralVector) provides a compact representation for unit vectors with an even distribution of precision and efficient encoding and decoding routines, using just 4 bytes of memory for each unit vector.

> 虽然 Vector3f 是使用单位向量进行计算的方便表示，但它的存储效率不高：使用 12 字节内存但能表示任意长度的三维方向向量。OctahedralVector 为单位向量提供了一种紧凑表示，具有均匀的精度分布和高效的编码解码例程，每个单位向量仅使用 4 字节内存。

As indicated by its name, this unit vector is based on an octahedral mapping of the unit sphere. The algorithm to convert a unit vector to this representation is surprisingly simple: divide the vector components by the vector's L1 norm, then for the upper hemisphere, take the x and y components directly.

> 正如其名称所示，此单位向量基于单位球的八面体映射。将单位向量转换为此表示的算法出人意料地简单：将向量分量除以向量的 L1 范数，然后对于上半球，直接取 x 和 y 分量。

#### Equal-Area Mapping

The third spherical parameterization used in pbrt is carefully designed to preserve area: any area on the surface of the sphere maps to a proportional area in the parametric domain. This representation is a good choice for tabulating functions on the sphere, as it is continuous, has reasonably low distortion, and all values stored represent the same solid angle. It combines the octahedral mapping with a variant of the square-to-disk mapping from Section [A.5.1](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Sampling_Algorithms/Sampling_Multidimensional_Functions.html#sec:unit-disk-sample).

> pbrt 使用的第三种球面参数化经过精心设计以保持面积：球面上的任何面积都映射到参数域中的比例面积。这种表示是在球面上列表化函数的好选择，因为它是连续的、失真合理地低，并且存储的所有值都代表相同的立体角。它结合了八面体映射和 A.5.1 节中正方形到圆盘映射的变体。

### 3.8.4 Bounding Directions

In addition to bounding regions of space, it is also sometimes useful to bound a set of directions. For example, if a light source emits illumination in some directions but not others, that information can be used to cull that light source from being included in lighting calculations for points it certainly does not illuminate. pbrt provides the [DirectionCone](#DirectionCone) class for such uses; it represents a cone that is parameterized by a central direction and an angular spread.

> 除了约束空间区域外，有时约束方向集合也很有用。例如，如果光源只向某些方向发射照明而不向其他方向发射，该信息可用于将该光源从它确定不照亮的点的光照计算中剔除。pbrt 提供了 DirectionCone 类用于此目的；它表示一个由中心方向和角度展开参数化的锥体。

For both the constructor parameters and the cone representation stored in the class, the cosine of the spread angle is used rather than the angle itself. Doing so makes it possible to perform some of the following operations with [DirectionCone](#DirectionCone) s using efficient dot products in place of more expensive trigonometric functions.

> 对于构造函数参数和类中存储的锥体表示，使用展开角的余弦而不是角度本身。这样做使得可以使用高效的点积代替更昂贵的三角函数来执行 DirectionCone 的某些后续操作。

Given a DirectionCone, it is easy to check if a given direction vector is inside its bounds: the cosine of the angle between the direction and the cone's central direction must be greater than the cosine of the cone's spread angle.

> 给定一个 DirectionCone，很容易检查给定的方向向量是否在其界限内：方向与锥体中心方向之间角度的余弦必须大于锥体展开角的余弦。

BoundSubtendedDirections() returns a DirectionCone that bounds the directions subtended by a given bounding box with respect to a point p.

> BoundSubtendedDirections() 返回一个 DirectionCone，它约束了给定包围盒相对于点 p 所张开的方向。

Finally, we will find it useful to be able to take the union of two [DirectionCone](#DirectionCone) s, finding a [DirectionCone](#DirectionCone) that bounds both of them.

> 最后，我们会发现能够取两个 DirectionCone 的并集、找到一个约束两者的 DirectionCone 很有用。
