## 1.6 A Brief History of Physically Based Rendering

Through the early years of computer graphics in the 1970s, the most important problems to solve were fundamental issues like visibility algorithms and geometric representations. When a megabyte of RAM was a rare and expensive luxury and when a computer capable of a million floating-point operations per second cost hundreds of thousands of dollars, the complexity of what was possible in computer graphics was correspondingly limited, and any attempt to accurately simulate physics for rendering was infeasible.

> 在 20 世纪 70 年代计算机图形学的早期，最重要的待解决问题是可见性算法和几何表示等基础问题。当一兆字节的内存还是稀缺且昂贵的奢侈品，当一台能进行每秒一百万次浮点运算的计算机要花费数十万美元时，计算机图形学中可能实现的复杂度相应地受到限制，任何试图精确模拟物理进行渲染的尝试都是不可行的。

As computers have become more capable and less expensive, it has become possible to consider more computationally demanding approaches to rendering, which in turn has made physically based approaches viable. This progression is neatly explained by *Blinn's law*: "as technology advances, rendering time remains constant."

> 随着计算机变得更强大、更便宜，考虑计算要求更高的渲染方法成为可能，这反过来使基于物理的方法变得可行。这一进展被 *Blinn 定律*简洁地解释："随着技术进步，渲染时间保持不变。"

Jim Blinn's simple statement captures an important constraint: given a certain number of images that must be rendered (be it a handful for a research paper or over a hundred thousand for a feature film), it is only possible to take so much processing time for each one. One has a certain amount of computation available and one has some amount of time available before rendering must be finished, so the maximum computation per image is necessarily limited.

> Jim Blinn 的简单陈述捕捉到了一个重要的约束：给定必须渲染的一定数量的图像（无论是研究论文的几张还是故事片的十万多张），每张图像只能花费有限的处理时间。在渲染必须完成之前，可用的计算量和时间都是有限的，因此每张图像的最大计算量必然受限。

Blinn's law also expresses the observation that there remains a gap between the images people would like to be able to render and the images that they can render: as computers have become faster, content creators have continued to use increased computational capability to render more complex scenes with more sophisticated rendering algorithms, rather than rendering the same scenes as before, just more quickly. Rendering continues to consume all computational capabilities made available to it.

> Blinn 定律还表达了这样一个观察：人们希望能够渲染的图像与实际能够渲染的图像之间始终存在差距：随着计算机变得更快，内容创作者继续利用增加的计算能力来渲染更复杂的场景和更精密的渲染算法，而不是更快地渲染与以前相同的场景。渲染继续消耗所有可用的计算能力。

### 1.6.1 Research

Physically based approaches to rendering started to be seriously considered by graphics researchers in the 1980s. Whitted's paper (1980) introduced the idea of using ray tracing for global lighting effects, opening the door to accurately simulating the distribution of light in scenes. The rendered images his approach produced were markedly different from any that had been seen before, which spurred excitement about this approach.

> ### 1.6.1 研究
>
> 基于物理的渲染方法在 20 世纪 80 年代开始被图形学研究者认真考虑。Whitted 的论文（1980）引入了使用光线追踪实现全局光照效果的思想，为精确模拟场景中的光分布打开了大门。他的方法产生的渲染图像与之前见过的任何图像都截然不同，这激发了人们对该方法的热情。

Another notable early advancement in physically based rendering was Cook and Torrance's reflection model (1981, 1982), which introduced microfacet reflection models to graphics. Among other contributions, they showed that accurately modeling microfacet reflection made it possible to render metal surfaces accurately; metal was not well rendered by earlier approaches.

> 基于物理的渲染另一个值得注意的早期进展是 Cook 和 Torrance 的反射模型（1981, 1982），它将微面反射模型引入了图形学。在其他贡献中，他们展示了精确建模微面反射使得准确渲染金属表面成为可能；金属在早期方法中渲染效果不佳。

Shortly afterward, Goral et al. (1984) made connections between the thermal transfer literature and rendering, showing how to incorporate global diffuse lighting effects using a physically based approximation of light transport. This method was based on finite-element techniques, where areas of surfaces in the scene exchanged energy with each other. This approach came to be referred to as "radiosity," after a related physical unit.

> 不久之后，Goral 等人（1984）建立了热传递文献与渲染之间的联系，展示了如何使用基于物理的光传输近似来融入全局漫反射光照效果。该方法基于有限元技术，其中场景中的表面区域相互交换能量。这种方法后来被称为"辐射度"（radiosity），取自一个相关的物理单位。

While the radiosity approach was based on physical units and conservation of energy, in time it became clear that it would not lead to practical rendering algorithms: the asymptotic computational complexity was difficult to manage, and it was necessary to retessellate geometric models along shadow boundaries for good results; researchers had difficulty developing robust and efficient tessellation algorithms for this purpose. Radiosity's adoption in practice was limited.

> 虽然辐射度方法基于物理单位和能量守恒，但随着时间推移，人们逐渐清楚它不会带来实用的渲染算法：渐近计算复杂度难以管理，而且需要沿着阴影边界重新细分几何模型才能获得良好的结果；研究人员难以为此目的开发稳健高效的细分算法。辐射度在实践中的采用有限。

During the radiosity years, a small group of researchers pursued physically based approaches to rendering that were based on ray tracing and Monte Carlo integration. At the time, many looked at their work with skepticism; objectionable noise in images due to Monte Carlo integration error seemed unavoidable, while radiosity-based methods quickly gave visually pleasing results, at least on relatively simple scenes.

> 在辐射度盛行的年代，一小群研究者追求基于光线追踪和蒙特卡洛积分的物理渲染方法。当时，许多人对他们的工作持怀疑态度；蒙特卡洛积分误差导致图像中令人不悦的噪声似乎不可避免，而基于辐射度的方法至少在相对简单的场景上能快速给出视觉上令人满意的结果。

In 1984, Cook, Porter, and Carpenter introduced distributed ray tracing, which generalized Whitted's algorithm to compute motion blur and defocus blur from cameras, blurry reflection from glossy surfaces, and illumination from area light sources (Cook et al. 1984), showing that ray tracing was capable of generating a host of important soft lighting effects.

> 1984 年，Cook、Porter 和 Carpenter 引入了分布式光线追踪，将 Whitted 的算法推广到计算运动模糊和相机散焦模糊、光泽表面的模糊反射以及面光源的照明（Cook 等人 1984），表明光线追踪能够生成大量重要的柔和光照效果。

Shortly afterward, Kajiya (1986) introduced path tracing; he set out a rigorous formulation of the rendering problem (the light transport integral equation) and showed how to apply Monte Carlo integration to solve it. This work required immense amounts of computation: to render a 256×256 pixel image of two spheres with path tracing required 7 hours of computation on an IBM 4341 computer, which cost roughly $280,000 when it was first released.

> 不久之后，Kajiya（1986）引入了路径追踪；他提出了渲染问题的严格公式化（光传输积分方程）并展示了如何应用蒙特卡洛积分来求解。这项工作需要巨大的计算量：在 IBM 4341 计算机上使用路径追踪渲染一张 256×256 像素的两个球体图像需要 7 小时的计算，而这台计算机首次发布时的价格大约为 28 万美元。

Both Cook et al.'s and Kajiya's work once again led to images unlike any that had been seen before, demonstrating the value of physically based methods. In subsequent years, important work on Monte Carlo for realistic image synthesis was described in papers by Arvo and Kirk (1990) and Kirk and Arvo (1991). Shirley's Ph.D. dissertation (1990) and follow-on work by Shirley et al. (1996) were important contributions to Monte Carlo–based efforts.

> Cook 等人和 Kajiya 的工作再次产生了前所未有的图像，展示了基于物理方法的价值。在随后的几年里，Arvo 和 Kirk（1990）以及 Kirk 和 Arvo（1991）的论文描述了蒙特卡洛方法在真实感图像合成方面的重要工作。Shirley 的博士论文（1990）以及 Shirley 等人的后续工作（1996）是基于蒙特卡洛方法的重要贡献。

Hall's book, *Illumination and Color in Computer Generated Imagery* (1989), was one of the first books to present rendering in a physically based framework, and Andrew Glassner's *Principles of Digital Image Synthesis* laid out foundations of the field (1995). Ward's *Radiance* rendering system was an early open source physically based rendering system, focused on lighting design (Ward 1994).

> Hall 的书 *Illumination and Color in Computer Generated Imagery*（1989）是最早以基于物理框架呈现渲染的书籍之一，Andrew Glassner 的 *Principles of Digital Image Synthesis* 奠定了该领域的基础（1995）。Ward 的 *Radiance* 渲染系统是早期的开源物理渲染系统，专注于照明设计（Ward 1994）。

A crucial step forward for physically based rendering was Veach's work, described in detail in his dissertation (Veach 1997). Veach advanced key theoretical foundations of Monte Carlo rendering while also developing new algorithms like multiple importance sampling, bidirectional path tracing, and Metropolis light transport that greatly improved its efficiency. Using Blinn's law as a guide, we believe that these significant improvements in efficiency were critical to practical adoption of these approaches.

> 基于物理渲染的一个关键进步是 Veach 的工作，在他的博士论文（Veach 1997）中有详细描述。Veach 推进了蒙特卡洛渲染的关键理论基础，同时开发了多重要性采样、双向路径追踪和 Metropolis 光传输等新算法，大大提高了效率。以 Blinn 定律为指导，我们认为这些效率上的重大改进对于这些方法的实际采用至关重要。

Around this time, as computers became faster and more parallel, a number of researchers started pursuing real-time ray tracing; Wald, Slusallek, and Benthin wrote an influential paper that described a highly optimized ray tracer that was much more efficient than previous ray tracers (Wald et al. 2001b). Many subsequent papers introduced increasingly more efficient ray-tracing algorithms.

> 大约在这个时期，随着计算机变得更快、更并行，一些研究者开始追求实时光线追踪；Wald、Slusallek 和 Benthin 写了一篇有影响力的论文，描述了一个高度优化的光线追踪器，比以前的光线追踪器高效得多（Wald 等人 2001b）。随后的许多论文引入了越来越高效的光线追踪算法。

### 1.6.2 Production

With more capable computers in the 1980s, computer graphics could start to be used for animation and film production. Early examples include Jim Blinn's rendering of the *Voyager 2* flyby of Saturn in 1981 and visual effects in the movies *Star Trek II: The Wrath of Khan* (1982), *Tron* (1982), and *The Last Starfighter* (1984).

> ### 1.6.2 制作
>
> 随着 20 世纪 80 年代计算机能力的增强，计算机图形学开始可以用于动画和电影制作。早期的例子包括 Jim Blinn 在 1981 年渲染的*旅行者 2 号*飞越土星，以及电影*星际迷航 II：可汗之怒*（1982）、*电子世界争霸战*（1982）和*乌龙王*（1984）中的视觉效果。

In early production use of computer-generated imagery, rasterization-based rendering (notably, the Reyes algorithm (Cook et al. 1987)) was the only viable option. One reason was that not enough computation was available for complex reflection models or for the global lighting effects that physically based ray tracing could provide. More significantly, rasterization had the important advantage that it did not require that the entire scene representation fit into main memory.

> 在计算机生成图像的早期制作使用中，基于光栅化的渲染（特别是 Reyes 算法（Cook 等人 1987））是唯一可行的选择。一个原因是没有足够的计算能力来处理复杂的反射模型或基于物理的光线追踪所能提供的全局光照效果。更重要的是，光栅化有一个重要的优势：它不要求整个场景表示能放入主内存。

When RAM was much less plentiful, almost any interesting scene was too large to fit into main memory. Rasterization-based algorithms made it possible to render scenes while having only a small subset of the full scene representation in memory at any time. Global lighting effects are difficult to achieve if the whole scene cannot fit into main memory; for many years, with limited computer systems, content creators effectively decided that geometric and texture complexity was more important to visual realism than lighting complexity (and in turn physical accuracy).

> 当内存远不如现在充裕时，几乎任何有趣的场景都太大而无法放入主内存。基于光栅化的算法使得在任何时候只有完整场景表示的一小部分在内存中的情况下渲染场景成为可能。如果整个场景无法放入主内存，全局光照效果就难以实现；多年来，在有限的计算机系统下，内容创作者实际上认为几何和纹理的复杂性比光照复杂性（进而比物理精确性）对视觉真实感更重要。

Many practitioners at this time also believed that physically based approaches were undesirable for production: one of the great things about computer graphics is that one can cheat reality with impunity to achieve a desired artistic effect. For example, lighting designers on regular movies often struggle to place light sources so that they are not visible to the camera. Computer graphics offers the opportunity to, for example, implement a light source model that shines twice as much light on a character as on a background object.

> 当时许多从业者还认为基于物理的方法不适合制作：计算机图形学的一大优势就是可以不受惩罚地"欺骗"现实以达到期望的艺术效果。例如，常规电影中的灯光设计师经常费力地放置光源使其不被摄影机拍到。计算机图形学提供了这样的机会，例如，实现一个对角色照射两倍于背景物体光量的光源模型。

Visual effects practitioners who had the specific need to match rendered imagery to filmed real-world environments pioneered capturing real-world lighting and shading effects and were early adopters of physically based approaches in the late 1990s and early 2000s.

> 需要将渲染图像与拍摄的真实世界环境匹配的视觉效果从业者，在捕捉真实世界的光照和着色效果方面开创了先河，并在 20 世纪 90 年代末和 21 世纪初成为基于物理方法的早期采用者。

During this time, Blue Sky Studios adopted a physically based pipeline (Ohmer 1997). The photorealism of an advertisement they made for a Braun shaver in 1992 caught the attention of many, and their short film, *Bunny*, shown in 1998, was an early example of Monte Carlo global illumination used in production.

> 在此期间，蓝天工作室采用了基于物理的管线（Ohmer 1997）。他们在 1992 年为博朗剃须刀制作的广告的逼真度引起了许多人的关注，他们在 1998 年展示的短片 *Bunny* 是蒙特卡洛全局光照在制作中使用的早期范例。

After *Bunny*, another watershed moment came in 2001, when Marcos Fajardo came to the SIGGRAPH conference with an early version of his *Arnold* renderer. He showed images in the Monte Carlo image synthesis course that not only had complex geometry, textures, and global illumination but also were rendered in tens of minutes.

> 在 *Bunny* 之后，另一个分水岭时刻出现在 2001 年，当时 Marcos Fajardo 带着他的 *Arnold* 渲染器的早期版本来到 SIGGRAPH 大会。他在蒙特卡洛图像合成课程中展示的图像不仅具有复杂的几何体、纹理和全局光照，而且只需几十分钟就能渲染完成。

Fajardo brought *Arnold* to Sony Pictures Imageworks, where work started to transform it to a production-capable physically based rendering system. *Arnold* was first used on the movie *Monster House* and is now available as a commercial product.

> Fajardo 将 *Arnold* 带到了索尼图像工作室，在那里开始将其转变为可用于制作的基于物理的渲染系统。*Arnold* 首次用于电影*怪兽屋*，现在已作为商业产品提供。

In the early 2000s, Pixar's *RenderMan* renderer started to support hybrid rasterization and ray-tracing algorithms and included a number of innovative algorithms for computing global illumination solutions in complex scenes. *RenderMan* was recently rewritten to be a physically based ray tracer, following the general system architecture of pbrt (Christensen 2015).

> 在 21 世纪初，Pixar 的 *RenderMan* 渲染器开始支持光栅化和光线追踪的混合算法，并包含许多用于在复杂场景中计算全局光照解决方案的创新算法。*RenderMan* 最近被重写为基于物理的光线追踪器，遵循 pbrt 的总体系统架构（Christensen 2015）。

One of the main reasons that physically based Monte Carlo approaches to rendering have been successful in production is that they end up improving the productivity of artists. These have been some of the important factors:

> 基于物理的蒙特卡洛渲染方法在制作中成功的主要原因之一是它们最终提高了艺术家的生产力。以下是一些重要因素：

- The algorithms involved have essentially just a single quality knob: how many samples to take per pixel; this is extremely helpful for artists. Ray-tracing algorithms are also suited to both progressive refinement and quickly computing rough previews by taking just a few samples per pixel.

> - 所涉及的算法基本上只有一个质量旋钮：每像素取多少样本；这对艺术家极其有帮助。光线追踪算法也适合渐进式细化和通过每像素只取少量样本来快速计算粗略预览。

- Adopting physically based reflection models has made it easier to design surface materials. Earlier, when reflection models that did not necessarily conserve energy were used, an object might look great in one lighting environment but appear completely wrong when moved to another.

> - 采用基于物理的反射模型使设计表面材质变得更容易。以前，当使用不一定保持能量守恒的反射模型时，一个物体在一个光照环境中可能看起来很好，但移到另一个环境中就完全不对了。

- The quality of shadows computed with ray tracing is much better than it is with rasterization. Eliminating the need to tweak shadow map resolutions, biases, and other parameters has eliminated an unpleasant task of lighting artists.

> - 用光线追踪计算的阴影质量远优于光栅化。消除调整阴影贴图分辨率、偏移和其他参数的需要，消除了灯光艺术家的一项不愉快的任务。

As of this writing, physically based rendering is used widely for producing computer-generated imagery for movies.

> 在撰写本文时，基于物理的渲染已广泛用于为电影制作计算机生成的图像。
