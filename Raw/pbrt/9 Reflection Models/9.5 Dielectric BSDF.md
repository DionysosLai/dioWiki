## 9.5 Dielectric BSDF

In the dielectric case, the relative index of refraction is real-valued, and specular transmission must be considered in addition to reflection. The DielectricBxDF handles this scenario for smooth and rough interfaces.

> 在电介质情况下，相对折射率是实值的，除了反射外还必须考虑镜面透射。DielectricBxDF 处理光滑和粗糙界面的这种情形。

Figure [9.14](#fig:transparent-machines-glass) shows an image of an abstract model using this BxDF to model a glass material.

> 图 9.14 展示了使用该 BxDF 模拟玻璃材质的抽象模型图像。

![](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Reflection_Models/transparent-machines-glass.png)

The constructor takes a single Float -valued eta parameter and a microfacet distribution mfDistrib. Spectrally varying IORs that disperse light into different directions are handled by randomly sampling a single wavelength to follow and then instantiating a corresponding DielectricBxDF. Section [10.5.1](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Textures_and_Materials/Material_Interface_and_Implementations.html#sec:dielectric-material) discusses this topic in more detail.

The Flags() method handles three different cases. The first is when the dielectric interface is *index-matched* —that is, with an equal IOR on both sides (in which case )—and light is only transmitted. Otherwise, in the other two cases, the BSDF has both reflected and transmitted components. In both of these cases, the [TrowbridgeReitzDistribution](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Reflection_Models/Roughness_Using_Microfacet_Theory.html#TrowbridgeReitzDistribution) 's EffectivelySmooth() method differentiates between specular and glossy scattering.

> 图 9.15：使用 DielectricBxDF 渲染的玻璃物体。(a) 在每次散射事件中以相等概率选择镜面反射和透射。(b) 如 Sample\_f() 方法中所实现的那样，基于菲涅耳方程的值以概率选择。以与贡献成正比的概率选择散射模式，通过减少低贡献路径的跟踪显著降低了误差。

Since dielectrics are characterized by both reflection and transmission, the sampling scheme must randomly choose between these two components, which influences the density function. While any discrete distribution is in principle admissible, an efficient approach from a Monte Carlo variance standpoint is to sample according to the contribution that these two components make—in other words, proportional to the Fresnel reflectance R and the complementary transmittance 1-R. Figure [9.15](#fig:dielectric-scatter-by-fresnel) shows the benefit of sampling in this way compared to an equal split between reflection and transmission.

> 由于电介质同时具有反射和透射特性，采样方案必须在这两个分量之间随机选择，这会影响密度函数。虽然原则上任何离散分布都是可行的，但从蒙特卡罗方差的角度来看，高效的方法是根据这两个分量的贡献进行采样——换句话说，与菲涅耳反射率 R 和互补透射率 1-R 成正比。图 9.15 展示了与反射和透射均等分配相比，以这种方式采样的优势。

### 9.5.1 Thin Dielectric BSDF

Dielectric interfaces rarely occur in isolation: a particularly common configuration involves two nearby index of refraction changes that are smooth, locally parallel, and mutually reciprocal—that is, with relative IOR and a corresponding interface with the inverse. Examples include plate- and acrylic glass in windows or plastic foil used to seal and preserve food.

> 电介质界面很少孤立出现：一种特别常见的配置涉及两个相邻的折射率变化，它们是光滑的、局部平行的、且互为倒数——即相对折射率 η 和对应的逆折射率 1/η 的界面。例如窗户中的平板玻璃和亚克力玻璃，或用于密封和保鲜食物的塑料薄膜。

This important special case is referred to as a *thin dielectric* due to the spatial proximity of the two interfaces compared to the larger lateral dimensions. When incident light splits into a reflected and a transmitted component with two separate interfaces, it is scattered in a recursive process that traps some of the light within the two interfaces (though this amount progressively decays as it undergoes an increasing number of reflections).

> 由于两个界面的空间距离相对于较大的横向尺寸而言很近，这一重要的特殊情况被称为*薄电介质*。当入射光在两个独立的界面上分裂为反射和透射分量时，它经历一个递归过程，将部分光线困在两个界面之间散射（尽管随着反射次数的增加，这个量逐渐衰减）。

While the internal scattering process within a general dielectric may be daunting, simple analytic solutions can fully describe what happens inside such a thin dielectric—that is, an interface pair satisfying the above simplifying conditions. pbrt provides a specialized BSDF named ThinDielectricBxDF that exploits these insights to efficiently represent an infinite number of internal interactions. It further allows such surfaces to be represented with a single interface, saving the ray intersection expense of tracing ray paths between the two surfaces.

> 虽然一般电介质内部的散射过程可能令人生畏，但简单的解析解可以完全描述这种薄电介质内部发生的情况——即满足上述简化条件的界面对。pbrt 提供了一个名为 ThinDielectricBxDF 的专用 BSDF，它利用这些见解高效地表示无限次内部交互。它还允许用单个界面来表示这类表面，从而节省了在两个表面之间追踪光线路径的光线求交开销。

Of course, rays beyond the first interaction are displaced relative to the entrance point. > 当然，第一次交互之后的光线相对于入射点发生了位移。由于*薄*电介质的假设，这种空间偏移被认为是可忽略的；只有反射或透射光的总量才重要。通过这种简化，可以将无限次散射事件的效果聚合为反射率和透射率因子的简单修改。

### 9.5.2 Non-Symmetric Scattering and Refraction

![](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Reflection_Models/snells-window.png)

Figure 9.18: Snell's Window. If one looks upward when underwater in a swimming pool, the sky is only visible through a circular window because no light is refracted beyond the critical angle. Outside of the window, only the reflection of the pool bottom is seen.

> 图 9.18：斯涅耳窗口。如果在游泳池水下向上看，天空只能通过一个圆形窗口可见，因为没有光线能折射超过临界角。在窗口之外，只能看到池底的反射。

All physically based BRDFs are symmetric: the incident and outgoing directions can be interchanged without changing the function's value. However, the same is not generally true for BTDFs. Non-symmetry with BTDFs is due to the fact that when light refracts into a material with a higher index of refraction than the incident medium's index of refraction, energy is compressed into a smaller set of angles (and vice versa, when going in the opposite direction). This effect is easy to see yourself, for instance, by looking at the sky from underwater in a quiet outdoor swimming pool. Because no light can be refracted above the critical angle ( for water), the incident hemisphere of light is squeezed into a considerably smaller subset of the hemisphere, which covers the remaining set of angles (Figure [9.18](#fig:snells-window)). Radiance along rays that do refract therefore must increase so that energy is preserved when light passes through the interface.

> 所有基于物理的 BRDF 都是对称的：入射方向和出射方向可以互换而不改变函数值。然而，对于 BTDF，情况通常不是这样。BTDF 的非对称性是因为当光折射进入折射率高于入射介质折射率的材料时，能量被压缩到更小的角度范围内（反之亦然，当沿相反方向传播时）。这种效果很容易亲自观察到，例如在安静的室外游泳池中从水下仰望天空。因为没有光能折射超过临界角（水的临界角约为 48.6°），入射半球的光被挤压到半球的一个小得多的子集中，该子集覆盖了剩余的角度范围（图 9.18）。因此，沿折射光线的辐射亮度必须增加，以便在光线穿过界面时保持能量守恒。

Non-symmetric scattering can be particularly problematic for bidirectional light transport algorithms that sample light paths starting both from the camera and from the lights. If non-symmetry is not accounted for, then such algorithms may produce incorrect results, since the design of such algorithms is fundamentally based on the principle of symmetry.

> 非对称散射对于从相机和光源两端同时采样光路的双向光传输算法来说尤其成问题。如果不考虑非对称性，这些算法可能会产生错误的结果，因为此类算法的设计从根本上基于对称性原理。

We will say that light paths sampled starting from the lights carry *importance* while paths starting from the camera carry radiance. > 我们将说从光源开始采样的光路携带*重要性*，而从相机开始的路径携带辐射亮度。这些术语对应于在路径起始点记录的量。在重要性传输中，除非采取特殊预防措施，否则 BSDF 的入射和出射方向参数将被（错误地）反转。
