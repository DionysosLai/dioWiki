## 9.1 BSDF Representation

There are two components of pbrt 's representation of BSDFs: the BxDF interface and its implementations (described in Section [9.1.2](#sec:bxdf-interface)) and the [BSDF](#BSDF) class (described in Section [9.1.5](#sec:bsdf)). The former models specific types of scattering at surfaces, while the latter provides a convenient wrapper around a pointer to a specific BxDF implementation. The BSDF class also centralizes general functionality so that BxDF implementations do not individually need to handle it, and it records information about the local geometric properties of the surface.

> pbrt 对 BSDF 的表示由两个部分组成：BxDF 接口及其实现（在第 9.1.2 节中描述）和 BSDF 类（在第 9.1.5 节中描述）。前者对表面上特定类型的散射进行建模，后者则为指向特定 BxDF 实现的指针提供一个便捷的包装器。BSDF 类还集中了通用功能，使得各个 BxDF 实现无需单独处理这些功能，同时它还记录了表面局部几何属性的信息。

### 9.1.1 Geometric Setting and Conventions

Reflection computations in pbrt are performed in a reflection coordinate system where the two tangent vectors and the normal vector at the point being shaded are aligned with the,, and axes, respectively (Figure [9.2](#fig:bsdf-basic-interface)). All direction vectors passed to and returned from the BxDF evaluation and sampling routines will be defined with respect to this coordinate system. It is important to understand this coordinate system in order to understand the BxDF implementations in this chapter.

> pbrt 中的反射计算在一个反射坐标系中进行，其中被着色点处的两个切线向量和法向量分别与 x、y、z 轴对齐（图 9.2）。传入和返回 BxDF 求值及采样例程的所有方向向量都将相对于该坐标系定义。理解这个坐标系对于理解本章中的 BxDF 实现至关重要。

Section [3.8](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Geometry_and_Transformations/Spherical_Geometry.html#sec:spherical-geometry) introduced a range of utility functions—like [SinTheta()](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Geometry_and_Transformations/Spherical_Geometry.html#SinTheta), [CosPhi()](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Geometry_and_Transformations/Spherical_Geometry.html#CosPhi), etc.—that efficiently evaluate trigonometric functions of unit vectors expressed in Cartesian coordinates matching the convention used here. They will be used extensively in this chapter, as quantities like the cosine of the elevation angle play a central role in most reflectance models.

> 第 3.8 节引入了一系列实用函数——如 SinTheta()、CosPhi() 等——用于高效地计算以笛卡尔坐标表示的单位向量的三角函数，与此处使用的约定一致。它们将在本章中被广泛使用，因为诸如仰角余弦之类的量在大多数反射率模型中起着核心作用。

[![](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Reflection_Models/pha09f02.svg)](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Reflection_Models/pha09f02.svg)

Figure 9.2: The Basic BSDF Coordinate Setting. The shading coordinate system is defined by the orthonormal basis vectors. We will orient these vectors such that they lie along the,, and axes in this coordinate system. Direction vectors in rendering space are transformed into the shading coordinate system before any of the BRDF or BTDF methods are called.

> 图 9.2：基本 BSDF 坐标设置。着色坐标系由正交基向量定义。我们将这些向量定向为沿该坐标系中的 x、y、z 轴。渲染空间中的方向向量在调用任何 BRDF 或 BTDF 方法之前会被变换到着色坐标系中。

We will frequently find it useful to check whether two direction vectors lie in the same hemisphere with respect to the surface normal in the BSDF coordinate system; the SameHemisphere() function performs this check.

> 我们经常需要检查两个方向向量在 BSDF 坐标系中是否位于相对于表面法线的同一个半球内；SameHemisphere() 函数执行此检查。

There are some additional conventions that are important to keep in mind when reading the code in this chapter and when adding BRDFs and BTDFs to pbrt:

> 在阅读本章代码以及向 pbrt 添加 BRDF 和 BTDF 时，还有一些重要的约定需要牢记：

- The incident light direction and the outgoing viewing direction will both be normalized and *outward facing* after being transformed into the local coordinate system at the surface. In other words, the directions will not model the physical propagation of light, which is helpful in bidirectional rendering algorithms that generate light paths in reverse order.

> - 入射光方向和出射观察方向在变换到表面的局部坐标系后都将被归一化且*朝外*。换句话说，这些方向不模拟光的物理传播方向，这对于以反向顺序生成光路的双向渲染算法非常有用。

- In pbrt, the surface normal always points to the "outside" of the object, which makes it easy to determine if light is entering or exiting transmissive objects: if the incident light direction is in the same hemisphere as, then light is entering; otherwise, it is exiting. Therefore, the normal may be on the opposite side of the surface than one or both of the and direction vectors. Unlike many other renderers, pbrt does not flip the normal to lie on the same side as.

> - 在 pbrt 中，表面法线总是指向物体的"外侧"，这使得判断光是进入还是离开透射性物体变得容易：如果入射光方向与法线在同一半球，则光正在进入；否则光正在离开。因此，法线可能位于与一个或两个方向向量相反的表面一侧。与许多其他渲染器不同，pbrt 不会翻转法线使其与出射方向位于同一侧。

- The local coordinate system used for shading may not be exactly the same as the coordinate system returned by the [Shape::Intersect()](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Shapes/Basic_Shape_Interface.html#Shape::Intersect) routines from Chapter [6](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Shapes.html#chap:shapes); it may have been modified between intersection and shading to achieve effects like bump mapping. See Chapter [10](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Textures_and_Materials.html#chap:materials) for examples of this kind of modification.

> - 用于着色的局部坐标系可能与第 6 章中 Shape::Intersect() 例程返回的坐标系不完全相同；它可能在求交和着色之间被修改，以实现凹凸贴图等效果。相关修改示例请参见第 10 章。

The key method that [BxDF](#BxDF) s provide is f(), which returns the value of the distribution function for the given pair of directions. The provided directions must be expressed in the local reflection coordinate system introduced in the previous section.

> BxDF 提供的关键方法是 f()，它返回给定方向对的分布函数值。提供的方向必须在前一节介绍的局部反射坐标系中表示。

This interface implicitly assumes that light in different wavelengths is decoupled—energy at one wavelength will not be reflected at a different wavelength. In this case, the effect of reflection can be described by a per-wavelength factor returned in the form of a [SampledSpectrum](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Radiometry,_Spectra,_and_Color/Representing_Spectral_Distributions.html#SampledSpectrum). Fluorescent materials that redistribute energy between wavelengths would require that this method return an matrix to encode the transfer between the spectral samples of [SampledSpectrum](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Radiometry,_Spectra,_and_Color/Representing_Spectral_Distributions.html#SampledSpectrum).

> 该接口隐含地假设不同波长的光是解耦的——一个波长的能量不会被反射到另一个波长。在这种情况下，反射的效果可以用以 SampledSpectrum 形式返回的逐波长因子来描述。对于在波长之间重新分配能量的荧光材料，则需要该方法返回一个矩阵来编码 SampledSpectrum 光谱样本之间的转移。

Neither constructors nor methods of BxDF implementations will generally be informed about the specific wavelengths associated with SampledSpectrum entries, since they do not require this information.

> BxDF 实现的构造函数和方法通常不会获知与 SampledSpectrum 条目关联的具体波长信息，因为它们不需要这些信息。

<<BxDF Interface>>+=

[SampledSpectrum](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Radiometry,_Spectra,_and_Color/Representing_Spectral_Distributions.html#SampledSpectrum) f([Vector3f](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Geometry_and_Transformations/Vectors.html#Vector3f) wo, [Vector3f](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Geometry_and_Transformations/Vectors.html#Vector3f) wi, TransportMode mode) const;

The function also takes a [TransportMode](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Reflection_Models/Dielectric_BSDF.html#TransportMode) enumerator that indicates whether the outgoing direction is toward the camera or toward a light source (and the corresponding opposite for the incident direction). This is necessary to handle cases where scattering is non-symmetric; this subtle aspect is discussed further in Section [9.5.2](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Reflection_Models/Dielectric_BSDF.html#sec:non-symmetric).

> 该函数还接收一个 TransportMode 枚举值，指示出射方向是朝向相机还是朝向光源（入射方向则相反）。这对于处理散射非对称的情况是必要的；这一微妙的方面将在第 9.5.2 节中进一步讨论。

BxDF s must also provide a method that uses importance sampling to draw a direction from a distribution that approximately matches the scattering function's shape. Not only is this operation crucial for efficient Monte Carlo integration of the light transport equation ([1.1](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Introduction/Photorealistic_Rendering_and_the_Ray-Tracing_Algorithm.html#eq:rendering-equation)), it is the only way to evaluate some BSDFs. For example, perfect specular objects like a mirror, glass, or water only scatter light from a single incident direction into a single outgoing direction. Such [BxDF](#BxDF) s are best described with *Dirac delta distributions* (covered in more detail in Section [9.1.4](#sec:dirac-delta)) that are zero except for the single direction where light is scattered. Their f() and PDF() methods always return zero.

> BxDF 还必须提供一种方法，使用重要性采样从近似匹配散射函数形状的分布中抽取方向。这一操作不仅对光传输方程（1.1）的高效蒙特卡罗积分至关重要，而且是评估某些 BSDF 的唯一方式。例如，像镜子、玻璃或水这样的完美镜面物体只将光从单一入射方向散射到单一出射方向。这类 BxDF 最好用*狄拉克 δ 分布*来描述（在第 9.1.4 节中更详细地介绍），除了光散射的单一方向外其值为零。它们的 f() 和 PDF() 方法始终返回零。

Implementations of the Sample\_f() method should determine the direction of incident light given an outgoing direction and return the value of the [BxDF](#BxDF) for the pair of directions. They take three uniform samples in the range via the uc and u parameters. Implementations can use these however they wish, though it is generally best if they use the 1D sample uc to choose between different types of scattering (e.g., reflection or transmission) and the 2D sample to choose a specific direction. Using uc and u\[0\] to choose a direction, for example, would likely give inferior results to using u\[0\] and u\[1\], since uc and u\[0\] are not necessarily jointly well distributed. Not all the sample values need be used, and BxDF s that need additional sample values must generate them themselves. (The [LayeredBxDF](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Transport_II_Volume_Rendering/Scattering_from_Layered_Materials.html#LayeredBxDF) described in Section [14.3](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Transport_II_Volume_Rendering/Scattering_from_Layered_Materials.html#sec:scattering-layered) is one such example.)

> Sample_f() 方法的实现应在给定出射方向的情况下确定入射光方向，并返回该方向对的 BxDF 值。它们通过 uc 和 u 参数接收三个范围内的均匀样本。实现可以按需使用这些样本，但通常最好使用一维样本 uc 在不同类型的散射（如反射或透射）之间做选择，使用二维样本来选择具体方向。例如使用 uc 和 u[0] 来选择方向可能比使用 u[0] 和 u[1] 效果差，因为 uc 和 u[0] 不一定具有良好的联合分布。不需要使用所有样本值，需要额外样本值的 BxDF 必须自己生成。（第 14.3 节描述的 LayeredBxDF 就是一个例子。）

Note the potentially counterintuitive direction convention: the outgoing direction is given, and the implementation then samples an incident direction. The Monte Carlo methods in this book construct light paths in *reverse* order—that is, counter to the propagation direction of the transported quantity (radiance or importance)—motivating this choice.

> 请注意可能违反直觉的方向约定：给定出射方向，实现随后采样一个入射方向。本书中的蒙特卡罗方法以*反向*顺序构建光路——即与传输量（辐射亮度或重要性）的传播方向相反——这正是该选择的动机。

Callers of this method must be prepared for the possibility that sampling fails, in which case an unset optional value will be returned.

> 该方法的调用者必须准备好采样可能失败的情况，在这种情况下将返回一个未设置的可选值。

If sampling succeeds, the method returns a BSDFSample that includes the value of the BSDF f, the sampled direction wi, its probability density function (PDF) measured with respect to solid angle, and a [BxDFFlags](#BxDFFlags) instance that describes the characteristics of the particular sample. BxDF s should specify the direction wi with respect to the local reflection coordinate system, though [BSDF::Sample\_f()](#BSDF::Sample_f) will transform this direction to rendering space before returning it.

> 如果采样成功，该方法返回一个 BSDFSample，其中包括 BSDF f 的值、采样方向 wi、相对于立体角度量的概率密度函数（PDF），以及一个描述该特定样本特征的 BxDFFlags 实例。BxDF 应相对于局部反射坐标系指定方向 wi，但 BSDF::Sample_f() 会在返回之前将该方向变换到渲染空间。

Some BxDF implementations (notably, the [LayeredBxDF](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Transport_II_Volume_Rendering/Scattering_from_Layered_Materials.html#LayeredBxDF) described in Section [14.3](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Light_Transport_II_Volume_Rendering/Scattering_from_Layered_Materials.html#sec:scattering-layered)) generate samples via simulation, following a random light path. The distribution of paths that escape is the BxDF 's exact (probabilistic) distribution, but the returned f and pdf are only proportional to their true values. (Fortunately, by the same proportion!) This case needs special handling in light transport algorithms, and is indicated by the pdfIsProportional field. For all the BxDF s in this chapter, it can be left set to its default false value.

> 一些 BxDF 实现（特别是第 14.3 节描述的 LayeredBxDF）通过模拟生成样本，沿随机光路追踪。逃逸路径的分布是 BxDF 的精确（概率性）分布，但返回的 f 和 pdf 仅与其真实值成正比。（幸运的是，比例相同！）这种情况需要在光传输算法中进行特殊处理，由 pdfIsProportional 字段标示。对于本章中的所有 BxDF，该字段可以保持其默认值 false。

### 9.1.4 Delta Distributions in BSDFs

Several BSDF models in this chapter make use of Dirac delta distributions to represent interactions with perfect specular materials like smooth metal or glass surfaces. They represent a curious corner case in implementations, and we therefore establish a few important conventions.

According to these equations, can be interpreted as a normalized density function that is zero for all. Generating a sample from such a distribution is trivial, since there is only one value that it can take. In this sense, the forthcoming implementations of Sample\_f() involving delta functions naturally fit into the Monte Carlo sampling framework.

> 根据这些方程，δ 可以被解释为对所有非零值为零的归一化密度函数。从这样的分布生成样本是简单的，因为它只能取一个值。在这个意义上，接下来涉及 δ 函数的 Sample_f() 实现自然适合蒙特卡罗采样框架。

However, sampling alone is not enough: two methods (Sample\_f() and PDF) also provide sampling densities, and it is considerably less clear what values should be returned here. Strictly speaking, the delta distribution is not a true function but constitutes the limit of a sequence of functions—for example, one describing a box of unit area whose width approaches 0; see Chapter 5 of Bracewell ([2000](https://pbr-book.org/4ed/Reflection_Models/Further_Reading.html#cite:Bracewell:Fourier)) for details. In the limit, the value of must then necessarily tend toward infinity. This important theoretical realization does not easily translate into C++ code: certainly, returning an infinite or very large PDF value is not going to lead to correct results from the renderer.

> 然而，仅仅采样是不够的：两个方法（Sample_f() 和 PDF）还提供采样密度，而在这里应该返回什么值就不太清楚了。严格来说，δ 分布不是一个真正的函数，而是函数序列的极限——例如一个面积为单位、宽度趋近于零的矩形函数；详见 Bracewell（2000）第 5 章。在极限情况下，其值必然趋向无穷大。这一重要的理论认识不容易转化为 C++ 代码：返回无穷大或非常大的 PDF 值当然不会使渲染器产生正确的结果。

The f() function performs the required coordinate frame conversion and then queries the BxDF. The rare case in which the wo direction lies exactly in the surface's tangent plane often leads to not-a-number (NaN) values in BxDF implementations that further propagate and may eventually contaminate the rendered image. The BSDF avoids this case by immediately returning a zero-valued SampledSpectrum.

> f() 函数执行所需的坐标系转换，然后查询 BxDF。wo 方向恰好位于表面切平面中的罕见情况通常会在 BxDF 实现中产生非数字（NaN）值，这些值会进一步传播并可能最终污染渲染图像。BSDF 通过立即返回零值的 SampledSpectrum 来避免这种情况。
